Why Pryze?

Pryze is creating the first sweepstakes automation protocol built on the Ethereum blockchain. We are developing our application and website to make it easy for new and veteran sweepstakes creators to easily run and maintain sweepstakes. Our aim is to bake compliance, security, and scalability into the Pryze protocol and therefore eliminate the need for costly administrative services and hosting platforms. We make all of this possible through the power of the Ethereum blockchain.
