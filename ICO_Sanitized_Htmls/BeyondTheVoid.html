DRAFT, HARVEST AND DESTROY TO DOMINATE THE GALAXY

EXPERIENCE THE THRILL OF BEING A CAPTAIN!

Beyond the Void is a new hybrid strategy game in space, with RTS and MOBA mechanics. It is a unique combination of strategy, action and competition. Venture out into a new galaxy and experience the thrill of being a Captain who leads epic space wars in 1 vs1. Design your own strategy and fleet to become the one who’ll dominate the galaxy!

Select your mothership and its capabilities. You have only one true goal: design and build an invincible fleet to conquer your opponent’s home planet! Now Captain, board your mothership and combine your strategy and resources to take the war beyond the void.
