Speculative PoW / PoS altcoin with CPU mining capabilities and PoS minting at the rate of 100% per annum on staked coins. AAA COIN is a DAO and is not owned by any one individual, government, corporation; or is domiciled in any one country.

ICO is now over. The coin is listed on:

  • CREX24 exchange under AAA ticker
    • AAA / BTC

AAA COIN is easily mined with just a laptop, no GPU required. Prof of Stake is used instead of Proof of Work where holding coin in your wallet and running wallet 24/7 contributes to the blockchain network. As a reward, your coin grows at 100% annual return on staked coins (~0.3% a day).

Action items to mint your own coin:

  1. Download wallet
  2. Buy coin on CREX24 and transfer coin to your hardware wallet
  3. Run wallet 24/7 to mint more coin
  4. Trade and speculate coin on CREX24
