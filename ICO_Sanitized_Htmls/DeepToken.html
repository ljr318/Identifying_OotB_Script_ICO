2018-12-11

Deeptoken Announcement

Earlier this year MindArk developed and promoted DeepToken, a token offering with the ambition of raising funds to create an intellectual property and digital asset bank, as well as improving Entropia Universe.

With time it has become evident that the ambitious project of DeepToken will not be able to be fulfilled. Therefore, the MindArk Board of Directors has decided to suspend the project and refund all the investments made to MindArk related to DeepToken.

All purchases of DeepToken made via credit/debit cards will be automatically reimbursed within 15 business days of this announcement. For those who purchased DeepToken via ETH (Ethereum), please contact support@deeptoken.io to coordinate a refund to your current ETH wallet. For purchases made via bank transfer, please contact support@deeptoken.io to coordinate your refund.

Best regards,

Henrik Nel
CEO
