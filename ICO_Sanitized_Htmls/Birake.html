Frequently asked questions

What is Birake Exchange Network?

Birake mission is to offer both exchange owners and users of Birake network the largest database of orders / users and in the same time to achieve high trade volumes.
Birake shall inter-connect exchanges from its network and share orders & assets between them. Moreover, it will provide future trading platform owners the flexibility to incorporate their own domain name brand, logo and personalisation of their exchange platforms. The Birake Network is a central database of orders which are openly shared among all exchanges run on the network. Similarly, traders can have access to multiple exchange platforms at once through the Birake exchanges network.

Is the White Label Exchange Platform ready?

The cryptocurrency exchange platform is live and ready for delivery. You can check it at the following link https://trade.birake.com . Right now we are testing the layer on top of Birake exchange for gathering the statistics for webmasters.

What are the requirements for setting up my exchange?

Birake offers 4 different business models to start your crypto exchange. First you have to choose the package that suits your business model. After you fill in the request form, you will receive and email form us with all the necessary steps to conclude our partnership. Please be aware that all the emails from us are ending in @birake.com

How does the White Label Exchange Platform work?

Having a White Label Exchange Platform means you have no worries regarding technical implementation, servers, hosting, customer support, client support, development, security...all of this and much more will be supported by Birake company.
After you choose the package you are interested in and we conclude the agreement, you will have access to a demo trading platform which you can customized the way you like. It will be hosted on your domain, with your logo, branding etc.

Do I need any technical knowledge for the set up?

No, setting up the exchange doesn’t require any technical knowledge, but in case you need help, our support team is ready to assist you.

How long does it take to set up the exchange?

In general, the exchange platform with standard functionality can be deployed in a few days. It is highly advisable to first have a well defined frontpage and provide us your logo for trading platform. If you require further customization after the initial consultation this will delay the Go Live date as the customizations need to be developed and tested. If you want to receive information about the estimated implementation time for added features we’re happy to provide an estimation.

Which currency pairs are available on the trading exchange platform?

You will have access to all the available currencies from Birake Exchanges Network. We will start with BTC based coins, BIR and ETH.

What can be traded on the exchange software?

ANY cryptocurrencies, in the future we will implement also FIAT.

Why are you sharing orderbook, assets and users?

Think about how it is to start an exchange full of active orders since the beginning. Every new trader that comes through you will see your exchange as an established one, he can trade and interconnect with other exchanges from Birake network. Even if your exchange has 1 trader on our network he will find a full orderbook and other traders to trade with.

Can I see a demo?

We have more than a demo. We have a live product which can be seen at https://trade.birake.com
