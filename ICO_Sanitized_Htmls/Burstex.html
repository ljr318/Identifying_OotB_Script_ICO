Industry Details

Government regulations, mandatory licenses and red tape stop many investors from entering the crypto exchange market. Therefore missing out on a reliable and profitable investment opportunity.

Many exchanges today are built on old and outdated platforms making them vulnerable to attacks and exploits.

Each day over 40 Billion USD is traded on exchanges worldwide, resulting in exchanges profiting millions.

Very few exchanges offer marginal trading and if they do it is for a very low amount. We see this as an issue and a limiting factor for any trader. Marginal trading (Leverage) is necessary for cryptocurrencies to become mainstream tradeable assets.

Solutions

Burstex is the World's First crowd owned exchange, meaning all profits are distributed among BEX token-holders.

Burstex is built on the Burst blockchain. One of fastest, secure and most eco-friendly blockchains to date.

Achieving just a small market share will result in million dollar profits for BEX token holders.

Up to 200x leverage allows our traders to perform to their highest abilities and potential.

why burst

Burst is a completely decentralized cryptocurrency run by an open source community of skilled and enthusiastic developers.

Burst is mined using PoC (Proof of Capacity) technology and finally making mining eco-friendly.

PoC allows Burst to make instant transactions and be infinitely scalable, giving us an advantage over our competitors.

The Burst blockchain enabled us to provide a fast, secure and trustless environment.

platform

Never miss another opportunity, trade on the go with our mobile platform. Compatible with iOS and Android mobile devices. Burstex offers both a browser platform as well as a mobile one.

With mobile becoming the preferred way to use the internet, our goal is to provide our users with a successful on the go trading experience.
