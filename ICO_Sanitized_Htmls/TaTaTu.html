Tatatu Coin

TAT01 is a newly created coin used by players in the entertainment, media, e-commerce, advertising and general retails industry

Players can use it instead of other currencies such US, EURO etc to pay or receive TTU Coins.

TaTaTu has launched an international memorandum of understanding to establish a fixed rate of exchange for the TTU Coin of 0.25 USD.

  • The TaTaTu Coin is used to reward users and their friends to watch content in TaTaTu.
  • Users can spend their TTU to buy products in the dedicated e-commerce store and to buy products and services in the various retail shop that accept TTU.
  • Affiliated businesses that accept TTU are listed and featured in the TaTaTu App.

If you would like to be part of the network accepting the TTU Coin as a person or company complete the form and agree to the Memorandum of Understanding.

Companies can buy advertising in TTU Coin in exchange of media Ads space.

TTU active market

Participate to the TTU active market has the following characteristics: (i) it is a means of exchange payment as the company has signed contracts with suppliers where the payment in TTU is accepted; (ii) acts as a standard numerical unit because the contracts provide a fixed dollar / TTU Coin exchange ratio; (iii) the setting of the exchange ratio makes possible to consider this asset as a reserve of value that maintains its value over time and is not subject to instability.

The above concept is an extraordinary marketplace especially for listed companies because it adds to the concept of easy exchange payment method, a tremendous opportunity of fidelization of their main stakeholders such as clients and suppliers.
