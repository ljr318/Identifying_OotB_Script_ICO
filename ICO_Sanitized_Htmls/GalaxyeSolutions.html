Key Features

Marketplace on Blockchain

Decentralised marketplace allows highly transparent trade without the concern of trust issues.

Fair deals only

Escrow payments made possible through the use of Smart Contracts that protects both sellers and buyers

Information Security

On a decentralized platform, information and transaction records are safe from hacking and alteration.

cost effective

Both transaction costs and time become more efficient comparing to traditional practices. The focus can be put on the business not documents handling.

Less middle men

Smart Contracts allows direct transactions without any trust concerns that reduce the need on middle man services.

Easy entry and fairness

Smaller business can join easier into the ecosystems with less barriers and more direct reach to the market. It drives a fairer environment.
