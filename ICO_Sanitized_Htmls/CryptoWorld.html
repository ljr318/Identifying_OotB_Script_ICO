The CWV Concept

  • What is CWV?

    The CryptoWorld.Vip (CWV) public chain is the world's first integrated main chain based on the multi-chain mechanism. It is a blockchain 3.0 product. The CryptoWorld game is a blockchain world created on the CWV chain based on a 1:1 mapping to the real world. Buildings in the CryptoWorld [game] access different chains and blockchain services based on their functions.
  • What technical solutions does CWV offer?

    CWV combines Raft+DPos algorithms with alliance nodes and the integrated main chain technique of individual public chains, adopting a better technical framework.
    CryptoWorld is an open platform of inter-chain networks made up of cross-chain free conversion agreement, smart contracts, SDK for Docker operations, shared account book systems and a decentralized wallet system. It is an open platform of blockchain applications and a multi-chain integrated network with side matchmaking technology supporting cross-chain transactions. The core concept of CryptoWorldâs inter-chain network mainly involves two parts:
    1) a decentralized wallet system, supporting both an in-house CryptoWorld wallet and any hot or cold storage wallets from third parties;
    2) a decentralized transaction docking system. CryptoWorld supports cross-chain free Token-to-Token conversion agreement.
  • What is the motivation for developing this project?

    Due to factors including; low market penetration, lack of seamless connectivity, missing compatibility between blockchains, and the effort required for the accessibility of the DApp (decentralised applications), it is currently difficult for users to fully experience the many services provided by blockchain technology.
    With the rise of blockchain applications, users will require a portal that allows them to effectively experience blockchain applications through visualized scenarios. The virtual space created by the CryptoWorld.Vip Platform provides the user with a visual interaction between themself and the blockchain technologies through the form of digital assets. Users will be able to participate in social networking, financial transactions, gaming, and many other activities in the virtual world, all with blockchain serving as its infrastructure. The CryptoWorld.Vip Platform is envisioned as an entry-level platform for gaming related cross-chain applications.
  • The scope of business: What products or services are provided and what other services will be provided in the future by CryptoWorld?
    Virtual "real estate" is the basic framework of the CryptoWorld game. To enrich application and to increase activity, the CryptoWorld.Vip Platform will develop a series of mini-games. CryptoWorld Foundation Ltd (the Foundation) will manage the development of a large-scale application aggregation platform (the CryptoWorld.Vip Platform) that focuses on game applications in the early stage, then extending this to other applications with the growth of the platform operation and expansion of the user base.

    Simulating the Asset Management Game Platform

    • Can you provide detailed introduction about the game platform that simulates asset operation?

      The game platform in CryptoWorld that simulates asset transaction maps real estate in the real world in 1:1 mapping to ensure consistency with real estate in the real world. The quantity of virtual real estate is limited. Buildings access different blockchains and blockchain services according to their function attributes and provide users with virtual experience of blockchains penetrated to the real world. Real estates consist of landmark buildings, functional entry buildings, and common buildings. The building attributes (with personal geographical location, size, and function) determine the values of buildings and therefore enable transactions among users.
      The game platform maps functions of virtual buildings in the real world to corresponding games (that is, scenario-based applications) to provide reality simulation game services for CryptoWorld users. By using CWV that can be circulated on the platform, users can conveniently consume services same as those in the real world. For example, play games in an entertainment city, watch live broadcast of sport events in a stadium, buy licensed video resources from a cinema, or buy online courses from a "school".
      What's more, users can define the modes for interaction with others; playing games, making transactions, and communication on the platform. In this way, each user can transfer from a consumer to an operator. For example, the entertainment city has the game function. A user who purchases the real estate can access the game chain to operate various games. The transaction exchange center accesses the decentralized transaction platform to provide token and transaction exchange services for all users. The casino has the quiz game operation function. Quiz game blockchains under a cooperation agreement with the platform will be incorporated to develop the casino businesses on the platform. CryptoWorld has rich game operation modes, enabling users to obtain benefits together with the platform. Various functional buildings meet the operation scenario requirements of different blockchain applications.
      In the first phase of the game platform, it is planned that the America map is online. A huge entertainment city will be provided in the virtual world, where, landmark buildings, such as Metropolitan Museum of Art in New York, entertainment center of Las Vegas, and Hollywood of Los Angeles are available. In the preliminary stage, the platform focuses on games. With optimization of the ecosystem and expansion of the user scale, the platform will add other elements related to our daily life, such as film, sport, education, and culture, ensuring that CryptoWorld comes from but excels the real life. The platform is a subversive platform for life and entertainment value creation. It provides new life concepts that are hard to be realized in the real world.
    • Can CryptoWorld be managed in a manner similar to that in the real community?
      CryptoWorld can be managed autonomously. Based on the DPoS consensus algorithm, users can vote for operational matters according to the amount of CWV they own. The weight of each vote positively correlates to the amount of CWV the user owns.
    • How does one obtain game assets in CryptoWorld?

      1) Random drawing: When a user owns 50,000 CWV, the user has a chance to draw a CryptoWorld real estate. No matter whether the user succeeds, the CWV owned by the user will not be deducted. Each user only has one drawing chance until all real estate has been distributed. By calling APIs of Google Maps, a user can draw the longitude and latitude in the CryptoWorld real estate database. The real estate displayed on Google Maps is the asset owned by the user. To ensure fairness, the drawing process is assisted by a miner in a random manner.

      2) Exchange for real estates using purchased areas: Users can purchase an area by bidding from the real estate exchange on the platform every hour. Those who obtain the area can exchange for a real estate with the same area from the real estate exchange. Houses in different locations will consume different cost resources, which are settled using CWV.

      3) Listed trading: The real estate exchange supports listed trading of resold real estates between users.
