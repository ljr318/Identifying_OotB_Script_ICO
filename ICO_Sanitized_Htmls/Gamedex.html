overview

Gamedex is a platform, similar to Steam, for digital collectible cards and their games.

Play exciting multiplayer games

The Gamedex game store lets you find and play games which use your cards. Play for fun, or wager tokens for a chance at riches.

Collect and trade digital cards.

Buy and sell digital cards against our token on the Gamedex exchange, or trade directly with other collectors. Trades are global, fast, and free.

Why blockchain?

1. True Ownership

With blockchain, you truly own your digital assets. Other digital assets are merely an entry in someone else’s database. If you get banned, or the company shuts down, you’ve lost everything.

2. True Scarcity

Gamedex’s Limited Edition collectibles are truly limited, and you don’t need to take our word for it. Thanks to blockchain technology, an asset’s issuer can’t simply create more.

3. Trustless Authenticity

Counterfeiting digital assets is impossible. Everyone can trustlessly verify for themselves whether or not an asset is genuine.

Platform Features

Cards and Games

Collect digital cards and play exciting multiplayer games.

Gamedex is your digital card wallet, exchange, and game store, all in one. Gamedex will run on both desktops and smartphones.

SCRATCH-AND-REVEAL PACKS

No credit card? No problem. Buy GDX tokens in stores with cash.

While Gamedex’s provisional chains allow for credit-card payments, in-store cash packs allow for adoption throughout the developing world and among teens and others who may not have access to credit cards. A similar method allows Gamedex partners to sell their digital cards in stores in physical form.

TRADE SAFELY

Peer-to-peer trading is simple and safe with our free escrow.

Our smart contract escrow system allows for collectors to choose the exact cards that must be sent to the escrow by both parties before anything is released.  This makes direct peer-to-peer trading risk-free.

COLLECTIBLE EXCHANGE

It’s never been so convenient to get hooked on collecting.

Our in-house digitable collectible exchange pairs all issued assets against GDX tokens. Snag a top card to help you win your next game or sell that rare limited edition to the highest bidder. Collectors can buy any card they wish, provided someone is willing to sell it.
