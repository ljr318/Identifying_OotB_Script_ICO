A BlockChain is a constantly growing Ledger which keeps a permanent record of all the transactions that have taken place in a secure, chronological, and immutable way.

By allowing digital information to be distributed but not copied, BlockChain technology created the backbone of new type of internet. Originally devised for the digital currency, Bitcoin BlockChain the tech community has now found other potential uses for the technology.

BlockChain can be used for the secure transfer of money, property, contracts etc. Without requiring a third party interventions like bank or government. BlockChain is a software protocol but it could not be run without the Internet.
