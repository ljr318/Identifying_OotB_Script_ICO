ASURE BLOCKCHAIN

The Asure Blockchain will be a performant and secure proof-of-stake based blockchain that enables high transaction throughput at a low cost, which would be necessary for implementing blockchain based social security systems within countries.

The Asure Blockchain is built on top of Substrate - a blockchain framework developed by the Web3 Foundation / Parity Technologies. Substrate provides a great abstraction of common blockchain functionalities and allows us to reuse common features, innovate faster, and develop new modules that solve problems within the domain of social security systems.

Platform

The Asure platform provides a solid foundation for a broad spectrum of insurance solutions and products. It enables individuals, companies, and governments to develop, run and maintain solutions which satisfy customers needs.

The Asure platform is open-source (MIT licensed) and development is happening in the public. The Asure Foundation, together with the broader community, will ensure the future growth of the platform. Therefore, the Asure Foundation specifies open standards (AIPs) within the insurance industry using the Asure platform and blockchain technology to enables the transition towards an open and decentralized insurance infrastructure of the future.

TRANSPARENCY
Data around products and services will be openly available to engage community developers in platform support and development. Also, Asure can easily be verified and extended by the broader community as all specifications and the source code will be publicly available.
COST REDUCTION
A full insurance platform with advanced, but easy to use functions for participants, including predictive analytics, artificial intelligence, machine learning and robust self-sustaining economy, based on smart contracts that will reduce administrative expenses by up to 98%.
FLEXIBILITY
We believe that insurance is a fundamental human right. New products can be contributed by and for everyone around the world. In a free and self-regulated market, the best services and products prevail.

Customer-centric

Understanding and comparing the details of various kinds of insurance can be complicated and time-consuming. At Asure, we make understanding insurance products easy. This will be achieved by a combination of diverse interactive data exploration and visualization techniques, product calculators, and artificial intelligence-driven chatbots.

Blockchain-based smart contracts

To fulfill our promise of openness about business operation, Asure platform leverages the blockchain technology. Business logic is implemented as smart contracts and is run within the public blockchain of Ethereum . Due to the unique properties of blockchain technology, the implemented business logic can be inspected and verified by the public. Another advantage is, that smart contracts are tamper-proof.

Automation

At Asure we are working towards a self-regulating and fully autonomous platform. This will be made possible by building upon the latest achievements in technology like IoT, Big Data, Artificial Intelligence and many more. In the following you find some key areas of interest:

  • Big Data and Predictive Analytics for product and risk management.
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning for automation and self-regulation.
  • Automated validation, claims processing, chatbots and autonomous agents.
  • Increased levels of artificial intelligence and AI algorithms will provide self-sustainability in operations and value creation at the platform.

Asure economy system

Asure offers a reward system which allows its users to make profit by contributing to the platform. Asure economy system supports two basic types of rewards: a proof-of-stake and a proof-of-activity which will reward active participants.

  • Users of the Asure platform can invite their friends and get a reward.
  • Investors can buy risks of others as part of our reinsurance system and get a reward for taking risk.
  • Asure platform is open-source and is developed in the public. Anyone can help to improve it which also will be rewarded.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What exactly is your business model?
Just as Bitcoin does not belong to any organization but aims for decentralization, so we do not have a bussines model but want to make social insurance a way out in a decentralized network so that the benefits of the new technology can serve the common good to improve people's lives.
Which blockchain will you use?
We are are developing a new blockchain for social security applications wich will be connected via bridges to other public blockchains like ethereum-blockchain, binnace-blockchain, polkadot-blockchain, etc.
What cryptocurrencies are accepted in the token sale?
ETH is the only cryptocurrency accepted during the token sale in exchange for ASR tokens. You will be required to have an Ethereum wallet to participate in the token sale. ASR are Ethereum derived tokens. If you hold BTC or some other cryptocurrency it can be exchanged for ETH and used to participate in the token sale.
What is an ERC20 token?
An ERC20 token is a blockchain-based asset with similar functionality to bitcoin, ether, and bitcoin cash: it can hold value and be sent and received.
Will the price of your token rise?
We cannot comment on that. All we can say is that basic economics dictate that if there is a fixed supply of an item and social security transactions, and the demand for it rises, then the price will rise accordingly.
Should I invest in your token?
We do not offer any investment advice. Our goal is to build the largest, globally distributed social security blockchain network and community.
Are there any plans for an IEO?
Yes, our crowdsale contract is designed for that and we are talking to several exchanges about the options and the right time.
Where is your organisation based?
Our organisation is established in Zug, Switzerland of the Swiss Crypto Valley.
What is Asure Network?
Asure is a a network for decentralized social security applications, powered by distributed ledger technology innovation.
How can I buy ASR tokens?
The first to sign up for whitelisting.
From December 1st to December 31th there will be smart contract addresse on this website.
What does the token do?
The ASR token is a utility token. It is implemented as an Ethereum smart contract and supports the ERC-20 token standards. The token will be use by Network Validators as well as Service Providers to participate as stakers in the proof-of-stake consensus mechanisms of the Asure network.
Do you have a fixed supply of ASR tokens?
Yes. See website for more details.
Are you on any exchanges yet?
No. We have targets on our timeline to have the ASR token available on exchanges.
Do you have a working version of Asure Network?
Almost, we are very close to an MVP (minimum viable product) which our beta tester will be using. Out Testnet is live and can be found here. http://explorer.asure.network
Which wallet can be used to store ASR?
ASR is an ERC20 token, meaning it can be stored in ERC20 compatible wallets such as MyEtherWallet, TrustWallet and others.
How can I follow your updates?
You can join our newsletter update list, Telegram, and via our social media. Please see website for full details.
