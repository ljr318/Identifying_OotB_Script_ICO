TUNE TOKEN
A Decentralized Music Production Marketplace.

Tune Company is a software development and management company primarily focused on creating blockchain based technology, smart contract design and digital tokenized applications.  Tune’s primary mission is to solve digital songwriter rights and royalty transparency issues for the music industry. Furthermore, the establishment of our tokenized products will enable applications the ability to offer user tipping and user/community incentivized rewards thru the sharing, creation and engagement of content as well as other features.  Tune is proud to announce a licensing agreement with the TechCrunch Disrupt Cup award winning RecordGram application.

CRE8TOR.app

The Award Winning Mobile App formerly known as RecordGram.

Cre8tor.app looks to become the next disrupting force in the music industry. Cre8tor.app’s vision is to establish itself as a decentralized mobile application that truly democratizes music collaboration and talent discovery. At the same time, it looks to provide those users who generate value on the platform, with an opportunity to get compensated for their involvement. All this is to be done by tapping into the power of blockchain technology and cryptocurrencies.

Current Platform CRE8TOR

Currently, you can download Cre8tor.app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Users are able to unlock beats, create songs & videos and share their content. All this done using fiat currency.

Future Platform CRE8TOR

Once blockchain technology has been implemented, users will be able to convert their TUNE Tokens into Cre8tor Credits. Then use the Cre8tor Credits to unlock beats, tip artists, etc. Royalty Payments will be paid using TUNE Tokens. All user generated content will be place on the blockchain and can be transparently tracked.
