What is the mission of Futbol Coin?

Our mission is to place Futbol Coin as the leading currency in the soccer universe. For it Futbol Coin will have an active participation in the search and acquisition of players of lower divisions with great soccer projection. It will be able to be used in its own page of sport betting -exclusive of soccer-; in online and physical sport stores- exclusively for products and services related to the world of the Soccer-; and in the creation of soccer fields of 5, 8 and 11.

What is the vision of Futbol Coin?

Our vision is to modernize the operations in the soccer universe and all the activities related to it by using our Futbol Coin (FUCO) token.
