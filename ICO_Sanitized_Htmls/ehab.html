Our platform automates construction contracts



Streamline your contract administration & avoid disputes.



EHAB is an online platform which helps contractors and clients manage their contract more effectively.



We use real time data, a shared portal and codified contract workflows to reduce admin and avoid costly disputes. Within this same system we offer parametrc insurance which automatically pays out based on the information held within the portal, helping both parties cap their risk



EHAB is leading a consortium of partners on an Innovate UK Grant project.



We are working with world leading partners to redefine the way that the industry interacts with its contracts. Gone are the days when arguments go on for years after the close of a project. Using cutting edge digital technologies we can deal with issues in real time and get the best outcome for all parties involved.
