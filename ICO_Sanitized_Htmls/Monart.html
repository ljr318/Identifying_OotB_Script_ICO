Carpe Diem Charity Sales (Stage I)

On June 20, 2020, over 150 artists came together virtually in support of Fondation for Medical Research, France.

Our goal is for the arts community to raise money, support, and awareness for the scientific and medical community working relentlessly on treatments and a vaccine for COVID-19. For a 24 hour period on June 20 th , over 150 artists will be live broadcasting via Instagram from their work spaces, studios and the street. Although physically distanced, we will come together to lend our talents and resources to aid in the effort to end the COVID-19 crisis. Across France and the world, artists will be creating live works to be donated to support Fondation pour la Recherche Médicale, France.
