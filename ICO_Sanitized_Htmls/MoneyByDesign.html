UNIVERSAL PAYMENT SYSTEM

Money by Design aims to offer its customers a complete payment interface that integrates the best features of this time.

The payment interface of Money by Design will allow tomorrow small and large companies as well as Administrations and Institutions to have a traceable, transparent, fast and efficient payment system thanks to the blockchain technology.

While current bank payment systems are slow, expensive, risky and only allow to manage fiat money, the payment system of Money by Design is designed to process fiat, crypto and accounting units of value .

This universal payment system will be accessible to consumers by means of a classic payment card:

  • It will give companies the opportunity to offer their customers new means of payment.
  • It will enable governments to implement a more efficient fiscal policy.
  • It will allow development banks to make fund distributions without intermediaries and with better control.

THE BEST OF BOTH WORLDS

Today, Blockchain and Institutions (public, private and social) exist in two separate worlds.

Institutions are skeptical about the Blockchain owing to issues concerning:

  • Its lack of scalability,
  • Its lack of control,
  • Its lack of structure.

Blockchain enthusiasts criticize institutions for:

  • Their highly centralized environment,
  • Their rigidity and slow adaptation pace,
  • Their lack of intelligence and social dimension.

Money By Design wants to gather them together by building on the best of both worlds.

Decentralized architecture

Hybrid structure between public blockchain and classic digital world.

Authority nodes

Possibility of exercising control over the blocks.

Amend function

Error, fraud, disturbances control and correction.

Compliance & transparency

Required by Institutions to enter the world of blockchain.

Environmental concern

Proof of authority doesn’t use classical mining and saves energy.

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    You should find the answers to your questions here. Still need help? Get in touch with us!

    • What is the Smart Tokenization?

      The Smart Tokenization is the ability to create new tokens for third-party looking for the growth of their activity. The aim is to finance economic, social and political actors who request Money By Design’s service offer to tokenize entire sectors of the economy. The intensity of the actors’ demand for the GeoToken lies in the quality of the features available. New token emissions create new trading zones, including in areas where fiat financing is difficult or impossible.

    • What makes Money By Designâs Geotoken (GTK) unique?

      The Geoteoken is the first token to integrate the 7 functionalities and services of the currency, far beyond the means of payment and wallet management. It is a non-speculative monetary tool dedicated to users and fully oriented towards their satisfaction. Its value is based on both economic and social values.

    • What are the economic and social values of the GTK?

      The economic value is based on the value of technological assets and user benefits. In other words, our users will reap more important benefits of using the GTK in their socio-economic relations (ie. they will be able to pay for a good or service, to vote for a decisionâ¦) than with a classic tool.
      The social value is based on the Metcalfe formula and the growth of our users. That means that the more users, the higher the value. This is what a real virtuous network should be.

    • Why do you call the Geotoken an Augmented Cryptocurrency?
      • Saving money (GEO) enables to manage a wallet as a multi-support fund composed of sovereign currencies (fiat) and cryptocurrencies in unlimited number.
      • Consuming (PAY) allows to pay for a good or service with any device (IoT, smartphone, debit card)
      • Voting (VOX) provides the right to participate in community decisions.
      • Measuring (CAN) permits to work on infinitesimal scales and therefore increase the security of the wallet. The token holder is the only one able to recompose the real value of his wallet.
      • Producing (PROD) enables to monetize assets and to guarantee the authenticity of value. It is essential in any artistic industry for example.
      • Marketing (GRANT) allows to make transactions through new models of network exchanges, in particular circular network.
      • Monitoring (LAW) lets you frame and structure all social and economic relations the way you want.
    • How your architecture makes your blockchain different and more secure?

      The technical architecture we chose is a hybrid structured blockchain located between public BC (highly distributed and then out of control) and classic digital world (centralized and regulated). This decentralized structure guarantees authority nodes the possibility of exercising control over the activity of the blocks and a strong regulatory power that will allow institutions to uphold certain rules of compliance and transparency they require to enter the BC universe. It prevents from money laundering and fraud.
      Unlike Bitcoin or Ethereum, this decentralized architecture allows to introduce an Â«amendÂ» function which gives the system the ability to correct itself via the action of the higher nodes.
      Such a function gives the higher nodes a power of control and sanction against the malicious acts.
