General

Why FCC?

Full circle coin is the eco system that covers all kinds of recycling from plastics to paper to metals to anything that can be converted into a production raw material. This eco system where is we close the loop between waste and production. The name full circle coin demonstrates that we are not specific on any particular recycling material, and that we are determined to ensure that the material we collect and process does get used back in a suitable product as part of a full circle of material flow.

How are we different?

We have assembled an experienced multinational management team with international experience in waste and recycling, social and technology sectors. Having operated in multiple countries, we are able to leverage our know how to overcome implementation hurdles, and to develop and implement scalable processes. We understand how to further develop the current recycling process and to further generate value to the various stakeholders. There is waste from wealth, and to deliver value, it is important to ensure it is managed correctly.

How will you implement

We have already identified participants in the recycling process, ranging from collectors to recycling facilities. After the 1st rollout, because we are using a platform and have manuals in place, further expansion is on a plug and play basis.

Index update

Prices of the coins will be set every quarter based on a data from at 3 reputable publicly available sources of recycled raw materials. The index may also change over time to reflect moving up the value chain as more and more material is collected and enabling economies of scale for setting up recycling plants.
