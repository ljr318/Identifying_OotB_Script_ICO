Synergy

Synergy is a peer-to-peer (P2P) energy trading platform that allows for the trading of energy among individual producers of energy, reaping stable revenues to consumers across city-wide energy grids, providing grater energy options at fairer prices.

Electrify Marketplace

Marketplace was designed for the deregulation of Singapore’s energy market to provide residential, commercial and industrial consumers the convenience and freedom to select the most suitable option based on their consumption all on a single platform.
