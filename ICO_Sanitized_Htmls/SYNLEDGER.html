How does SYN works ?

We are committed towards creating a future that swears by the concept of Renewable energy. Based on this common goal, our modus operandi involves creating software units for handling the following markets:

Electric Vehicle Charging

A Peer-to-Peer EV Charging Blockchain based application makes that charge station owners can easily share energy across a unified platform with other EV drivers offering excess energy to those who are in need. Syn Ledger aims to developing a secure, seamless, and extremely smart EV charging arena.

Synergy Trade

Syn Ledger brings a wholesale energy trading marketplace over a blockchain network. The Decentralized platform enables p2p energy transactions from producers to energy buyers, retailers and market makers without the involvement or need of any intermediary by leveraging smart contracts makes the trading of energy extensively cost-effective, quick, transparent, and efficient.

Solar Leasing Platform

Syn Ledger provides a platform for investors to invest in solar cells and rent them to producers to generate, maintain and distribute the generated power from the rented solar cells. The blockchain-enabled platform incentivizes investors for renting solar equipment and other necessary resources while providing them constant ROI on their investment depending on their ownership
