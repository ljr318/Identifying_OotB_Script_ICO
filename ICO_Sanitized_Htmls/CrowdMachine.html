What Can Crowd Machine Do?​

Rapid Application Development Platforms Are Not Created Equal, It’s That Simple.

Legacy Modernization

Legacy modernization with Crowd Machine isn’t about deploying faster hardware, or moving onto a cloud with higher performance, and at reduced costs. Crowd Machine takes it to the next level by giving your company better business agility, endless scalability, complete business transparency and freedom from outdated dependencies. Crowd Machine becomes the centerpiece of your business applications, uniting your systems and data and giving you access and clarity you didn’t know was possible.

Drive IT/Business Collaboration

Collaboration between IT and business lines yields faster and better results. When these teams begin defining requirements, this is also the start of development of the application. While doing design work and logically defining how the app is going to function, IT can start dragging and dropping components onto the pallet and creating functional capabilities that will actually run. Within days, IT can show a working prototype, get immediate feedback from the business line in that development process and quickly move the app to staging and into production.

Application Integration

Crowd Machine gives you the tools to create the framework that helps your business integrate mission-critical systems – such as customer relationship management (CRM), enterprise resource planning software (ERP), business intelligence applications (BI), payroll and human resource systems, and supply chain management (SCM). This ensures that all data and information within the organization is stored correctly, is accessible yet secure, and is transmitted accurately when called upon.

True No-Code Development

Crowd App Studio is a true no-code environment that can address a broad array of needs through a drag-and-drop interface. An app’s business logic is constructed through rule-building within the platform. Patterns, activities, attributes and packages replace traditional code. Integrations to third-party apps or existing code can be achieved with APIs instead of traditional coding. Crowd Machine’s platform supports the creation of comprehensive solutions, from the very simple to the very complex.

Cloud Migration

Crowd Machine provides intelligent cloud migration by shortening the time required to migrate your business to the cloud, and without losing any of the functionality and stability within your existing systems. Crowd Machine can take legacy applications and expose components, or in some cases, entire applications, to the cloud. In doing so, we also can extend the functionality and capability of those applications, and thus enhance productivity and the user experience.

Full-Featured, Cloud-Native PaaS

The Crowd Machine platform has everything you need to create even the most sophisticated applications. It is based on a fully automated, self-contained hosting infrastructure that can be run in a public or private cloud, or on premise. The public cloud option is fully managed by Crowd Machine on your behalf. Build, test and deploy new and innovative solutions faster and easier than ever before on our cloud-native Platform-as-a-Service.

Crowd App Studio

Crowd App Studio is a comprehensive and true no-code development platform that enables rapid creation and iteration of enterprise-class applications and intelligent microservices using a drag-and-drop rules-based visual builder. It reduces development down to a three-stage model that automates and drastically accelerates the development processes to deliver market-ready apps at unprecedented speed.

Your business can increase productivity and innovation with Crowd Machine’s microservice-driven architecture that automates the rapid creation and delivery of functional components and sophisticated applications. Your IT department is able to quickly construct smart services and complete applications in a visual environment, making solutions easy to construct and iterate, quick to test, and ready to be deployed, when and where you need them.

Build With App Studio

Crowd Machine GO

Once applications or microservices have been created in Crowd App Studio, Crowd Machine GO enables the business to snap together these components, along with third-party systems, using our GO Gadget technology. This allows line of business workers to easily create and integrate their own applications on demand, all while remaining compliant with company policies and security protocols.

Your business can integrate systems and data involved in enterprise operations into this single pane of glass for better organizational efficiency at every level. Crowd Machine GO synchronizes data and applications to automate and streamline business processes, delivering real-time data visualization and actionable insights for better business decisions and to maintain a competitive advantage.

Innovate With GO

How Does Crowd Machine Work?

Create Your Microservices In Crowd App Studio

Using Crowd App Studio’s intuitive no-code development environment, your IT professionals quickly construct highly functional components and smart microservices, known as GO Gadgets. IT can also extract and express functionality from your existing legacy systems as GO Gadgets or use RESTful interfaces to bring in third-party app components. GO Gadgets can be anything business users need; for example, a process to add new hires to the payroll system, or a process to authenticate user login to an app. Collaboration between IT professionals and business users results in self-service, customized, flexible, and compliant solutions that solve business problems, the first time.

Build Out Your Crowd Machine GO Gadget Library​

Your GO Gadget Library will continue to grow as your IT department creates new GO Gadgets. GO Gadgets can be stand-alone highly functional components, intelligent and aware units of capability, complete business applications, or anything you can imagine. They are reusable, so once a GO Gadget is created, it can serve many applications.  From the GO Gadget library, the business line selects, self-serves, and snaps together GO Gadgets to immediately render compliant applications that meet any and all business line requirements. Unlike other technologies, Crowd Machine GO gets bigger, faster, and better as time goes on.

Customized Solutions For Your Business Line Users

Within Crowd Machine GO, the business user easily selects IT-created GO Gadgets from the library and quickly snaps them together to make their very own user-specific custom solutions. A different user can select different GO Gadgets, rendering a different solution. Crowd Machine GO allows business users to create powerful applications in minutes – making it faster and easier than any other approach on the market. GO is a digital experience capable of integrating all the applications, data and systems used on a daily basis to deliver efficiencies across the entire enterprise.
