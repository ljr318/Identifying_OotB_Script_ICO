Introduction to Root BlockChain

Root Architecture

Blockchain is an exciting new technology that has the potential to disrupt industries and at the same time presents new opportunities similar to other platforms like Bitcoin trader . However, blockchain has its share of drawbacks, namely:
1. Complexity
2. The blockchain size - this directly affects the speed of operations on the blockchain
3. Transaction data size - this will add to the blockchain size as well as result in increased transaction costs.

Concept :

Root Blockchain solves the above problems with its unique offering - Blockchain as a Service (BaaS) - the first hybrid blockchain with flexible options for both enterprise and retail customers and also to the companises involved in developing blockchain apps such as bitcoin trader ..

Approach :

Root Blockchain is completely API driven, which means that businesses can reap the benefits of blockchain technology without the accompanying complexity. Just connect your existing business to the Root API and you are good to go.
