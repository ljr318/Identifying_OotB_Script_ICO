Why Xtock?

Xtock is blockchain based OTC Market Finance Network Platform that connects companies,
trustees agencies, and investors so that investors may exchange STO tokens for OTC stocks.
Xtock will continue to create a transparent and safe next generation OTC financial transaction ecosystem
that can be financed more easily.

  • Transaction Transparency

    Xtock is able to record and manage
    the price, transaction history, contracts,
    and owner information of a company`s
    underlying assets in real-time.
    Smart contracts for transparent investments,
    and to efficiently deal with all transactions
    via a distributed processing system.

  • Re-evaluation of
    Corporate Values

    Xtock manages potential risks
    by relying on objective metrics analysis
    as well as multi-variable evaluation
    using the XEM (Xtock Evaluation Matrix).

  • Secure Transactions

    Xtock separates the asset and contract
    features of tokens to protect users
    from the risk of duplicate sales.
    A vulnerability in OTC transactions,
    Xtock allows smart contracts to trade
    digital tokens transparently, securely,
    and privately with unlisted companies.

  • Stock Conversion

    Xtock protocol is the key that connects
    companies, trustees agencies,
    and investors so that investors
    may exchange to OTC stock.
    Credited investors may apply for
    stock conversion in Xtock platform.
