Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any special offer for contributors?

The first 999 people to invest at least 3 ETH will have a lifetime 0% fee on the CMA marketplace along with a founder badge.

What is the minimum deposit amount and which coins do you accept?

We are accepting only Ethereum (ETH). For Pre-sale it is 1 ETH, for the main stage - 0.1 ETH.

What will happen with unsold CMA tokens?

The remaining tokens will be destroyed - "burnt".

What wallets support ERC20?

We recommend to use https://www.myetherwallet.com and other ERC20 wallets. Please note, when you participate in our sale, you are not able to do so directly from an exchange.

Do you have a bounty campaign?

Yes, to participate in it, please follow instructions and register as publisher here .

When and where will I receive tokens for a bounty campaign?

Bounty tokens distribution will start 2 weeks after the end of main token sale. You will can withdraw tokens from your CMA marketplace account.

Do you have a referral program?

Yes, we have a referral program. You can participate in it by registering on CMA marketplace. After registration you will find a referral link you can use to earn CMA tokens by referring new users to CMA marketplace.
