GET STARTED

We are a next-generation digital asset exchange working to bridge the gaps between cryptocurrency and legacy finance. You buy and sell your bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies on our exchange. We offer our services in 185 countries and 43 U.S. states ...

Our primary goal is to help our users build their financial future. We accomplish this by increasing our trader’s exposure to a diverse range of assets, providing them with advanced trading tools, and giving direct & consistent support that demonstrates our care for the well-being of the community and our commitment to helping traders improve their life.

As a global entity, we take every possible measure to ensure that our exchange complies with all applicable laws, regulations, and licensing requirements. This allows us to serve users from around the world.

What sets Beaxy apart from other platforms?

The popularity and demand for cryptocurrency are still growing. That is why Beaxy ensures that every user can benefit from the following features that make our exchange stand out from the competition:

  • We know that most of our users spend a lot of time away from their computers. To ensure that they never miss an important move in the market, we have built a robust and easy to use mobile app that is available on the iOS and Android app stores.
  • We offer the highest grade of trading technology in the world, helping your orders get processed in the most efficient way possible and maximizing your ability to be successful.
  • We not only help you trade, but we also help you understand. Free educational resources, loyalty rewards, one of the largest global communities, and 24/7 support are just the beginning.
  • Customer account security is Beaxy’s number one priority. There are many levels of security to prevent phishing, data tampering, and wallet hacks
  • Quick and easy registration. We know that the registration and KYC process can be very frustrating with other exchanges. To alleviate these issues for our customers, we utilize AI-driven verification which can be completed in less than five minutes.
  • Fiat is coming to Beaxy. Shortly after the rollout of our new platform, we will implement fiat pairs that will allow traders to instantly convert their fiat holdings to crypto and vice versa

Beaxy is an exchange with a remarkable future!

Beaxy is here to form a long-term relationship with our traders. We are confident that Beaxy has everything you need to have the best trading experience. We’re so much more than an exchange. We offer custody of coins to improve security, tax reports & trade calculators to make your life easier, and partnerships with banks & other providers to create an inclusive financial ecosystem for you.
