ABOUT US

DES – Decentralised Escrow Service (Descrow) is the first in the world and unique by its nature decentralised escrow platform for investments.

Our project mission:

  • To make safer invest for all participants.
  • To eliminate the excessive distrust of the potential investors to the new startups.
  • Give a boost to the further invest market growth.
  • To integrate our idea as a versatile instrument into all applicable investments.
