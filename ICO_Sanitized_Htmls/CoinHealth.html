CROWDSALE (200,000,000)

Crowdsale begins on the 21st of February 2018 at $0.35 and will continue for 60 days with the sales price of Coinhealth tokens increasing by $0.01 per day for 60 days or until the 21st of April 2018 at a final sales price of $0.95 per token.
