V-ID & AmSpec

AmSpec inspects batches of crude oil and determines the value by means of laboratory research. The result is recorded in an inspection report. The parties that subsequently trade the oil base their prices on the inspection report.

This direct link to high value commodities makes these reports vulnerable to manipulation.

V-ID's certifies AmSpec's reports so any recipient of (a copy of) a report can check its authenticity in 5 seconds on any internet-connected device.
