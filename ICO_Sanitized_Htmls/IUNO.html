Breaking banks with chains

It’s time to break the banks with chains. Centralized banks impose fees after fees. They dictate where money goes and how it is spent without thinking about you, the consumer.

Millions of people around the globe are unbanked because they cannot afford the hefty fees. Did you know that when a transaction goes through a bank account and there are insufficient fees, the bank hits that person with even more charges? Those charges make it even harder to get ahead financially. This is why so many choose to be unbanked than to deal with the centralized banks and their money-making schemes.

The solution

At IUNO, we have a solution. Decentralized banking as a service allows for P2P lending while being able to pay for anything, anywhere using a transparent ledger. Additionally, both Fiat and digital currency can be used.

Combines Fiat currency with cryptocurrency

The beauty of IUNO is that it combines Fiat currency (US Dollars, Euros, etc.) with cryptocurrency. You can choose to have both within your decentralized wallet. If you need to pay someone, you can do so with any form of currency that you have. If a person only takes Fiat, the conversion is made transparently within your IUNO wallet using our Stablecoins. As long as the person you’re paying is within the network, you pay no fees. Additionally, the transaction is completed almost instantaneously. With your current bank, it might take up to 7 days for payment transfers to go through – and that’s simply unacceptable!

A small, transactional redemption fee for withdrawals into Fiat would be charged as well as if you pay a vendor outside of the IUNO ecosystem.
