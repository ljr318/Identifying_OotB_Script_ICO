Selecting a Online Pharmacy in the UK

An online pharmacy is where a company dispensing medicines operates over the internet. Once the order has been completed online, the orders are sent out through the mail using shipping companies or couriers such as Royal Mail or DPD. It's important to choose legitimate internet pharmacies (which should be in the same country as the person ordering), to ensure patient safety. Look out for the pharmacy's accreditation and regulatory certificates; UK Meds is registered and legally operating in the UK, regulated by the GPhC and MHRA.

When you choose a pharmacy online, select one that has fast shipping. In most cases, we can ship your medication to you within 24 hours (on most days). Some days you may have to wait an extra day to receive your prescription medicine, based on approval and cutoff times.
