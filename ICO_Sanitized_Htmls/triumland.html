Triumland Park

Property Development Project

Triumland Park is a fertile Nature Tourism Area, located in an environment supporting the growth and beauty of nature. It has length of 40 to 200 hectares and is located on Klabat mountain, North Minahasa, North Sulawesi, Indonesia. Total capital for development is estimated at Rp.1.500.000.000.000 which will be built under the Build-Operate-Transfer(BOT) agreement system between Triumland Corp and the investors, with a share composition ownership in Triumland Park, 60% is for the Investor and 40% for Triumland Corp.

TRDSHARE

Cryptoshare Development Services

TRDS Cryptoshare (TRDShare) is the first investment property shares that is based on blockchain. Our team combines the cryptoshare services with the property development.  The share composition ownership in Triumland Park property project, 60% is for the Investor and 40% for Triumland Corp. The 40% of Triumland Corp shares will be in the form of cryptoshare with a total of 1,000,000,000 shares. With the development of the Triumland Park property project, it will be able to have an impact on rising cryptoshare prices in the future.
