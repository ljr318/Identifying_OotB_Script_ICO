The GetDoIt Service

The GetDoIt platform is the first internet portal worldwide that covers all spheres of services connected with moving. Our service is developed as an intermediate between a Customer and a Service Provider.

Overview

The GetDoIt platform functions as a mediator between a Customer who needs definite services and a Contractor who provides them.

Our portal covers all services connected with moving services: transportation and logistics, assembling of furniture and household appliances, renovation of apartments and houses, repair and maintenance.

The GetDoIt platform

Is designed to be user-friendly and simple, a customer can choose between moving or a variety of services. The highlight of our Project is that GetDoIt is a blockchain-based platform, which provides both security and transparency.

Reasons you need our token

Being unique, secure, progressive, and created according to the Market needs, our project will develop fast. Our service will launch all over the world and quickly gain success. In such a way, the token price is planned to go up soon.

Legal. Responsibility. Perspectives

The GetDoIt service is completely transparent and legal. The platform starts in 4 countries: Australia, Germany, Austria, and Switzerland, with the perspective of being launched worldwide.
