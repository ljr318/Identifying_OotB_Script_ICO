How does it work?

  • 1 Registration

    Users register on Roobee via the mobile app or web service. They can choose the preferred way to use Roobee or go both ways.

  • 2 KYC

    Users will need to pass a KYC procedure to get a personal Roobee ID granting secure and decentralized data storage.

  • 3 Profiling

    Users can use the platform's profiling functionality to determine their investment profiles. RoobeeScore, equipped with AI and Machine Learning, will take into account all the details and user's goals and will provide the user with a tailored investment portfolio.

  • 4 Investing

    Users will be able to make payments with a bank card and cryptocurrencies. It will be possible to link a bank card or use RoobeeWallet and invest in automatic mode without any hassle.

  • 5 Token Usage

    5a. Spending

    ROOBEE tokens may also be used to get priority access to high-demand investment products with a limited allocation.

    5b. Burning

    If the user has used Roobee tokens, for instance, to reduce fees, Roobee may burn part of those tokens to reduce total circulation.

  • 6 Distribution

    User funds will be automatically distributed among investment products: from venture funds and real estate to crypto, ETFs, and stocks. All transactions and movements will be recorded on the blockchain to ensure transparency.

  • 7 Final step

    As a result of the successful investment, users will receive digitized assets that symbolize investment products they have invested in. All digitized assets will be safely stored in RoobeeWallet.

Roobee platform features

All markets in one app:

Roobee platform will give everyone an access to the world of investments. Users will be able to build their own investment portfolio consisting of the products from various markets in a matter of minutes. For example, one can invest starting from $10 into stocks, ETF, venture markets, precious metals, cryptocurrencies, IEO, and IPO.

Transparent statistics:

Each user's investment in investment products will be recorded in blockchain, proving that it is real and belong to this particular user.

Low entry threshold:

Each person will be able to invest starting from $10 in investment products that are only available to big institutional players, qualified investors, millionaires and funds. The parity of investment opportunities is the core of our concept.
