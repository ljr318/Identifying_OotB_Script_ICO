Bartcoin token
Bartcoin (BARC) is a protocol based on the Ethereum Blockchain (ERC20), where retailers can reward buyers for multiple behaviors like spending money in their stores and accepting to share their information against Bartcoin. Shoppers can then spend their Bartcoin tokens at all merchants in the network for future purchases or trade them on the Bartwallet platform.
