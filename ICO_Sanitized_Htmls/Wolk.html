Fight COVID-19 guided by causal models of disease transmission and AI-powered devices.

Six foot rules and masks were the start. Finish the fight with location-specific safety policies, guided by devices that monitor people, temperature, humidity, air flow, and mask usage. Create safe social distancing practices unique to your people and building environment.

SafetyBands or SafetyCards are distributed to each person to ensure that everyone maintains a safe distance between each other in your environment. If users get too close to each other, they are alerted by the device in use.
