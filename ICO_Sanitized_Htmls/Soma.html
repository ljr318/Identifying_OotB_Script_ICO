Our Mission

SOMA platform utilize blockchain technology to combine transparency, anonymity and security to generate immutable and independently verifiable certificates and preserve your products' originality, provenance and ownership .

EFFORTLESS

We provide branded certificates with the full power of blockchain, without installation, cryptocurrencies or variable gas fee complexities.

INSTANT VALIDITY

With a simple drag & drop, anyone holding your certificate can instantly verify its authenticity from anywhere in the world.

SECURITY TO LAST

By using blockchain we secure your certificates' fingerprint within most powerful computer network in the world.
