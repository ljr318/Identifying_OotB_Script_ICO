About Cryptobuyer

As the adoption of Crypto continues to grow, the demand for access to Crypto will grow along with it. A world where people are free of traditional institutions to transact, and one where everyone equal access to the network. Cryptobuyer will help solve the problems that stand in the way of mass adoption by giving people a way to use their cryptocurrencies by providing merchants with PoS solutions, and providing the people access to a vast Crypto ATM Network that they can use to buy and sell.

Making the difference in the Crypto World

Cryptobuyer plans on being an industry leader, with every intention of being apart of the future growth of cryptocurrencies. We plan on:

* Providing an alternative payment method for businesses.

* Offering financial inclusion and helping fight against inflation and economic collapse by giving people easy access to cryptocurrencies.

* Giving people control over their finances, instead of trusting institutions.

* Expanding across Latin America before expanding globally.

Problems & Solutions

The biggest problem in cryptocurrency is mass adption. Cryptobuyer aims to be a leader in crypto comunity, creating solutions focused on usability so mass adoption occurs.

Problem

The Gap
  • To better define the complexity in cryptocurrencies, it's important to realize the difference in economic models that a represented by traditional markets that many are used to, versus the new market that few have had any experience in. The key to bridge this gap, is to create a solutions that simplifies the new market and allows tradicional market users to easily adapt.

Solution

Bridging the Gap
  • The easiest way to bridge the gap between Traditional markets and Crypto markets, is to have it available to everyone in a way that allows the to buy, sell and use crypto very easily. With a vast ATM Network to buy and sell crypto, accompanied with a PoS system to allow merchants to accept and sell crypto, people will finally have a way to add crypto to their daily lives.

Problem

Usability
  • A major problem with most cryptocurrencies is that they have little to no real life use case. Most of the time, these cryptocurrencies are only used for purchase of another cryptocurrency or held in hopes of inflation. This has become one of the biggest hurdles in the crypto community, and is at the forefront of mass adoption.

Solution

Access to Crypto
  • Our goal over the next 5 years, we aim to provide access and usability all over North, Central, and South America by creating a vast network of crypto ATMs and POS machines. This will enable the individual to buy, sell, and spend their cryptocurrencies freely.
