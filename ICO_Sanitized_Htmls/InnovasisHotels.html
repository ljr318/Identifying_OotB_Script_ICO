PAWEL ALVA NAZARUK

CEO

Innovator, inventor, entrepreneur, designer. Pawel holds many patents to his name. Patents on tech solutions, as well as unique designs. Paweł started his first company at the age of 19 and successfully sold it by the age of 23. His early success opened up a whole new world of golden opportunities. Pawel has traveled to 46 countries, and based on the observed need and opportunity, he created this idea of a new type of hotels. Pawel previously ran a social impact tech company from Palo Alto, in Silicon Valley. His client list consisted of companies like eBay, LinkedIn, and Crunch Fitness. Pawel is also the author of a bestselling book, Investment Awakening. It remains on bestseller lists in Poland even 5 years after the original publication. He is now fully devoted to his newest innovation project, Innovasis Hotels.

DAVID MYSLIWIEC

Project Manager

David was one of the first investors in Innovasis Hotels, and by showcasing his exceptional dedication and business knowledge early on, he joined the company management board as a Project Manager. He has several years of experience in the cryptocurrency market and has combined this knowledge with his management skills to help drive the company forward. David is responsible for coordinating the project and contacting organizations, institutions, and companies that we are interested in cooperating with.

AMJAD OMAR

Desalination Plant Expert

Amjad has over 25 years of experience in the development, engineering and project management of water and wastewater treatment systems, resource recovery, and value-added solutions. He developed and pursued a patent for a new process for the treatment of black liqor and recovery of saccharides from non-wood paper mills. Amjad holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Jordan, where he ranked first among chemical engineering graduates. He is currently with Enviromatch, an environmental engineering firm that specializes in the planning, design, and construction of water and wastewater facilities. Enviromatch, Inc. team members have developed several water treatment desalination projects in the Middle East, North America, Asia, and South America.

JASON CARRIER

Modular Farms Solution Expert

Jason Carrier is a trained chef and world traveler who is deeply passionate about finding sustainable solutions for the global food crisis. Before founding Local Food Champions, Jason was the Director of sales at Modular Farms and was promoted to Director of business development at NHXT INC 6 months later. In his new position he was instrumental in the development of technology, streamlining workflows and the application of irrigation and HVAC systems within the farms. He has globally coordinated manufacturing teams for the deployment of multiple farms across North America, Asia, and Australia. His deep knowledge of food production and his ongoing accomplishments as he drives Local Food Champions forward through the ever-evolving technologies of sustainable agriculture, make him an invaluable asset in this growing industry. In other words: a true Local Food Champion.
