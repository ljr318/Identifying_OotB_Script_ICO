The Elesol Alliance
Starting from a shared vision of natural capital, the contribution of both public and private approaches to the development of natural capital assessment and accounting, XEL’s has partnered with Elesol Inc. a Japanese Energy solutions provider that has created an innovative electromagnetic harmonic attenuation plate that promotes reductions in energy consumption by up to 20%.
The outcome of this alliance will be to boost progress on assessment and integration of natural capital into decision making in an aligned, harmonized and more efficient way through collaboration and a common understanding and language. This will be a valuable contribution to both companies’ processes including the implementation of the common sustainable development goals.
