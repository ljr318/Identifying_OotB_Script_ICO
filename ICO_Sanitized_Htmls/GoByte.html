What is GoByte Pay?

GoByte Pay makes using cryptocurrency easier than ever.

GoByte Pay is more than just a payment processing solution that allows merchants and professional service providers to accept cryptocurrency payments for goods and services. Integrated into your GoByte Web3 Wallet, GoByte Pay provides a suite of tools that allow you to invoice, send, receive and manage cryptocurrency payments directly from your GoByte Web3 Wallet.

Suitable for personal and business applications, GoByte Pay makes using cryptocurrency for goods, services and cross border transactions easier than ever.

GoByte Pay has low transaction fees (priced lower than credit cards, PayPal and Square), payment processing times averaging 1.3 seconds per transaction and integrated tools which make it as powerful as it is easy to use for businesses and their customers who want to pay with GoByte (GBX), Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH) and other cryptocurrencies.

Earn GBX with GoByte Masternodes

GoByte Masternode holders earn GBX coins as block rewards for verifying transactions and helping secure the GoByte Network. Simply put, as a reward for securing the network and verifying transactions, GoByte Masternodes literally mint new GBX coins for their owners. GBX coins earned may be spent, traded, liquidated or accumulated into new masternodes to compound earnings.  Additionally, GoByte Masternode holders earn the right to vote (once per Masternode) on proposals in GoByte's Governance.

As an additional bonus, GoByte Masternode holders may withdraw GBX coins with no fees whatsoever.
