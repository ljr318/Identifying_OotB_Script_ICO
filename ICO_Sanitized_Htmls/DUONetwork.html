Learn from the Past

Mature market instruments are essential for any financial market to help manage volatility, increase liquidity, and hedge downside risks. Yet, current crypto-derivative marketplaces are far from perfect:

  • OTC derivatives lack proper industry standards
  • Centralized exchanges entail security, transparency, and position transferability concerns
