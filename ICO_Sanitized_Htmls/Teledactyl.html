How Teledactyl Can Help Me

Who Are We

Teledactyl helps organizations break down barriers to care with customized technology solutions. We help underserved communities via telemedicine and other health technology through a network of securely connected organizations. Our mission is to achieve health equity across the United States by reducing costs and increasing access to quality care.

Our Technology

Our integrative approach uses blockchain technology and decentralized applications (DApps). Our HIPAA-compliant backend technology is tested and has two patents surrounding interoperability and patient matching.

Access

Health isn’t just physical; it’s the “conditions in the places where people live, learn, work and play” (HealthyPeople.gov) . One driver of the gap in health equity is lack of access. Let the Teledactyl and Teledactyl eVisit , our telemedicine platform, help you expand your reach and impact with virtual touch points, in addition to face-to-face, particularly in rural and underserved areas.
