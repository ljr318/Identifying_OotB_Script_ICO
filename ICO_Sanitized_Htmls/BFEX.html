Key Features

Thank you, investors and future users, for your valuable support to BFEX. We appreciate your trust and opportunity for us to develop a platform that will tremendously impact those whose lives are in need of new beginning.

Decentralized Scoring to revolutionize the traditional financial accessibility limited by Credit Bureau and informational scarcity.

BFEX’s hybrid Decentralized Scoring system is powered by Blockchain, AI, Machine Learning and Behavioural Sciences. The alternative scoring system that allows the science of psychology to invigorate the ability of peer-to-peer lending to another level of disruptive technology. Both the online behaviour and offline documents are required in order to validate consumer’s identity and scoring.

Social Capital is the new collateral

It is a fact that peer pressure and connections around an individual affect their behaviour. The repayment rate for nano-finance increases by having peer pressure as one of the factors. Truly decentralized is when the power is in the hands of users, not the platform.

One step at a time, KYC to start lending/borrowing

BFEX’s KYC only requires the essential personal identity documentation and on-time utility payment to verify user’s identity and repayment prediction analysis.

Hyperledger Fabric to protect customer’s privacy

Hyperledger Fabric offers the semi-transparent exposure of information to the public which BFEX implements into our system, in order to protect our user’s anonymity.

Being motivated is all you need to enhance scoring with BFEX

The first and the only platform in the world which enables users to use social network and boost their credit scores.

Cashback with Every Payment

BFEX connects with merchant partners to allow more liquidity of BFEX tokens and to encourage the wide acceptance of BFEX tokens into utility spectrum.

BFEX Application

Peer-to-Peer Lending Platform: A lending platform which connects the lenders and the borrowers, allowing them to interact and agree on the terms and conditions of the loan, such as the interest rate and payment due date, all by themselves.

How it works?

While banking institutions are restricted under traditional loaning systems, BFEX is determined to develop nano-finance services to meet the consumers’ demands worldwide.

Everyone can join

With BFEX’s Decentralized Scoring, people will gain access to essential fundings more effectively.

Blockchain and Smart Contract

With Blockchain and Smart Contract, BFEX system is transparent and highly secured in the field of cross-border currency transactions.

Set your own interest rate

The lenders and the borrowers will set their own interest rates. The information of the loan will be automatically recorded on Hyperledger Fabric.

Cash Back 1-5%

The BPAY Wallet allows users to purchase goods and services from BFEX business partners with BFEX tokens. Payments will be counted towards users’ credit scores. Cash back will be transferred to the users’ wallet at the end of each month.
