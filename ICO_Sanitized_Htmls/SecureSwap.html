Solutions

Problems & Solutions

The problems with centralized platforms are well known by the crypto-currency trading community.
We provide simple and innovative solutions.

Problems

Both centralized and decentralized Exchanges suffer from significant disadvantages:

  • Centralized Exchanges:
    • Transfer of ownership of digital assets to these Exchanges, which serve as trusted third parties.
    • This centralization of assets is very attractive for hackers.
    • Risk of sudden closure, making the assets deposited there inaccessible.
    • Frequent unavailability of services during periods of market panic.
  • Decentralized Exchanges:
    • Insufficient liquidity. This further slows down the execution of orders.
    • Their order books and matching systems are executed by smart contracts (On-Chain). This slows down order execution and adds network costs.
    • Choice of crypto-currencies limited to the Ethereum network (Ether and Tokens).
    • No exchange from/to fiat currencies.
  • And overall:
    • Lack of ergonomics, some of which are puzzles to use, even for an experienced trader.
    • No margin-trading.

Solutions

Secure Swap is a cryptocurrency exchange ecosystem, built around a decentralized service (P2P), a client application for trading, and optional micro services to take advantage of the new options offered by this new network.

Secure Swap provides both the advantages of centralized and decentralized exchanges:

  • High liquidity.
  • Quick matching of orders, free of charge.
  • Enhanced security \"by design\".
  • No intermediaries holding the users' crypto-currencies.
  • Ergonomics inspired by the best stock market trading software.
  • Scalability (P2P network) guaranteeing service availability.
  • Potential support for all cryptocurrencies.
  • Margin trading.
Key Strengths

Our advantages

Main advantages of Secure Swap over its competitors

High liquidity

Secure Swap provides very high liquidity. Thanks to our arbitration system, liquidity is supported by that of centralized exchangers.
In the future, liquidity will also be supported by the sharing of order books between DEX exchanges.

Fast order execution

Its architecture and operation make Secure Swap unaffected by the speed of execution of smart contracts on the various blockchains.
Each instance of the client application, Secure Trade, performs the matching of its own orders against the complete order book.
Combined with the available liquidity, this ensures us a speed of execution comparable to centralized exchangers.

Security by Conception

System secured by design, with detection of hacking attempts, alert and automatic shutdown of the parties concerned.
Real decentralized system, users remain in possession of their crypto-currencies until the time to perform an exchange.
No concentration of crypto-currencies, which is a target of choice for hackers in centralized (or decentralized but which keep the wallets) exchangers.

Controled and Operated by Community

Its distributed and community aspect, makes the service independent of the existence of the company that created it and does not need it to function.
An open system that allows everyone to control the code, operation and security.

A Extensible Exchange

Due to its open source and modular nature, users who so wish can add new crypto-currency support to the exchanger and also crypto-currency to fiat currency exchanges of their choice.

Revenus Sharing for investors

Those who support the operation of the service, by operating parts of the system (nodes), are rewarded by earning a portion of the fees paid by traders on exchanges.
Permits to initiate a legal and professional activity of exchange of crypto-currency into fiat currencies wherever it is legal in the world (nodes of conversion into fiat currencies).

A low-cost exchanger

Secure Swap only involves smart contracts in the event of an anomaly. An exchange that proceeds without malfunction nor hacking does not use any functionality from the smart contract.
Thus, the placement of orders, order modifications, order cancellations, do not generate any cost for the user, unlike most DEXs. The platform also does not take withdrawal fees, since it does not have wallets.
In the end, with its single fee of 0.15% on the execution of an order (decreasing according to volumes), Secure Swap will be as competitive as the centralized exchangers.

Ergonomics

The client application, Secure Trade, offers advanced trading tools, which are common in the world of traditional trading (stock markets, futures markets...), but still not available on current platforms.
The user interfaces are designed to be simple to use, accessible to all, in order to offer optimal ergonomics. A simplified mode is available to exchange and send crypto-currencies as easily as possible. In this mode, simple orders (market, limit, stop) are made easier by assisted entry.

Advanced Trading

Secure Trade's user interfaces offer an advanced mode that activates all of Secure Trade's sophisticated trading tools.
This includes money management tools, all advanced trading orders (multiple orders), scalping support, automatic trading (with strategy editor).
The advanced mode also allows access to margin trading, with information from each supplier node, such as: its continuous connection time, prices, pairs offered for margin trading, etc.

I'm a trader

I download the Secure Trade application and install it on my computer, Windows, Mac, Linux, or on my smartphone, Android or Apple.

  • it serves as proof of stake. The people who operate the nodes of the network must assign a certain amount of tokens to it. This quantity is directly included in the calculation of the rewards they will receive as a reward.
  • it serves as a lever for margin trading.
  • Like blockchain mining, the exploitation of Secure Swap nodes generates rewards for each exchange made by traders.

The $1 Token

Proof of stake

In addition to being an instrument of speculation,
the $1 token is the way to strengthen the $2 community.

Proof of stake

Exchangeable on traditional exchange platforms, the $1 token is the proof of stake that guarantees the functioning of the network nodes. Traders will be free to speculate on the price of the $1 token.

Its main function is to serve as proof of stake for the $2 ecosystem.
It thus allows NodeOwners to withdraw a source of income. NodeOwners operate gateway nodes or fiat currency conversion nodes. They are rewarded by earning a portion of the fees paid by traders on their trades. These exchange fees are fully redistributed to the NodeOwners, in proportion to the tokens allocated to the node. The $1 token is therefore a major source of income for all those who invest.

Example :
• A NodeOwner (John) operates a node-gateway to Ethereum.
• He allocates 100 tokens for the operation of this node.
• If other NodeOwners (Paul, Max, Jim) also operate Ethereum gateway nodes, and all of these nodes have 1000 tokens allocated for this,
then our NodeOwner John will receive 10% (100/1000) of the fees collected on all exchanges involving Ethereums.
• If John has other $1 tokens, and he operates other nodes connecting to other blockchains, NEO for example, then he will also receive his share of the NEO blockchain exchange fees.

This blockchain allocation system encourages NodeOwners to make their nodes work for blockchains where few nodes are in operation, which tends to reinforce system redundancy and availability.
