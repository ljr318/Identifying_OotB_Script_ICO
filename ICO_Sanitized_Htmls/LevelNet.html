ABOUT
LEVELNET

Cyber security still has limited possibilities today. Not all existing programs immediately react to the appearance of viruses, wasting time searching for viruses in the system. Most viruses use this “window of time” and infect more devices. LevelNet has long solved this problem by optimizing virus search and destruction processes.

As new and improved cybersecurity systems become available, they integrate seamlessly into the LevelNet network, thus providing complete protection for all components.

If you’re a fan of new revolutionary technologies, and you’re interested in learning about the Cyber Security internals, then take a look at our White Paper!

PROBLEM

Once the computer age began, so did malware. The more technology developed, the greater the complexity of virus attacks, which were aimed at harming our personal data. Thus, traditional antivirus programs did not have time to develop in tandem with emerging problems from viruses, so they did not keep up sufficiently with the constant influx of malware.

It is no secret that different antivirus services detect the same problem at different times. After finding new threats, it is necessary to release a new update to the security system, which of course will not take long, which means that many users are left alone, with new viruses and without protection for some period of time. First of all, all programs should be informed about new threats so that they can be quarantined. The next task for developers is to create an update for clients so that they can find an updated attack library. In this case, the client must accept the program update before their computer is protected at this point.

This repetitive chronology has resulted in an almost 100% reduction in the ability to provide updated protection for each individual consumer on a global scale.

HOW IT WORKS

Posted: 2018-02-19

LevelNet is a modern information security network. Connected users exchange information about new viruses detected on their PC and any other computer threats. It produces everything in real time with other users who are currently connected to the network, thus preventing further malware attacks.

Threats are detected as a result of monitoring the reaction of malware protection systems that are installed on your PC. This incident is analyzed by the client software and other analysis tools that are located on the user’s device and beyond.
