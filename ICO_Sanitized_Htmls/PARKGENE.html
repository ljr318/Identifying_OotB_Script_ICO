How it works

List a spot

​Use the PARKGENE Application to list your available spot.

Accept a Booking

​Once your parking spot is listed, you’re notified when a driver wants to book it. All that remains is your approval to rent out your parking spot

Get Paid

Once you confirm the reservation, PARKGENE handles the secure financial transaction and you receive the parking fee directly in your GENE wallet, the companion app for PARKGENE.

Find a spot

​Enter your destination, compare parking spots and prices near where you want to park and you’ll find a number of parking spots listed by PARKGENE parking spot owners

Book your Spot

Browse through available parking spots and select the best one. Book a parking spot and pay using your GENE Wallet.

Park

Use the application to navigate to your parking spot at the time of your booking and park safely and conveniently.
