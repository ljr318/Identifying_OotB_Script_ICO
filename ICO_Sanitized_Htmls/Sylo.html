What is Sylo?
Sylo is a decentralised ecosystem made up of Apps, the Network itself, and a Digital Token used to incentivise parties for providing services to the network. Sylo exists to free us from needing to rely on a central company for things like communication, storage and payments. Instead, now everyone can provide these services to eachother, and earn for doing so.
