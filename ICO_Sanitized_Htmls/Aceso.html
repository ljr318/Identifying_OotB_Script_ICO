FAQ

What is ACESO Network?

The aim of ACESO Network is to decrease the expenses of protecting a computer from malware to a minimum, where users will no longer have to pay for software, but for a service instead. The created platform would allow regular users to fix their malware issues in a much cheaper way, while malware specialists would earn income by providing malware samples/helping others solve their malware problems. It is a win-win situation for everyone.

Is ACESO a working business, if yes where can I see it?

As mentioned before – ACESO is based on the MVP – commercially available WiperSoft Antimalware (wipersoft.com), which is already operating in +100 countries, with +1M users and 950% sales grown in 2017.

  • Real and successful business;

Why is Aceso better than regular antivirus?

Our users can pay for a computer fix, instead of software/update. This will decrease their antimalware protection costs by around 80% in a year (Current market average is around $30, our solution will cost around $7 per year, unless you are keen to explore suspicions files and websites – but will never exceed $10). There is no other model like this in the market now.

If you have to wait for analysts to find and resolve one malware issue, why shouldn’t you use existing affordable anti-malware services?

To put it simply, the way antimalware protection works is that you need to find a solution to every new malware. If the malware is new and unique, none of the existing services will have a fix for it. Once the solution for that malware is found and the first infected computer is fixed, all other infected computers can be fixed in seconds. We have been in this industry for more than 10 years and have seen plenty of examples of premium vendors not being able to fix malware issues. Users could have 2 or 3 established anti-malware programs installed, none of which could help them with particular malware. We would analyse that specific threat and prepare the solution. Our proposed model will make this process much faster as we’ll empower the community.
