What is AIQ?

AIQ is a decentralised network for repositories of code libraries, tools, algorithms and datasets that collectively contribute toward building up augmented intelligence applications.

AIQ’s holistic and community-led approach allows users to discover other ideas similar to their own, connect with their peers and further enhance their existing work or even develop further ideas.
