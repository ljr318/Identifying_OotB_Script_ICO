What is CraftR?

CraftR is a marketplace for the future of trading digital creative assets and freelancer services powered by the blockchain.

The project was born to bring the e-commerce to the Web 3.0 world through a decentralized platform that will attract freelancers, programmers, designers, and musicians into the new world of cryptocurrencies and they will be in the right place to sell their skills. Customers of every kind of experience level will reach their desired products through a user-friendly online system.

CraftR marketplace marketplace

Once you’ve entered into our platform, you will be able to trade any kind of digital asset through a P2P system, allowing you to keep total control of your finance.

Anyone with an idea can buy & sell through CraftR marketplace that will offer all the necessary tools and resources, helping people to develop their desired projects. The store will offer a wide choice of products, like graphic design, sound design or scripting resources. From video-editing to game development, customers will be able to choose their needs from our storage.

Frequently asked questions FAQS

Below we’ve provided informations about the ICO, CraftR and its token. If you have any other questions, please get in touch using the contact form below.

What is CraftR?

CraftR - is a unique platform; that is a secure, transparent and easy accessible platform, and completely disrupting the way customers buy and sell digital creativity.

What kind of products will you offer?

The marketplace will offer a huge amount of digital items & skills made available by vendors and freelancers. Such as scripts, templates, 3D models, illustrations, video editing components, and music.

Where is the team placed?

The foundation is placed in Switzerland but we are an international team from America, Europe, and Asia.

What is the supply of tokens?

The initial supply is 33.3 millions CRAFTR and 66.7 millions will be generated during the POS phase (3+ years duration).

What is POS and how can I participate on it?

Proof-Of-Stake (POS) is a way to earn tokens through staking. You can follow this link for a correct procedure.

Where can I deposit the tokens?

Where ERC20 can be supported. For example you can use MyEtherWallet, Mist, Metamask, Parity, Cipher, Ledger, or Trezor.

When will the airdrop and bounty distribution take place?

During the year of 2018 we had some issues regarding the distribution. We will find a solution to distribute the tokens to over 10'000 users during 2019.

How can I participate in the bounty program?

Simply join CraftR marketplace to earn the signup bonus and then go through the specific section.

Why did you swap to a new token?

Our first coin (CRFT) was gaining interest on an exchange during December 2017, but after an exit scam we had to swap to a new token (CRAFTR) due to a huge amount of fundings locked. This decision saved all pre-investors and team funds. The swap not only saved investors from a scam, but also brought benefits in terms of development and partnership.
