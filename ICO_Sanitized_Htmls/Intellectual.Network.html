PROGRAM OF INDIVIDUAL DEPOSIT (PID)

PID connects one single participant to another single participant of BANKllect network for the purpose of signing a deposit contract (business contract).

Need more info ...

PROGRAM OF COLLECTIVE DEPOSIT (PCD)

In compared with the Program of Individual Deposit, PCD connects two or more participants of BANKllect network for the purpose of sharing risks between each participant involved in the deposit contract (business contract). PCD also gives an opportunity for earning extra money through the mechanism of collectively accumulated electronic currency "Sphere".

Need more info ...

PROGRAM OF INDIVIDUAL LOAN (PIL)

PIL connects one single participant to another single participant of BANKllect network for the purpose of signing a loan contract (business contract).

Need more info ...

PROGRAM OF COLLECTIVE LOAN (PCL)

In compared with the Program of Individual Loan, PCL connects two or more participants of BANKllect network for the purpose of sharing risks between each participant involved in the loan contract (business contract). PCL also gives an opportunity for earning extra money through the mechanism of collectively accumulated electronic currency "Sphere".

ABOUT US

BANK llect is a decentralized worldwide bank network .

​

Its main task is to create a unique and new generation bank ecosystem where each its participant will have the possibility to choose a needed bank service and earn on it.

​

​

At the heart of BANKllect network is a participant . The participant is an individual or company with or without initial financial capital. Our own technology « Proof of Participation (PoP)» allows the participants to earn money by a degree of its activity in BANKllect network.

BANK llect 's motto is

ALL PARTICIPANTS OF BANK LLECT NETWORK WILL BE PAID FOR THEIR SPENT LIFE TIME
