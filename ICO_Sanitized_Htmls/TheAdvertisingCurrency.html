Make Friends

The TAC Social Network is unique. We are not hear to steal any data from you or turn you into a product. Instead we are here to help members connect and help idea's grow.

This social network exists to help bring a new world forward where people come together to change the state of the world.

Publish Posts

Publishing Posts is a requirement of any modern social network. The TAC Social Network also allows users to recommend new features and vote for those features.

The belief is this allows everyone to create their ideal social network by actively getting involved. It also allows users an unusual ability to engage with the creators.

Send Emails

The TAC Social Network email system is incredibly unique. It allows members to send anyone else on the network a message whether they are connected or not.

The catch is that each email send to another user has a small cryptocurrency fee that must be paid. The receiver earns a cryptocurrency reward for opening.

Discover Content

Users can add content to our content discovery network and can surf the content discovery network to find new websites, new projects, businesses, or pretty much anything else.

Viewing the content discovery network requires adding a site first. Each site added costs a fee in cryptocurrency and view pages earns cryptocurrecy.

Earn Crypto

The TAC Social Network elimates the concept of users of social networks being the product and instead rewards them with cryptocurrency based on their popularity and contributions.

Members earn cryptocurrency the more connects they make, the more their content is engaged with by others, and the more they comment and engage with others content.
