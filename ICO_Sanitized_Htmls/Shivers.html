What is Shivers Token?

An ERC20 token which fuels an innovative platform for creating and distributing horror films. Shivers aims to set the standard for the way exclusive movies are presented to the audience using blockchain technology.

Redeemable For Digital Content

Shivers can purchase digital items on our marketplace such as movies and future content.

Escapes An Outdated System

The film industry struggles to generate quality content due to the demands of financing. We will take power away from the boardroom and give it back to the filmmakers.

Circle Of Life

Shivers will exist in a perpetual cycle due to the limited supply and the requirement of their use as a pass-key to our platform.
