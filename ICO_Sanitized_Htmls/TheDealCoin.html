Borrower & Investor

The borrower completes the application form via our Decentralised Application (DAPP), supplying additional information to ensure compliance with our KYC & AML policy.

The DAPP connects securely to the Deal Lending Solution Database (DSD), where the information supplied is processed and triggers a number of tasks awaiting verification.

Initially these requests will look to complete the following tasks;

  • Asset verification and tokenization
  • ensuring that the assets are valid and valued against the potential loan through our asset tokenization partners.
  • Application/Contract underwriting
  • Smart insurance ensuring that application and contract are protected.
  • Identity Verification
  • Confirmation of the KYC information supplied by the borrower.
  • The DAPP has the capability to work with any number of partners both via blockchain or traditional API connection.
  • Once all tasks are returned verified, the borrower is notified and their application is listed within the DSD and displayed via our DAPP for ease of use for the potential investors.

Users who intend to lend on the platform must initially complete the KYC information to participate within the DSD Ecosystem, once the information is confirmed as a verified task, the lenders have the ability to view and communicate with borrowers via the DSD.

Platform users connecting seamlessly with borrowers globally, are able to discuss rate or terms acceptable to both parties.

Funding Loan

Once the borrowers funding target is reached via a single or multiple lenders and terms are accepted, all information is collated and held within the DSD.

The borrower purchases the relevant value of The Deal Coins to pay for the service providers fees such as legal, conveyancing and potentially insurance. The Deal Coins can be exchanged back to FIAT by the service providers on successful completion of their work.

The Deal Coin Solution can combine a smart legal contract and smart insurance contract to offer a protected agreement, secured within the DSD and The Deal Coin blockchain protocols and protected by our blockchain cybersecurity shield.

This includes but is not limited to;

  • Borrowers application
  • Borrowers KYC Information
  • Smart insurance policy
  • Asset securitisation
  • Credit referencing
  • Lenders KYC information
  • Agreed rate repayment frequency
  • Terms of loan agreement
  • Borrowers and lenders legal contract
  • Arbitration process
  • Debt collection provisions
  • Loan value

On reaching the funding target the investors deposit their FIAT funds into the escrow account which are then released to the borrower.

Administration & Repayment

The DSD then acts as the administrator of the loan, ensuring all terms of agreement, loans and interest are satisfied, having the ability to invoke any of the features included within The Deal Coin contracts, such as the smart insurance, arbitration process or debt collection provisions if the conditions of the agreement are not met.

The borrower repays the loan in line with the monthly capital and interest repayment option or interest only throughout the loan period with the capital repayment at the end of the term. On successful completion of the loan repayment the borrowers account is updated and stored within the DSD (Deal Solution Database)

This combination of blockchain technology, connectivity and global reach offers unparalleled advantages for both borrower and lender alike.
