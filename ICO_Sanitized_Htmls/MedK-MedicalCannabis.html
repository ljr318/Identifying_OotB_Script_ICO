In order to answer the most frequently asked question about the nature of our business, the one that MedK carries out on Romaniaâs territory, the legality of our activity and the legal framework governing this field:

YES, it is perfectly legal,
and we respect all the rules in force.

With the authorization of the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, it is allowed in Romania to cultivate plants containing controlled substances, if they are processed for technical purposes in order to produce strains, fibers, seeds and oil for scientific and medical use.
This is exactly what we are doing with the MedK project: growing cannabis plants and extracting the CBD oil, a product used only for medical purposes.

Legislative framework: Law No.339 / 2005 with subsequent amendments and modifications, which regulates and establishes the legal regime for the cultivation, production, manufacture, storage, trade, distribution, transport, possession, offering, transmission, intermediation, acquisition, use and transit on the national territory of spontaneous or cultivated plants, narcotic and psychotropic substances and preparations.
Article 12(1) It is allowed to cultivate plants containing substances under national control only if they are processed for technical purposes for the production of strains, fibers, seeds and oil for medical and scientific purposes and only with the authorization of the Ministry of Agriculture, Forests and Rural Development, through the directions for agriculture and rural development of the county or the municipality of Bucharest, on the basis of the annual estimates established according to the provisions of art. 42 para. (1) letter e) of this Law and the methodological norms for its application. [Legal entities authorized to carry out cultivation, production, manufacturing, storage, import and export activities with plants, substances and preparations containing narcotic and psychotropic substances are obliged to submit to the Ministry of Health the following documents: a situation regarding the estimation of the need of plants, substances and preparations for the next calendar year, according to the forms provided for in the methodological norms for the application of this law, no later than 31st of May, each year.

Art.15 (3) Authorizations for the operations mentioned in paragraph (1) [The production, manufacture, storage, trade, holding and distribution of plants, narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances and preparations] shall be released if the use of the plants, substances and preparations listed in Tables II and III of the Annex is limited to be used in industry or for the production of seed, as well as for medical, scientific or technical use (Table II - Plants, substances and preparations containing narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances of interest in medicine, subject to strict control; Point 18 - Cannabis, Resin cannabis, cannabis extracts and tinctures).

Upshort: the Romanian legislation allows us to cultivate cannabis, with a low concentration of THC, in order to extract the CBD oil from the plants, which is not a psychoactive substance, but has multiple beneficial properties in many areas of research, and especially in medicine. So, the business is legal, because the final products are used 100% for medical purposes.
