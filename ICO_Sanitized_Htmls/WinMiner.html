How it works

WinMiner makes you the most profits by turning your computing power to the highest paying use. Mining altcoins is a computational process that awards a chunk of coins, that can be sold into cash, to the confirmed solver of the computational challenge.

WinMiner identifies the most profitable coin to mine by your machine at any given moment and put your machine to work by:

  • Fetching the best mining softwares we know for the job
  • Collecting the mined coins
  • Exchanging the coin into US dollars
  • Providing a real time estimate in real US dollars for your
    machine work
  • Holding your balance in US dollars (so it wont shrink when
    Bitcoin price tumbles)
  • Offering you a wide selection of easy payouts and low withdrawal
    minimums (starting from $10)
  • And most importantly, it senses when you need to use your computer and stops mining to allow you best use of your machine
  • All for a small fee of 3% charged at withdrawal.
