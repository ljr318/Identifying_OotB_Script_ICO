What is Wise?

Wise is a developer of an analog-mixed-signal microchip-based system. Based out of Costa Rica, we have innovated by creating a digital eco-system which enables a domain of applications for IoT and Mobile devices using distributed Ledger technologies and backed-up by integrated-circuit-product-development. Be part of this ecosystem by acquiring our ERC20 Token.

Ansuz Chip

Ansuz Chip is an advanced development of analog-mixed-signal microchip-based system (AMS-SoC). Powered by a 13 nanometer IC integrated circuit for IoT and Mobile networks, it introduces a new concept: radio based IoT Mesh Networking.

Transportation

A smart city where digitally enabled cars or trucks can:

Swiftly pay Tolls and Parking spots automatically. Locate parking spots easily, and generate savings as you are paying for specific services.

Supply Chain Logistics

By removing intermediaries we save resources and increase effectiveness with supply chain logistics.

A regular Container requires:

  • Manufacturing
  • QA
  • Tracking
  • Customs
  • Paperwork

Applying M2M connections would transform and improve these requirements.

Enabling Data Markets

The case of the Data Silos.

The Data Markets hold by acknowledging 2 propositions as true:

  • Any IoT device contains data that is useful for someone
  • Data is Trustable (Ledger Technology - Trust-less)

For example, a farmer can access data in the data market about:

  • Climate Condition Monitor Sensors
  • Greenhouse automations and condition adjustments for given parameters
  • Crop Management
  • Animal Monitoring

Any farmer could use his data to improve workflow or sell his own operation data in the market.
