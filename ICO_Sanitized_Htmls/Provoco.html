PROVOCO – a Social Challenge Network made for Ethereum blockchain

Provoco provides a new design of the social network and implements solutions that can be used in other areas.

#blockchain

Trustworthy reputation
system

Users will have a personal reputation rating, depending on the history fairness of their previous activities. The social profile data will be logged as a hash signature on the Ethereum blockchain using the anonymisation algorithm, ensuring that all user statistics and history is trusted, private, verifiable or destructible on demand.

Utility token with
value

PROVOCO is using ERC223-based (compatible with ERC20) VOCO tokens. The VOCO is a utility token used for exchange between users for activities operations in the social challenge network. The primary use of VOCO tokens is for challenges. The secondary use is as a reward for daily social activities, such as posting content, liking, voting and other actions.

Guaranteed payments

Every payment made using our Provoco system will be guaranteed. Because of Blockchain, every bit of information will be stored safely and your transactions will be instant. Our system is made in a way so that transactions are instant and payments for your challenges will be always completed.

Ethereum smart contracts

Agreements between challenge creators and challenge executors are saved to the blockchain’s smart contracts, ensuring that payments and other obligations are fulfilled. The creation, acceptance and accomplishment of challenges, as well as community voting reports, payments in VOCO tokens and all other vital activities will be written to the blockchain.

Distributed application

PROVOCO uses blockchain technology for the most critical and trust-requiring operations. This is how it works: a smart contract is generated by the PROVOCO back-end Oracle module and sent to the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) to execute predefined lines of code, and then it starts acting as a Decentralised Application (DApp). Such applications run exactly as programmed, without any chance of fraud, censorship or third-party interference under any circumstances.
