Two networks, endless possibilities

Getting traditional collaborators and longtime competitors to agree on the bold steps needed for blockchain transformation isn’t easy. GoChain Public Blockchain

The ultimate in transparency and trust.

Built using the novel consensus mechanism Proof-of-Reputation (POR), GoChain is governed by a consortium of up to 50 geographically diversified enteprises and organizations.

As a result, the network is the fastest, most sustainable web3 based public blockchain ledger.

Learn more >

GoChain Private Blockchain

The ultimate in privacy and control.

GoChain’s private networks allows you to control who has access to the network and restrict their participation as needed.

You still benefit from the immutable ledger and can scale the network as much or as little as needed.

Learn more >

GoChain Hybrid Blockchain

The best features of both blockchains.

GoChain hybrid blockchains offer scalability and control but still offers public blockchain features such as integrity, transparency, and security.

Set rules to decide which transactions are made public and which are kept private.
