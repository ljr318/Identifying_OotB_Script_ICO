DIGITAL BANK

Our fully licensed and legal bank is the heart of our ecosystem. It provides the following services:

  • Bank accounts both in fiat and digitalcurrencies.
  • Deposits in fiat and digitalcurrencies.
  • Instant money transfers.
  • All the other traditional banking services.
  • Visa/Master Card with BTC and other major digitalcurrenciesas the card currency.
EXCHANGE
  • Intuitive and multifunctional trading terminal.
  • All the major technical analysis options.
  • A wide range of financial instruments.
  • Instant deposit and withdrawal options including ZODIAQ bank card.
  • Hedging by trading the index of 10 major digitalcurrencies to avoid significant rate changes of any specific pair.
hedge fund

AI-based assets management. Our Artificial Intelligence system uses big data analysis and deep machine learning. We have anatomized successful strategies and scenarios to create a wide range of investment options in digitalcurrencies and related markets. For now, the trading algorithm is being tested on Poloniex Exchange, and it has performed more than 100% profit for the initial 20 days.

payment system

The payment platform is implemented in two stages.

At the first stage of implementation, our payment platform will be a bridge between existing banks and current payment services.

For the second stage of implementation, ZODIAQ project will replace the banks, rather than simply become a payment platform.

ZODIAQ card

Similar to your habitual bank cards. Easy and convenient - you can pay wherever you want and withdraw by ATMs.

You can choose whether your card operates with your funds from fiat or E-commerce account.

Switchable between them easily via mobile or web application.

SMART WALLET

Functions as a cold wallet to store digitalcurrencies safely and conveniently. Non-contact NFC payments.

Bind your third parties bank cards.

Receive payments as POS terminal including NFC transfers of fiat and digitalcurrencies among gadgets.

The smart wallet is equipped with the e-ink screen, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and GSM modules, and it is connectable to your PC or Mac by USB.

The functioning prototype already exists.
