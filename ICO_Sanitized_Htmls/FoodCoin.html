ABOUT
FCE GROUP AG is a Swiss company engaged in the development, adaptation and improvement of blockchain technologies for modern business.

Blockchain can bring together different parties in conditions of lack of trust. This technology eliminates malicious modification of data and program code and unauthorized interference from any side. By enabling each party to see the same data, its origin and timing, blockchain can help eliminate complex and costly data reconciliation required by most data management systems.

Availability of relevant data for sharing between different actors does away with the lack of connectivity and encourages collaboration across the entire value chain in a secure and reliable way.

The main field of work of the company is the food market, which is in desperate need of reorganization and simplification of supply chains. But other types of production and distribution are also possible for implementation.

OUR CORNERSTONES AND VALUES
Our IoT sensors based on blockchain are comprehensive solutions for traceability of your supply chains, brand protection, risks reduction, increasing your business efficiency and brand protection.
Traceability of your supply chain will ensure the safety and quality of products, make it transparent for the consumer, increase trust and brand loyalty and enhance the reputation.
DLT and Blockchain as a decentralized systems make entire value chain transparent and visible giving an equal access to the markets to all players. It alliminates the gap between the small and large manufactures.
Direct marketing is an essential aspect for any business. It is the key to building loyal relationships between producers and consumers. FCE technologies help to meet and know your end consumers.
Risk management is a safety cushion for business. When it comes to the food business, it becomes crucial. Use our IoT and DLT solutions to reduce losses and increase sales.
Industry 4.0 , total transparency and interconnectedness of data are simple. We collect and combine big data across the whole value chain in a single, secure and transparent database. This is a key to planning, analyzing and improving production and management.
