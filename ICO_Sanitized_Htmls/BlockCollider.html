    A MULTICHAIN NOT A BLOCKCHAIN
  • Block Collider's mission is to protect and grow the entire blockchain industry.
  • Currently supports trading and building apps between BTC , ETH , USDT , WAV , LSK , NEO , DAI , and Tether Gold .
  • Any blockchain can be added, algorithm agnostic. No centralized validators, hosted orderbooks, and/or oracles.
  • All trades are anonymous, no accounts , open to anyone with an internet connection. No fees, no deposit/withdrawal limits.
  • Supports partial orders. No atomic swaps. Spot rate settlements in under 3 seconds.
  • Block Collider is a Level 2 protocol designed for Borderless & Overline (launches Q3 2020).
    • FOR DEVELOPERS
  • Hiring Rust and Node.js backend developers.
  • Manage interblockchain contracts and transactions with the Timble meta-contract language.
  • Developers can easily upgrade exchanges built on Ethereum, Tron, Stellar, and EOS to use Block Collider using RPC .
    • FOR OWNERS
  • Two coins, "Emblems" (EMB ) is for owners and Non-Relational Graphs" (NRG ) is mined (no premine).
  • Emblems perminently and algorthmically boost mining power and mining reward .
  • Emblems can be leased via built-in smart contract for 6, 12, and 24 months.
  • 300M Emblems total, 100M locked in equity in Borderless Services .
    • FOR MINERS
  • 5-8 second block time.
  • NRG pays for multichain transactions and trades, Miners get 2 NRG per block (without EMB), no premine, 9.8B NRG mined over 40 years.
  • Algorithm is custom proof-of-work called "Proof of Distance" based on the inner product of vectors .
  • Emblems can be purchased or leased from owners who live in areas where costs make mining infeasible.
  • Diversify mining operations by automatically selling NRG into for any blockchain coin in the multichain.
