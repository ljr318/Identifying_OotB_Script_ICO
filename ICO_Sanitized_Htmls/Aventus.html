About

Aventus is an open source, decentralised protocol that revolutionises the way digital assets are issued, owned and transferred across value chains

Watch Video
Aventus powers:

Problem

Lack of control over digital assets as they move through their value chains

Lack of oversight over who owns the digital asset, and when; and any data associated with its lifecycle as it moves over various channels

Inability to retain communication with the digital asset-owner as the ownership changes over

This results in
Constrained innovation around digital assets due to the siloed nature of value chain entities
Distrust amongst different entities within a value chain more specifically around how competitors and/or partners interact with each other, and monetise digital assets

Solution

Whether it's tickets or loyalty points, vouchers or virtual goods, financial assets or in-game collectibles, the innovative nature of the Aventus protocol enables oversight, standardisation and trust across all enterprises value chains. It acts as a secure and independent backbone of inter-operability, communication and value exchange.
Create digital assets with comprehensive rules that cannot be circumvented
Immutable audit trails and full oversight over the lifecycle of any digital asset
Communicate directly with the owner of the digital asset no matter how or through whom the owner acquired it
Standardise partner interactions through a universal API based on smart contracts you define
Allow access of digital assets to third parties, in order to expand the value chain with full accountability

The Aventus Advantage

Aventus is a Layer 2 solution that fuses the benefits of scale and anonymity achieved on private blockchains with the advantages of security and independence from public blockchains. All without the shortcomings of either world.

Private Permissioned Blockchains

  • Scale

  • Privacy

  • Security

  • Independence

Public Open Blockchains

We do this by:
Leveraging key properties of digital asset-transactions to overcome challenges around scale that public smart-contract blockchains face today.
Using industry-leading innovations around zero-knowledge proofs to ensure privacy.
Ensuring that all fraudulent behaviour in Layer 2 can be automatically challenged on the base blockchain without long challenge-periods or subjective voting.

Ecosystem

Please select a tier to view more information.

  • Tier 2
  • Tier 3

  • Tier 0: Custom Implementation Layer

    A public and open blockchain-network from which the Aventus network harnesses its security. Must be a turing-complete smart contract blockchain that facilitate zk-SNARK verification support

  • Tier 1: The Aventus Smart Contract Realm

    Aventus-based smart-contract protocol built on the public blockchain network. Enforces the Aventus economic model and ensures the integrity of the Tier 2 Aventus Network layer. Consists of registration as an Aventus validator, merkle root publishing, automatic challenges against tier 2 digital asset transactions, and protocol governance

  • Tier 2: Aventus Network Realm

    • Peer-to-peer network of Aventus validators, who receive and process digital asset transactions using delegated proof-of-stake consensus
    • Validators must check zero-knowledge proofs, ensure transaction integrity and combine transactions into Merkle trees, whose roots are then checked into the Tier 1 Aventus Blockchain Protocol
    • Validators must register by depositing a fixed amount of AVT and each Merkle root published on the Tier 1 requires a fixed AVT deposit
    • Merkle-tree transactions can be automatically challenged on the Tier 1 Aventus Blockchain Protocol, which results in losing the Merkle root deposit (goes to the challenger)

  • Tier 3: Custom Implementation Layer

    In the same way that HTML and HTTP can be used to build and deliver a variety of websites, the powerful abstractions offered by the Tier 2 Aventus Network layer can serve a variety of needs across digital asset value chain. This is the client application domain, which can be Software-as-a-Service; Web or App; which utilises the key features of the Tier 2 Realm to create proprietary offerings. This applies to clients in ticketing, vouchers or any other vertical that wants to monetise the benefits of an open blockchain for their digital asset value exchange

  • Some examples of what can be built:

    • Domain-specific implementations of Tier 2: dashboards and software tools for issuing, transferring and redeeming vouchers, financial assets and loyalty points, to name a few
    • Specialised rules engine for digital assets which are automatically enforced no matter who interacts with the assets
    • Powerful communication systems between stakeholders and customers, where messages are encrypted and delivered by the blockchain to the asset itself as opposed to the owner
    • Data analytics and visualisations around the rich blockchain digital asset data sets - everything from issuance to redemption
    • Restful APIs to optimise insertion of transactions into the network, taking out all crypto tokens of the equation, making blockchain truly enterprise grade applicable
