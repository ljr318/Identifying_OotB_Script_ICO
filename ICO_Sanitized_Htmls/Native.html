What is Native?

Native is a platform that enables communities to unlock their inherent value. This occurs through encouraging local spending within the community by providing monetary incentives to the consumer. In addition to the economic benefits, users enjoy an improved purchasing experience while proprietors see increased patronage.

Communities can be focused around municipalities, businesses, celebrities, digital publications, non-profit organizations, conferences and more. The Native model also enables any community organization to set up a rewards program that turns passive members into active participants, and a membership structure to provide the most loyal community members with exclusive benefits.
