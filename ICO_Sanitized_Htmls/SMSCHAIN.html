Anyone in the world with an unlimited SMS bundle or unused SMS messages can become an SMS Chain Unit by installing our app on their Android device.

Why And How
SMS CHAIN Users Get Paid
WHO NEEDS TO SEND A2P SMS

Various Telecom companies (SMS Aggregators) that offer SMS Gateway services aggregate billions of A2P (Application-to-Person) SMS messages on a monthly basis. These companies are interested in delivering them at the lowest possible price to subscribers. SMS Aggregators pay on average 0.01$ to deliver an SMS, guaranteeing they are willing to pay it to anyone who can provide them with reliable SMS delivery.

SMSCHAIN NETWORK

SMSCHAIN creates a network of users (Units) that can exchange their unused SMS messages for financial incentives. SMSCHAIN provides an Android application and technical blockchain infrastructure that enables the delivery and semi-automatic trade of SMS messages between Units and SMS Aggregators.

FINANCIAL REWARDS
FOR APP USERS

Anyone who buys SMSCHAIN Token and installs the app can earn money automatically by selling their unused SMS messages. App users can set up the amount of messages sent by SMSCHAIN daily, as well as the type of content to deliver (example: transactional messages from Google, Facebook, Whatsapp etc).

Who Will Benefit
from SMS CHAIN
SMS AGREGATORS

SMS Aggregators and SMS Gateway service providers will gain access to global Unit network, enabling A2P SMS traffic delivery at a low cost. Short Message Token (SMSTO) will be the only internal currency for mutual settlements among the members of the network. SMS Aggregators can use SMSTO to order or purchase text messages from Units, as well as to pay fees to Nodes for the transactions.

APP USERS

You can start selling your unused SMS messages for a monetary reward and potentially earn up to $6,000/ month from your mobile device automatically after setting up your account! If you do not have substantial unused SMS or would not like to sell too many messages, you can still easily make a few hundred USD per month with the program.

blockchain nodes

If you would like to contribute to the infrastructure of SMSCHAIN and become a Node, you can earn 20% from all SMS transactions sent to you. You need at least 15,000 SMSTO in order to become a Node.

A2P (Application-to-Person)
SMS Market Size

The following statistics show the global A2P SMS market revenue from 2014 to 2017. In 2017, the A2P SMS market total revenue amounted to 62.1 billion U.S. dollars.

If you thought that was a lot, the SMS A2P market is expected to grow 70 billion USD by 2020. The market is primarily driven by strong growth in mobile subscribers around the world, particularly in emerging markets.

North America and Europe are the most important regional markets for the global A2P SMS ecosystem. In the coming years, Latin America, the Middle East, and Africa are also expected to witness significant growth in their A2P SMS markets. Large numbers of senders and receivers depend on the A2P SMS market for banking and marketing activities, especially to make mobile payments.
