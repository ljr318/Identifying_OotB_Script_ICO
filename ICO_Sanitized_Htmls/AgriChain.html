The platform

AgriChain brings together all stakeholders in the agricultural supply chain, allowing them make better-informed decisions, eliminate unnecessary paperwork and dockets, reduce supply chain inefficiency and risk, open markets and increase their bottom line, all on one easy-to-use platform.

Learn More

A powerful featureset

Designed by industry for industry, AgriChain is the world's most comprehensive agricultural supply chain software solution.

Stock management

Stock management

Manage stock levels in real-time by recording every outload and inload on our simple to use mobile app. Perfect for farmers, flour mills, stockfeed manufacturers, feedlots or any end user site.
x

Contracting

Contracting

Create and manage commodity contracts, freight contracts and more. As each delivery is recorded, users can track contract progress through to completion.
x

Supply chain tracking

Supply chain tracking

GPS technology tracks every load at every stage along the supply chain, from the paddock to the end user.
x

Logistics automation

Logistics automation

Digitise paper records with the assistance of optical character recognition (OCR) technology, to reduce paperwork and store everything in a single, secure system.
x

Broker integration

Broker integration

Create direct connections between growers and brokers to allow oversight and management of stocks and supply chain data.
x

Truck Receivals

Truck Receivals

Automate your inbound and outbound deliveries at your receival sites. Full weighbridge integration, auto tare off and license plate recognition technology. Match every truck movement to the correct order every time.
x

Traceability

Traceability

Understand where stock has come from and where it is going, with date and time stamping at every stage of the supply chain.
x

Position reporting

Position reporting

Make timely contracting decisions using live, accurate position reports.
x

Blockchain and agriculture
