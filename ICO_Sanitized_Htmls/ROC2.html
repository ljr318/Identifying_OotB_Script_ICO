ROC2 can be purchased exclusively by sending Ethereum to the ROC2 smart contract, the ROC2 smart contract will then upon receipt of your payment, send the equivalent amount of tokens to the wallet from which you made payment.

You will receive your ROC2 tokens shortly after your payment has been received by the ROC2 smart contract, the tokens will be automatically sent back to the wallet from which you paid. Subject to network traffic, the whole process should take lss than 5 minutes.
