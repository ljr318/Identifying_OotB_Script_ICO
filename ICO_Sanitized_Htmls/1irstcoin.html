The future of finance into your wallet

In March 2019 we had our own token listed under the symbol 1irst -FST- at several crypto currency exchanges. Ultimately, the FST mirrors the success of our business model, as 90% of the profits of this company are distributed to the holders of the FST Token. The reference currency for profits is Ethereum, short ETH. Blockchain based on Ethereum technology makes this possible. In addition to its utility function as a means of payment, the FST token also offers a real investment alternative, which is currently unique on the market.
