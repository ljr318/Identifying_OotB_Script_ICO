CLIENT`S FUNDS ARE SECURED

Client`s staking funds blocked in the active Breezecoin packages are secured with asset classes independent of inflation. The primary goal in general is to minimize the risk for our customers, especially during hard times or special situations. Breezecoin guarantees this protection. We take 10% of every amount put in our secured packages of our staking program for financial coverage. We separate these funds from our working capital. A renowned law company from Germany works as an independent trustee to insure the separation of the financial coverage from the funds of Breezecoin. We invest these funds in asset classes that will not be affected by inflation, such as e.g. precious metals. Breezecoin is fully responsible for this protection, so we decide in an permanent process where exactly to invest the funds in consideration of the current situation, especially the market situation.
