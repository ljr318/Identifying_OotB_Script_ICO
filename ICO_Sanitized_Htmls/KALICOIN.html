Technical Details

Algorithm

Kali Coin is based on Scrypt using Proof-of-Work algorithm

Block Targets:

To make transfer quicker and smooth, Kali Coin blocks are generated every 2.5 minutes

Block Reward:

50 Kali Coins per block as miner reward, halving every 2 million blocks.

Total Supply:

Total supply of Kali Coin for life-time will never cross 200 million.

Difficulty re-target:

Mining difficulty adjusts every 2016 blocks.
