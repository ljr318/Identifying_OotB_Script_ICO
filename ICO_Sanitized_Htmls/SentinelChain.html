WHAT IS SENTINEL CHAIN

Sentinel Chain is a B2B marketplace specifically designed to provide affordable and secure financial services to the unbanked.

Sentinel Chain - a blockchain-based international marketplace for cross-border financial services will be the world's first platform to accept the use of livestock as collateral.

Taking a unique approach to the last mile problem of financial inclusion, the Sentinel Chain model establishes livestock provenance through the creation of livestock insurance on blockchain — the registration of livestock provenance on blockchain unlocks 'dead capital' and provides the unbanked with a new opportunity: the real possibility of accepting livestock as 'collateral' for loans.

FARMTREK

FarmTrek, developed by InfoCorp Technologies, is the world’s first solution for livestock-backed insurance and financing with private, permissioned-based blockchain to tag and track livestock ownership.

Our solution is designed for companies and government agencies involved in livestock-related (business) activities, and livestock owners. FarmTrek reduces fraud by maximising data integrity from end-to-end, for the primary aim of solving unbanked farmers’ asset ownership problem. Through the use of physical tamper-proof tracking device, attestation process and digitally immutable data storage, our solution addresses issues of:

  • Identity fraud
  • Asset ownership fraud
  • Data security risk
  • Credit assessment risk
  • Livestock insurance moral hazard
  • Livestock tracking and traceability
  • Cost-effectiveness
