Supply and Demand of Mediar Token

Monetary policy

Mediar Token has limited supply and can not be re-released. During the initial stages of building a community, the MEDIAR platform can develop very dynamically. Ultimately, however,the rate will stabilize as the project reaches the maturity stage. In this case, the market will be more predictable and the price of MDR will stabilize, but it will still remain speculative. Users will benefit from platform development and increased demand for media tokens, which is a limited good. We want the token to have a really high value thanks to the benefits it offers its holders.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is it worth to get Mediar Token?

    We present a transparent development plan for our project and present so much information as is possible. In the interests of users, we focus on their safety and convenience because it's what we care about the most. All information regarding our projects is available in our White Paper.

  • What is Mediar?

    This is the official cryptocurrency for transactions within the MatchDiary platform. It will allow users to avoid commissions due to currency conversion and long waiting time for money transfers.

  • How can I use Mediar Token?

    How can I use the Mediar Token? We intend to provide MatchDiary users with direct savings on purchases made with the use of Mediar Tokens, which will result in a real demand for the token and increase its value. We want the token to have an increasing value for the fans thanks to their benefits, i.e. lower transaction costs, limiting the intermediaries’ role, unified registration platform of activity (betting, reading, playing).

  • What is Smart Contract?

    It is a legal bond that can function independently in the digital space. This is another step forward in the blockchain technology, enabling implementation of conditions contracts in an automated manner, allowing to minimize the risk of any manipulation and error.

  • When will you enter the cryptocurrency exchange?

    We have been in talks with several exchanges since the start of the project. We are planning first open to the domestic market, and then with the increase in the number of users, on the international market. We will keep you updated.

  • What will happen to unsold tokens?

    Based on our sales model, this will never occur. In the last phase of sales, the remaining tokens that have not been sold yet, will go to the joint pool. Depending on the share, this pool of tokens will be distributed to supporters.
