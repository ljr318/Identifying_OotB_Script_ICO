About GrabAMeal

GrabAMeal will be a global peer to peer connecting platform to connect buyers to sellers who wish to prepare a meal and serve them. The blockchain platform of GrabAMeal will work on different protocols to ensure the identity of each participant, regulate quality and trust, reward and incentivize transactions and also dispute management.

The GAM-Id protocol kicks in at the onset of a seller or a buyer listing themselves on the platform. Participants submit profile information. The GAM-Id algorithm continuously adjusts the ratings of the users on a graded scale and categorizes sellers and buyers accurately and fairly. The GrabAMeal Reputation Management protocol encapsulates the reputation and disputes and constitutes a reward and incentive mechanism for participants based on their reputation ratings. The participants are allocated rewards for transactions depending on their reputation rating and dispute history.

WHAT PROBLEMS DO GRABAMEAL SOLVE?

AFFORDABLE FOOD
GrabAMeal will decentralize the marketplace and eliminate intermediaries to the extent possible. Benefits will be felt in reduced cost. This will make every meal to be affordable to the buyers worldwide.
INCREASES TRUST
With special protocols based on blockchain smart contracts and also AI/ML technology the platform will ensure reputation of the participants and transparency of every transaction is maintained with maximum priority.
VALUE FOR ALL
The participants will be the primary stakeholders and will be distributed the value accrued from the growth of the network. Tokenization of transactions and rewards will reap maximum benefit from growth.
SUPPORTIVE ECOSYSTEM
GrabAMeal intends to bring together an independent set of food service providers to develop a self-sustaining ecosystem for conducting business operations. Buyers will get equal benefit from this well functioning ecosystem.
FLEXIBLE CHOICE
Buyers are able to choose meals anywhere in the world where GrabAMeal ecosystem is functional. The buyers can pre-order meals, pay in advance and enjoy a flexible cancellation/modification of orders, based on simple rules.
TOKEN COMMERCE
The tokens will be used to reward and incentivize participants and will be an efficient means of exchange of services and products. Token transactions will be faster, cheaper and safer.
