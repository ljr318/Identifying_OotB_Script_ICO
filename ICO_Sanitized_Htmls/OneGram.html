Digital Asset Backed by Gold

Gold is one of the oldest and most stable assets people around the world have been trading with for centuries. Each OneGram coin is backed by at least one gram of physical gold which provides a stable floor price. With this intrinsic feature of OneGram we have combined the best of the old world (gold) with features of the future (digital allocation).

Compliance with Shari'ah law

OneGram is the first cryptocurrency that has been certified in compliance with the ancient Islamic rules called Shari'ah. It is inherently regulated by rules imposed on financial operations in the Islamic world.

Growth with Every Transaction

OneGram is an asset that grows in value and rewards the members of the OneGram community through its transaction fee. Using a 70% portion of the low transaction fee of one percent, more gold is purchased for the backup reserve of OneGram itself. OneGram becomes more valuable with every single transaction and this makes it a strong asset.

Delegated Proof of Stake

While developing OneGram we considered the environment. We wanted to be as eco-friendly as possible that is why we have chosen the proof of stake (PoS) blockchain technology as it uses 10 times less energy than proof of work (PoW) protocols. On top of that, it is very fast, it takes only a few seconds to carry out a transaction. Delegates and validators are democratically elected by the community of OneGram stakeholders.

First Non-BTC Reliant Coin

The total of emitted OneGram coins is 12,400,786 (approx. half of BTC supply). This limited number is fully independent of Bitcoin and besides BTC is the only digital token to have real-world transactions. To sum up, unlike other cryptocurrencies on the market, OneGram does not rely on Bitcoin to assess its value.

Foundation of Charity

The OneGram team shows compassion to those in need and reaches out a helping hand through the OneGram foundation. This foundation is funded by 2.5% of the transaction fee (which is 1%). The OneGram Foundation is a pillar in communities across the globe.

OneGram Trezor

2019

OneGram TREZOR is the safest form of cryptocurrency storage and therefore highly recommended by the OneGram developing team. This is a hardware kind of wallet that saves private keys and is connected to a computer as an additional source of protection. Once this service is activated, it is not possible to send cryptocurrencies from a wallet without this physical device connected.

OneGram iOS Account (e-wallet) with Vault

2019

OneGram iOS Account is a fast and secure OneGram wallet for an iPhone providing high-security standards like 256-bit AES encryption for storing private keys. The app will feature Face ID and Touch ID to secure the wallet even better. OG Account will serve for real-time checking of the user’s balance and transactions in a well-arranged menu, automatic password filling for better comfort and auto-lock service for higher security. It will include a mobile version of the OneGram Vault service as well.

FLIB partnership

2019

FLIB is the new payment gateway launched for OneGram coin. Out of several functions FLIB will offer, we can mention fiat currency conversion and strong marketing tools such as a white label loyalty programme for merchants, featuring hot deals, and discounts for customers. The model will be first introduced in Dubai and Abu Dhabi where the payment institution license is already in place.

OneGram Android Account (e-wallet) with Vault

2019

OneGram Android Account is a fast and secure OneGram wallet designed for an Android smartphone as a secure storage of the user's private key. The app will feature a fingerprint scanner or PIN to secure all withdrawals and deposits. OG Account will serve for real-time checking of the user’s balance and transactions in a well-arranged menu, automatic password filling for better comfort and auto-lock service for higher security. It will include a mobile version of the OneGram Vault service as well.
