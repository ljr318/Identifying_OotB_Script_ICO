What is Navibration?

Navibration is a navigation system by vibration with which you can move about anywhere in the world with no Internet connection or maps, and no need to look at your device. It is designed exclusively for use on foot.
Patented navigation system.

HOW TO NAVIBRATE?

Your destination is just a hand motion away...

STEP 1: Hold your mobile horizontally

STEP 2: Turn around slowly until it starts to vibrate
(you are the compass; your smartphone is the arrow; the vibration is the alert)

The vibration shows the direction to follow.

Our wearables

One of our main objectives is that both the navigation system by vibration and Navibration Experiences routes can be enjoyed directly through our wearables. Can you imagine? Wait, the best is yet to come.

Navibration Watch

This will be our first wearable and will consist of a smart watch based on our navigation system by vibration, with which end users will be able to both navibrate anywhere in the world and perform audio-guided routes directly with their watch.

Navibration Stick

This will be another one of our main wearables, and will be aimed mainly at blind users or those who are visually impaired. It will function very similarly to the Navibration Watch, but in this case, the navigation system by vibration will be incorporated into a walking stick.

Founding Team

Navibration S.L. is a young Spanish company that came into being when we decided to dedicate our time and energy to our passion. We started out like children with a great dream: to rethink and question everything. We liked the existing ways of moving around the world, but didn’t like that it was sometimes difficult to interpret a map or continually have to look at a device while en route. With this in mind, we looked for something creative; something new. What if someday we decided on the route ourselves? We wondered. And what if one day we needed to be able to feel to find our destination? And this day finally arrived, fulfilling the first of our dreams. Like children, our heads are full of them, and we are putting our heart and soul into making them come true.
