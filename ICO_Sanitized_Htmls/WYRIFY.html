Shares Registry

Wyrify Shares Registry is a unique way to use blockchain technology and help businesses and investors manage, trade and track shares in any company worldwide. Our platform is utilizing a distributed, trusted node network to keep record of all shareholdings and ownerships in the companies using the Registry.

We allow any company to issue their own shares by securely record and store them on the blockchain allowing the administrator to set their own rules for the issuance and exchange of shares. No need for old school paper shares or paper trails hard to track - with our solution any company can keep a life long history of share ownerships and movements. We even offer an exchange where shareholders can trade their shares online with any interested party allowing any company to benefit from the upsides previously only available to public listed companies.

land-registry

Land Registry

Keeping an up-to-date and accurate registry is one of the biggest challenges for land governance in developing countries. Many countries do not even have a ledger and even for those who do, property records are typically vulnerable to inconsistencies, as well as issues like tampering, damage, and loss.

Wyrify land registry on blockchain is turning old paper deeds into assets on a blockchain ledger securely encoded with a unique identifier that allows for protection and tracking. This also allows records and information to be added, seen and amended to the network by permissioned parties, without having to rely on any intermediaries or a centralized authority, such as a national land registry.
