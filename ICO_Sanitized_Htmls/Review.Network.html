Review.Network - Become a part of REWolution!

Review.Network is an consumer-generated online review and primary market research platform employing blockchain technology and advanced data science tools. We are building the world's fastest, most trusted and intelligent market feedback platform by rewarding users, rebuilding trust in online communities and revolutionize user control and empower data ownership.

Review.Network Rewards users for their time writing comprehensive online reviews and answering market research surveys. Our Demographic Filters allow for faster decision making by cutting out the noise and quickly find relevant and verified information.

Review.Network Rebuilds trust on consumer-generated review sites through a smart community validation aiming to eliminate fake reviews. The risks of data manipulation is mitigated using blockchain technology.

Users Recover control and ownership of their personal data. Review.Network empowers users to monetize personal data by sharing online reviews with the Review.Network community.

Our initiative is making market research faster, more user friendly and much cheaper. The advanced tools we offer will immediately help companies move their market research to the next level.

Review.Network provides a global platform for product reviews across a myriad of industries which makes finding the information you need to know fast and easy. Earn tokens while reviewing!

Market Research Platform

Review Platform
