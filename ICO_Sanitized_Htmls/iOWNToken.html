iOWN CONCEPT

iOWN tackles problems in a two-pronged fashion: a digital Blockchain-powered program and a comprehensive Ecosystem. The twin concept will modernize and simplify investment opportunities through a decentralized platform accessible by all kinds of users (from trading beginners to experts) and business owners all over the world. To that end, we have classified the process of execution to the following parts:

The Problems In Finding Investors

  • FOR INVESTORS
  • FOR BUSINESSES

FOR INVESTORS

Accessibility

It is difficult to find profitable trusted businesses around the globe

Risk

High Risk of investing without reliable source for investment details

Time & Cost

Lack of time and knowledge for new income opportunities along with unforeseen high fees

FOR BUSINESSES

Accessibility

Hard to find trusted partners or investors

Risk

Risk of losing the power of decision making

Time & Cost

Is a main limitation with regards to finding resources & execution coupled with high fundraising fees

Solution (Our Competitive Edge)

iOWN provides easy access for people to be part of any trusted running business (or promising startup) with minimum effort and maximum value. We convert smart ideas to real businesses; we provide digital support to business owners to enhance, expand and market their businesses enabling them to generate more funds and to share their success with everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is iOWN?

iOWN is a global decentralized investment platform, bridging the gap in current investment systems by utilizing the power of Crowdfunding and Blockchain

Where is the company registered? Where is the iOWN office located?

iOWN is registered as an exempted company in the Cayman Islands, and has an information technology office in Dubai (Jumeirah Lake Towers) in the United Arab Emirates

What is an iOWN token? Where and how can I purchase it?

iOWN Token is your key to accessing iOWN Platform which is planned to be launched in the beginning of 2020, it will allow you to participate in the platform services, and reach your target investment goals.

iOWN Tokens can be purchased on a dedicated program ( iOWN Believers ) which is tailored for you to benefit from special bonus while also giving you the opportunity to be part of our community, and reward invites to the Believers Program, and spreading the word about us with even further bonuses.

What is the price of iOWN Token?

iOWN Token is released at a price of $0.01 on iOWN Believers program, for a limited time you are able to purchase it and get exclusive bonuses, and extra bonuses when you invite people to buy.

After the closure of iOWN Believers, the tokens will be available for trade on Top Cryptographic exchanges where it will be listed.

How many iOWN tokens have been issued? Is there a possibility for additional tokens to be issued?

There is a total of 450,000,000 iOWN tokens issued, 67% of which (or 300,000,00) will be available for sale. No further tokens will be issued, and the supply is limited to these numbers.

When would I be able to see my token balance?

Your tokens will be awarded on iOWN Believers website as soon as we confirm your personal information (or KYC), and confirm your payment.

The bonus tokens will be awarded toward the end of the program to allow for refunds.

Will the token sale be available for US Persons?

No, the US & Cayman Islands Persons will not be allowed to participate in the Token Sale

Is iOWN a security or a utility token?

iOWN Tokens are intended to function as a utility tokens to allow you to participate in the platform services, purchasing iOWN Tokens also does not entitle you to ownership rights in iOWN Company.

Would I be able to buy iOWN tokens against cryptocurrency directly from an exchange?

iOWN Tokens will be listed in cryptographic exchanges as announced in iOWN whitepaper and roadmap after September, 2019 at that point iOWN will have no control over the price, or otherwise offer refunds or any form of guarantee for the tokens purchased.

The cryptographic exchange will allow conversion of iOWN Tokens against other cryptocurrencies and vise-versa.

What will happen to the unsold tokens?

All tokens unsold during the Token Sale will be burned.

When is iOWN planning to be listed on crypto exchanges?

Following the completion of the Token Sale and the subsequent token distribution, listing of iOWN is planned on several major exchanges. Our team has reached preliminary agreements with representatives of these exchanges, however, we are not at liberty to disclose their names or the exact dates for which the listings are planned. Please refer to our Telegram Group and our Newsletter for official announcements.
