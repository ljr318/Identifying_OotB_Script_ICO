how it works

Working with Dashing is easy.

Use our simple app to send us a message about your home services project. Your project is handled directly by a member of the Dashing team who personally matches you with the best contractor, painter, pet walker, electrician, plumber, and so on in your area. The next thing you know, your sink is fixed, your dog is walked, or your house is clean. Dashing books, manages, and handles your project from start to finish.

Start a new project.

Tell us a little bit about your project . If you're not sure where to start, let us know and we will walk you through the process.

  • What projects do you handle?

    We handle everything home related. Please reach out to us if you have a project that you are not sure we will cover. We're more than happy to help when we can.

  • What if I'm just shopping around?

    We're always happy to help you find the right contractor. Let us know if you are just shopping around.

  • How much does it cost?

    Dashing is free to use!

  • How does Dashing choose the contractor?

    The Dashing team has over 10 years of experience managing home improvement projects. We personally research and choose the best contractor for your project based on a multitude of factors including their online reputation, credentials, past projects, and professionalism.

  • What if I don't like Dashing's choice?

    If anything goes wrong during the project, let us know! We're here to make sure your project goes smoothly.

  • How do I know this contractor is trustworthy?

    We personally research and choose the best contractor for your project based on a multitude of factors including their online reputation, credentials, past projects, and professionalism. We won't recommend a contractor that we wouldn't use for our own projects.

When you can.

Choose a time that works best for you.

  • Can I reschedule if the time I choose doesn't work out?

    Absolutely! Let us know and we will contact the contractor and reschedule the project for you.

  • What if I'm not home when the contractor arrives?

    When possible, please reach out to us if you don't think you will be home at the time of your appointment. The earlier we know, the better we can help you reschedule.

  • How does Dashing choose the time frame?

    We review both your schedule and the contractor to ensure your appointment is at the best time for everyone.

Submit your payment.

A member of the Dashing team finds the best professional for the project.

  • How do I know this is secure?

    Like Uber, Lyft, OpenTable, Blue Apron, instacart and many other online retailers, we run all payments through Stripe. If you have any security related questions, email us at hello@getdashing.com and we'd be happy to chat.

  • Do I need to pay the contractor when they arrive?

    Nope. We pay the contractor so you don't have to. No haggling or awkward moments while you search for cash or a check book. We have it covered.

  • How do I tip the contractor?

    If you'd like to tip the contractor, please let us know after the job is complete.

Finish your project.

Start a new project and tell us a little bit about it (include pictures if you can!).

  • Do I need to do anything after the contractor leaves?

    After the project is finished you're good to go! We will send you a message about how the project went where you can leave feedback for our team and the contractor.

  • How do I leave feedback?

    We will reach out to you after the project is finished. Let us know how the project went, how the contractor was, and if you'd recommend them for future projects.

  • Can I hire the contractor again?

    Absolutely! If you'd like to hire a contractor we've worked with again, let us know. We'd be happy to set up the appointment for you.
