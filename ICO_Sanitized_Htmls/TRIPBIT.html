5 Awesome ways to make your staycation in Udaipur memorable

The city of Udaipur in Rajasthan will leave you spellbound with its beauty, warmth, and hospitality. This place leaves a lasting imprint in your heart. Set on the green backdrop of Aravalli Hills, dotted by crystal clear lakes and adorned by historical monuments, this one is a royal charmer.

In Udaipur, not a single moment is boring. Every time it unfolds a new magic and wins your heart. Here are 5 awesome ways to make your staycation in Udaipur memorable.

Stay in heritage hotels

In Udaipur, many palaces are converted into grand hotels. They attract tourists from all over the world and give them a slice of royal Rajasthani life. With their artistic embellishment, vintage décor, glorious ambience, sumptuous cooking, and enchanting views, these luxurious palace resorts make your staycation unforgettable.

Some Heritage Hotels are beautiful places to stay in Udaipur.

  • City Palace
  • The Leela Palace
  • Madri Haveli
  • MewarHaweli
  • Shiv Niwas Palace
  • The Taj Lake Palace

Visit the lakes

Udaipur is called The City of the Lakes and also Venice of the East. No one can escape the serenity and the shimmering beauty these lakes reflect. Enigmatic and historical, the lakes in Udaipur are a sight to behold.  Don’t miss these famous lakes

  • Lake Pichola
  • FatehSagar Lake
  • Rajsamand Lake
  • DoodhTalai
  • Badi Lake
  • SwaroopSagar lake

Eat authentic Rajasthani cuisine

Rajasthan has great food history that has travelled from generation to generation. Sitting in royal ambience and enjoying tasty Rajputanafood completes your stay in Udaipur. Some of the much loved Rajasthani dishes are Ker-Sangri, Gatta Curry, PapadkiSabji, Lal Maans, DhoongarMaans, RajasthaniKadhi, MirchiBada, Ghevar, Kachori and last but not the least, Dal BaatiChurma.

Take a tour of the palaces

When in Udaipur, live like a royal, travel like a royal. Visit the palaces, and you will know why this city is loved and respected throughout the world. The ancient glory of the palaces in Udaipur will leave you entranced. Famous palaces in Udaipur are

  • The Monsoon Palace
  • The City Palace Museum
  • Udaipur City Palace
  • The Lake Palace
  • JagMandir

Visit Saheliyonki Bari

Your Udaipur staycation is incomplete without a visit to Saheliyonki Bari, an exquisitely crafted garden. Rana Sanga of the Mewar Dynasty built the garden for his beloved queen. Fountains, lotus pools, marble elephants, sculpted figurines add to the beauty of the lush green lawns and colourful flowerbeds.

Enjoy your staycation in Udaipur to the fullest with traditional shopping, puppet show, heritage walk, temple visits, horse riding, and a lot more. Go explore!

Al usar zapatillas de deporte, tendrás arena dentro. Empacar botas de montaña (o cualquier calzado cerrado) es óptimo. Caminarás sobre la arena, caminarás por los valles y deambularás por el desierto a pie por un breve momento.

The best 2 valleys of Marrakech

Located 30 km south of Marrakech, ourika stretches at the foot of the Atlas. Green, it is watered by the Ourika Valley surrounded by adobe hamlets, small houses, and small hotels, nestled in the mountains. At 1500 m altitude, you can reach Arhbalou, and further setti fatma; from there begin excursions and hikes to eventually reach the 7 waterfalls. Arriving at the top, we discover a breathtaking view of the entire village.

Our ourika vally day trip is one of the best excursions to make during a stay in Marrakech, it is totally exotic. You enter the High Atlas and can discover the Berber tribes.

The landscape is pleasant with its terraced crops and adobe houses backed by the mountains. This valley allows the Marrakchis to breathe a bit of fresh air and see a little greenery. One day is possible, but nothing beats two days to fully enjoy it. This valley is not served by bus.

The grand taxi or your own car (2 hours without counting the many stops and detours) will be your means of transport. It takes 15 dh for the taxi.

Places to visit in Ourika Valley.

The saffron garden:

A guided tour of the artisanal farm is offered to learn all about Crocus Sativus, this fragile purple flower from which saffron is extracted.

The Organic Aromatic farm:

Located in the village of Tnine Ourika.

The proposed walk is a real delight for the senses.

Setti Fatma and the waterfalls:

1500 meters above sea level. Guide for around 50 dh to reach the first waterfall and for 100 dh for the complete walk. The hike of the seven waterfalls is famous and poses no particular problem. In an hour’s walk, you reach the first waterfall. You will then see a series of small waterfalls all pretty in the middle of a magnificent setting.

You can swim there. Then count two and a half hours to reach the other waterfalls. This worth the pain.

Going back down to Setti Fatma , magnificent views of the village and the valley await you. Many accommodation and catering facilities are available.

Imlil day trip Excursion from Marrakech

Our Imlil day trip will start at 8 a.m. towards Asni, the most attractive valley of the Grand Atlas. You will discover the Toubkal National Park, the village of Imlil and several villages perched above agricultural terraces.

Once you arrive at the village of Imlil, perched at an altitude of 1,745 m and the preferred starting point for hikers to discover the High Atlas mountains, you will discover the Berber way of life and their customs and you will also have the opportunity to do an hour hike (optional) with a local guide to the prestigious village of Armed.

Back in Imlil you will taste a delicious traditional Berber meal at the inhabitant’s house composed of a Berber tagine, Moroccan salads and homemade bread where you will also have the opportunity to initiate yourself in the preparation of the famous Berber mint tea . Then you will take a short relaxing break at the foot of a hill in the village.

Then you will find your vehicle on the way back to Marrakech enjoying the scents of olive and apricot orchards and also beautiful views of the cereal crops of the Haouz plain.

Your Atlas Mountains day trip will be end at 19 :00 pm.

Strong points:

Explore the Atlas Mountains on your day trip.

Discover the famous village of Imlil and opt for a hike (optional) to Armed village.

Taste an excellent Berber tagine for lunch with a very welcoming local Berber family.
