Fully Functional Platform – Invested 30K Man Hours Thus Far

Synthium is an intelligent, blockchain enabled healthcare technology platform that addresses supply chain participants in Health Industry by seamlessly linking Manufacturers, GPOs, Distributors and Providers. Synthium saves healthcare providers time, money and effort to procure products at best active price and just-in-time delivery while keeping track of transactions and charge-backs. For Manufacturers, GPOs and Distributors, Synthium provides a transparent platform to maintain, update and track all transactions from RFQs, sourcing to settlement and enables on-demand reporting.

Platform Features

Smart Contract Management

Physical and virtual visibility from the Manufacturer through the GPO, Distributor, 3PL and all the way to the Point of Care leveraging Block chain technology. Current medical device supplies rely on disparate and untimely voice communications. Transition to event driven and proactive planning.

Order Placing

Synthium simplifies the process of getting what you need, when you need it. Orders can be placed directly and deliveries scheduled at the time that’s perfect for you.

Easier Transaction

Pay multiple vendors at one time and even set up payment schedules.

Surgical Management

Providers can schedule and manage surgeries using our surgery management module. On the other end, manufacturers have the ability to provide JIT delivery for any surgical schedule.

Medical Sales Rep Management

Leverage your most expensive resources to connect with your target audience and increase sales and brand awareness. Manage appointments, trunk stocks and related communications.

Simple Integration

Without any interruption to operations, Synthium can integrate with any current procurement process or system.

Data Analytics

Users get immediate access to reports on purchases, analytics, and other data collections.

Better Browsing

An easy-to-navigate browsing dashboard finally gives healthcare facilities a place to shop around and see various products – no matter what price range they’re looking for. Suppliers also enjoy unmatched visibility regardless of their brand’s clout.

Search Agent Technology

This proprietary technology retrieves specific product search results from multiple distributors/vendors and displays the results in a user-friendly matrix – all in real time.
