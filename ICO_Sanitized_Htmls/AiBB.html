FAQ

How do I fund my BlockBanc Wallet?

You can now buy BTC and ETH directly from the app. Simply click the buy button and input the amount you would like to purchase.

When can I start using BlockBanc to trade digital currencies?

V2.2.0 will be built with the swap function which will allow you to trade between currencies.

Is there a fee per transaction? If so, what is the amount?

There are different fees charged within the app.

When you BUY crypto:

USA Fees: 2.9% + 30c
International Fees: 4.9% + $0.30

Sending Assets on the app:
We do not charge a fee on the app.

Sending Tokens always has a NETWORK fee or GAS fee. We do not charge anything on top of this.

How long does it take to fund my BlockBanc wallet?

Depending on the Asset a user is funding with. Eg – BTC is 6 -8 confirmations. It takes anytime from 10 minutes to 3 hours for funds to be transferred to and from BlockBanc wallet.
