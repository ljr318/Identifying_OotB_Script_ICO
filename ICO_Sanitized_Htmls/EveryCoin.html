Aaron Platform

The Aaron Platform is a new financial platform for people who are excluded from modern finance systems for a long time.
We aim to spread the free financial accounts to all people around the world and allow them to exchange money freely.

EveryCoin
provides the financial services such as deposit, withdrawal, transfer, etc. for all currencies in the world by cooperation with TabiPay and Eco-Chains. And EveryCoin is used as the settlement currency of the Aaron Platform.
TabiPay is
a Bank-free Platform to provide free banking services to people who are financially excluded and need new financial services. TabiPay will extend services to all over the world, starting from 10 countries in South Asia initially.
Aaron Wallet and Aaron Exchange
offer the mutual exchange services in real time among Fiat currencies, Digital currencies of Eco-Chains as well as Cryptocurrencies through Currency Matching Engine technology.

Multi BlockChain

Aaron Platform consists of the Main BlockChain and Side BlockChains.

EveryCoin - MainChain
The Aaron Main BlockChain is a blockchain operated by the Aaron OS, which is the backbone of the entire system. It consists of a SideChain index system, a token system and a DDPOS agreement protocol. EveryCoin is a token used in the MainChain for key currency of side chain and Intelligent Exchange.
TabiPay - SideChain
TabiPay is not only a cryptocurrency used in the SideChain of the Aaron platform but also a banking BlockChain using transaction chain method. The nodes of the system in the TabiPay BlockChain are Consortium BlockChain consisting of the nodes our partner owns and operates. TabiPay BlockChain uses the Delegated Proof of Stake (DPOS) Consensus Algorithm.
ECO-Chain
The Eco-Chain System on the Aaron platform provides a BlockChain ecosystem which creates not only tokens but also other digital assets by evolving one step further in Ethereum technology. The MainChain of the Eco-Chain system will be EveryCoin. Hereafter, TabiPay MainChain based on the DAG model will be connected. Each of the Eco-Chains will evolve or fuse into SideChains.

Eco-Chain

Main BlockChain
validates and approves the BlockChain and Cryptocurrency of new Eco-Chains. You can convert your cryptocurrency into each country's currency through the Aaron Exchange, our new Currency Exchange.
A new Exchange method based on the Currency Matching Engine makes it easy to convert and use cryptocurrencies through both the bank-free platform, TabiPay , and the Vietnam ANYNEW Global Payment Card.
Through Eco-Chains
you can create BlockChains of various consensus methods and issue cryptocurrencies easily and conveniently.
In Aaron Wallet (DSW, Dynamic Smart Wallet) ,
in addition to existing Cryptocurrencies, you can keep FIAT currency and various digital currencies made by Aaron Blockchain while exchanging them in real time.

TabiPay System

It's a new financial service built by BlockChain technology for people who do not have financial accounts.

You do not need to visit an existing bank to open a financial account. Convenience stores, pawn shops or supermarkets near you are banks. You can deposit, withdraw, and transfer money at any time, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

You can pay at stores conveniently at any time through the TabiPay App without a credit card or debit card.

TabiPay is a Stable Coin which is exchanged 1 : 1 with Thailand Baht and can be exchanged for Everycoin which is used in Aaron Platform and for national currency or various digital currencies.

TabiPay is issued within the value limits of Everycoin to guarantee issuance. FIAT Currency's reserved deposit is fully escrowed to the bank for double security.
