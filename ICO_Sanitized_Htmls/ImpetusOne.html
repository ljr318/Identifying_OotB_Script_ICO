Like Pokemon Go with brands in real life. Paid in crypto. Deliver an (expensive, pestering) message and call to action: online ads want consumers to see, then click or buy. The reward comes later, after they bought. Most consumers don’t bother.
The game changer is The Nudge. Into otherwise unmet resolutions. Into better decisions. One brand mission at a time. Impetus One makes it happen.
How not to piss off your consumers Here’s a thought: let’s shove ads at the consumer, charge a fortune and hope they love it.” Abusive? Yes. Also true. That’s how the online advertising industry now operates.
Now there’s another way.
The online bubble wrap Consumer + Brand = Love. The economy runs on this one almighty rule. Which makes advertising Cupid. The matchmaker. The fixer.
Are you getting much love online?
No? Here’s why. And how to get it.
01. The advertiser, let’s say a sports brand, loads an ad in the platform. Impetus One requests and pays for an internal review to make sure it’s good to go. If positively rated, the ad is distributed to publishers, mobile apps on both iOS and Android, targeting the advertiser’s demographics or other parameters.
02. You see the ad on Runkeeper: run three miles each day for a week and a sports brand gives you 20$. You click the ad and go to Impetus.One, on the advertiser’s mission landing page.
03. To take on the mission, you sign up for a free account. Your mission will be tracked on the app you enrolled the mission on, Runkeeper this time. Just make sure to follow the validation instructions (it could be as simple as a check-in or a QR code scan).
04. When the mission is registered as completed, the platform charges the advertiser the total cost, pays 70% to you, 20% to the publisher (Runkeeper) and keeps 10% in.
05. To cash your reward, download the Impetus One app. The official Impetus One platform rate is 1$ for 1 Nudge token, applicable to all customer cash outs. Exchange it into your currency or make Impetus One payments wherever its Nudge token is accepted.
The platform will buy Nudge on the market up to the rate of 0.90$ per Nudge and use the reserves if the rate is over 0.90$. When the reserve exhausts, all transactions will carry on at the market price.
How it works

We are building a platform for reward based advertising and the cryptocurrency to run it. Meet Impetus One and the Nudge token. It’s where brands meet customers who actually want them in their lives.


Advertisers come up with and pay for brand missions consumers complete.


The fee is split between the customer, and the ad publisher where the customer joined the mission, with the smallest share for the platform.


Blockchain brings in low transaction fees, high velocity and geographically unbound means of currency distribution.

USE CASE 1/6
Get fit with Nike.
You have a fantasy with you hitting the running track, rain or shine. But life gets in the way. Time for a good old lifehack: baby steps.

You see a Nike ad on your fitness app: Burn 20% more calories a day for three days and get 20$. What’s there to lose? You click through, get the deets and sign up for a free account on Impetus.One. Then you just do it.

Each day. You check in to track it in the app you saw the ad on. Who’s so smart, it feeds the data into your Impetus One account. And when you’re done, the big pay off: you see the mission accomplished app screen! 20$ land into your account in Nudge token. To cash out, you install the Impetus One app and claim the token in your currency or keep it as is for Nudge payments. Maybe for that pair of running shoes in the Nike window.

THE PLATFORM

The website

Here, advertisers will upload ads, tweak their settings and track their performance. Publishers will register their apps and name all their tracking features. Customers will create user accounts and view a summary of their account. The site will also host a landing page for every mission, created from the data provided by the advertiser.

The app

The mobile app is the central piece of the Impetus One platform. Here users can search for new missions to enroll in and track ongoing goal progress. They can send money to contacts and businesses accepting Nudge token by scanning a QR code. Users will also be able to make joint payments with their friends using the app.

The review app

The reviewing cost will be supported by the Impetus platform. Before ad volumes reach a point of rentability, ads will be reviewed by an internalized team, which will also review app features. After that, everybody can register as a reviewer and start reviewing ads and earn Nudge tokens as a reward.
