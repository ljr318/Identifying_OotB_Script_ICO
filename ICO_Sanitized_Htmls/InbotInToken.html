What is algorithmic influence?

From the beginning of the millennium, our worldview has been increasingly shaped by algorithms. Artificial intelligence influences an ever larger portion of our daily decisions.

Social media is algorithmic.

Today, social media feeds are curated by algorithms to show you content that is most likely to keep you engaged. These algorithms operate with so much data about you that they have a holistic understanding of you, your interests and relationships.

Search results are algorithmic.

Search engines show you results based on complex personalization algorithms and deep understanding of your interests, current location, context and past behavior.

Notifications are algorithmic.

Algorithmic notifications on smartphones have become an always-on interface to your mind, enabling applications to engage you in regular intervals.

There's a lot more...

This is just scratching the surface. Your worldview and choices are being influenced by algorithms to an extent that is hard to fully grasp. This is what we mean by algorithmic influence.

If you want to build influence, you have to understand how all these algorithms work. Join the free Academy of Algorithmic Influence to learn how you can win with algorithms.
