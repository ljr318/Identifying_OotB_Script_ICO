What

What is Shping Coin

Shping is a world-first integrated shopper marketing, brand protection, product safety and global product database rolled into a single ecosystem that rewards consumers for using it and contributing to it.

Shping Coin will become a decentralised cryptocurrency that fuels the growth and adoption of the Shping Platform amongst shoppers. Brands, retailers, certification bodies and other authorised agents can use Shping Coins to incentivise and reward consumers in retail shopping situations to influence purchase decisions, validate product authenticity and drive customer engagement and loyalty .

How Shping Works

Developed and refined over many years, Shping will combine the world's most comprehensive product database with a shopper marketing and rewards platform that enables, consumers, organisations and brands to work together to help make shopping smarter and safer for everyone.

A Mobile Search Engine for Products

By scanning barcodes with the Shping app consumers can access comprehensive product information supplied by a variety of authorised data sources, participating brands and other consumers to help them make smarter, safer purchases.

Empowers Consumers,Brands & Retailers

Shping will open up a new ways for brands and retailers to directly communicate to consumers at the critical point of purchase, making the shopping experience more meaningful, helpful and rewarding.

Rewards Smarter Shopping Choices

Shping Coin is the central token of exchange that brands will use to incentivise and reward consumers to scan their products, influence buying behaviour and participate in pre and post-purchase marketing activities.

How

Our Vision for Shping

Shping aims to revolutionise the way we shop by offering brands and retailers the ability to market to consumers while they shop . Yet, unlike advertising, Shping enables brands and retailers to reward the consumer directly for their attention and engagement instead of paying large sums to media and advertising intermediaries. That's a win for businesses and an even bigger win for the customers who buy their products.

Shping Coins will become the currency for this innovative product and marketing ecosystem. As more consumers and brands discover the benefits, we expect Shping Coin to evolve into a desirable, new mobile payment methodology .
