Provably Fair concept in bitcoin casinos

Players are afraid of being cheated by online casinos. When reels are spinning or dice are rolling inside a virtual environment, some gamblers feel unsure about fairness of a game round, especially if a losing streak is long. Regular online casinos that operate only fiat currencies provide no tools for a user to check the game integrity. As contrasted to mainstream gambling sites, some of the best bitcoin casinos have implemented what is known as the Provably Fair technology that enables one to verify the result of each round and reinforce that the results have not been compromised. In simplistic terms, the Provably Fair method works like that: each result in a slot spin or dice roll is calculated using two variables, that is a server seed and client seed. By hashing the server seed and comparing the hashes with a special tool on the casino site, it is possible to see whether a player was cheated or not. Actually, the Provably Fair concept is a definitive reason in favor of bitcoin casinos.
