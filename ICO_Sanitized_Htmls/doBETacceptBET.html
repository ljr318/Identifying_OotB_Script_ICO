Trader

A trader can buy a bitcoin and buy a DOWN option. He will earn on the growth of Bitcoin, or make a profit on the option. Having correctly calculated the price of the option, it can earn when Bitcoin falls.

Hedger

A hedge fund may buy options in the opposite direction from their positions to protect them. As well as in usual options for currency assets!

Crypto loan

When issuing money, by laying a cryptocurrency, the borrower can insure the loan by buying the UP option so that with a sharp rise in the crypt he does not have to pay additional funds, and the Lender can buy the DOWN option to get additional profit when the crypt falls!
