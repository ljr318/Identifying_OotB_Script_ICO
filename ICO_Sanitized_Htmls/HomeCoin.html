Buyer's Agent

A BUYER'S AGENT is a new breed of real estate sales associate who strictly represents YOU, the BUYER. This legal distinction is important to understand because agents for the SELLER conduct the majority of today's real estate transactions. A seller's agent is contractually bound to obtain the price and terms most favorable to the selling party. That naturally puts the buyer at a significant disadvantage. The newhomesagents.com team is comprised solely of BUYER AGENTs. We represent buyers exclusively and our sole objective is to ensure that the best interests of YOU, the buyer, are protected.
