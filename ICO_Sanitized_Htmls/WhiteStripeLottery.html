What is WhiteStripe?

WhiteStripe is cryptolottery, where the prize fund is formed with the help of mining. This is a great way to test your luck, both for experienced miners and for regular PC users.
This is the only lottery in which, despite the impressive prizes, you do not have to buy a ticket, perform tasks or pass verification.
A lottery where the identification and awarding of the winner is fully automated and excludes any human intervention.
We change the concept of online gambling through honesty, transparency and independence.

Why WS?

Simple and accessible
To take part, you do not need to have special knowledge and skills. It is enough to go through the registration procedure.
Regular pay outs
No need to save a certain amount and even request a withdrawal. Prize and reward pay outs are made automatically every 24 hours.
Without borders
Anyone can participate. Our sweepstakes are not geographically restricted and are not taxed.
Attention to detail
We are always ready for dialogue with the community. After all, the easiest way to get better is to listen carefully to the suggestions and comments of your users.
Transparency
Thanks to the blockchain technology, you have the opportunity to see any transactions and operations inside the platform and make sure that the lottery is fair.
Deeds speak better than words
Deeds speak better than words WhiteStripe takes the best from the world of cryptocurrencies and blockchain to make a unique product that is interesting and accessible to everyone.

How it works

The WhiteStripe team has developed a self-sufficient ecosystem where players can participate in the big prize draw without buying a ticket or making a deposit. In addition, WSLT holders receive daily rewards for owning tokens.

  • How many tokens will be issued?

  • What is the cost of tokens at different sales stages?
    Pre-sale starts (05.04) with a price $0,6 for 1 WSLT.
    Crowdsale round 1 starts (05.05) with a price $0,75 for 1 WSLT.
    The final stage - Crowdsale round 2 will begin (05.06), the price of 1 WSLT will be $1.
    The offer of tokens at each stage of sales is limited.
  • How does the WS lottery work?
    Users around the world launch the app on their PCs and mine with WhiteStripe, forming a prize fund.
    Participants receive tickets, depending on the duration of mining and computing power of their computer.
    Every 24 hours, a raffle is held in which all tickets participate, during which the smart contract randomly selects 100 tickets and divides the prize fund between 100 players.
  • How to make money with WS?
    It is enough to have 300 or more WSLT on your account to receive daily rewards. A portion of each lottery prize pool is distributed among all token holders, depending on their current WSLT balance.
