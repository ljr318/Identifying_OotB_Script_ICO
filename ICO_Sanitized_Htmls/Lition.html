What is Lition?
Lition is a layer 2 blockchain infrastructure on top of Ethereum that enables commercial usage of dApps. The Lition protocol complements the Ethereum mainchain by adding features such as privacy , scalability and deletability for GDPR compliance. Everybody can choose to build on Lition without the need for permission.

Blockchain Certificates

Live

Lition is using blockchain technology to provide a modern and safe way for institutions to issue and verify certificates . Using cryptographic hashes , the use-case enables certificates to be stored in a way that ensure the data is always accurate and also private. Certificate holders can use the technology to prove that they own the document at any time and also share with others to verify the validity . Lition is currently engaging with e-learning providers like lawpilots to store sensitive data like certificates and transcripts.
GO TO LIVE DEMO
  • All certificates have a unique, immutable hash that can be validated by thousands of servers
  • Verification of certificates, transcripts and other documents can be done instantly
  • Proof of ownership is easy and available on the blockchain with easy access
  • Integrity of issued certificates are maintained till expiry or deletion

Run your own node

Be a validator today to be part of the Lition Proof of Stake consensus algorithm (LPoS) and help to ensure that transactions and smart contract interactions are propagated through the network efficiently. You can run a Lition node if you have enough LIT tokens and meet the hardware requirements.

Lition Pool Staking

The Lition Foundation allows you to run and validate a node without running your own hardware. The Lition Staking Network lets you use your tokens within a network of sidechains and share the rewards. This lets you enjoy the benefits of staking without having to run your own server.
