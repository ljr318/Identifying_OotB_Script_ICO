Free-to-Play

RewardMob offers all players on the platform Free-to-Play games where they can compete against each other to win RMOB and other prizes like cash, gift cards, and special prizes from our sponsors.

Tournaments run 24 hours a day 7 days a week and can be accessed easily through the RewardMob app.

RewardMob has created a game pool of tokens that players can earn and win for playing games.

RewardMob earns part of itâs revenue from in app advertising. 30% of this revenue is used to replenish the tokens in the Game Pool. RewardMob is able to refill the pool through a buy back program in which RMOB tokens are purchased from its built-in decentralized exchange.

Pay-to-Play

RewardMob also offers a competitive skill based platform where players can use RMOB to enter tournaments. These tournaments allow users that want to play at a more competitive level a place where they win RMOB for winning tournaments.

The format for these tournaments will be head to head and massive multi player formats.

In order to participate in the Pay-to-Play tournaments players will need to either earn RMOB in the Free-to-Play games or purchase it from another player using our internal app based Peer-to-Peer decentralized exchange.

The exchange allows players to trade tokens amongst themselves instead of a centralized cashier mechanism.
