The core problem of today’s news business lies in
fragmented readership and falling advertising revenue.

While fake news and unverified voice is undermining democracy, it is also hurting the reputation of the press.
To make matters worse, media corporatization is enforcing journalistic conformity, and it has become
increasingly difficult for newspaper businesses to survive, let alone remain relevant.

Watch video Read whitepaper

Using blockchain technology, we aim to restore readership
and secure financial sustainability for publishers.

By retaining yet enhancing the triangle relationship between publishers, readers and advertisers using blockchain technology,
PUBLISHprotocol effectively remedies the failing world of journalism and struggling core participants within traditional
digital newspaper business.

As a real project with real people, we have a market-ready
product backed by an alliance of industry leaders.

Our mission is to create a highly-accessible and global blockchain-based news publishing platform for publishers to adopt and build on.
Built by the press for the press, we offer an intuitive CMS optimized for digital newspaper businesses, a blockchain-based data storage module,
and a viable token economy-based reward system to fairly reward and thereby proliferate good journalism.
