How we reach our goals

Additional programs into our ecosystem:

The Social Sports Network is a rewards system powered by our SCOneX Coins, built for the sports industry where Fans, Clubs, Athletes and Brands can get rewards for creating quality posts and sharing media and ideas.

The Payment Program brings vendors and buyers together without a central bank.
We offer vendors a store in our multi-vendor Sportcash One shop, where clients can spend their purchased, earned or won Sportcash One Coins ($SCOneX) with ease.

The Tokenized Clubs Program is a service we provide helping Sports Clubs create special tokens for their Fans. The Fans can be rewarded with the Token of their favorite clubs and use it in our network to buy special products, services, tickets or gadgets of their Club and can even use it to make donations to support their favorite athletes or social projects.

Through our Charity Program, users can donate funds to support projects that are providing work for a good cause.
And last but not least there is the Athletes Program, where Brands and Fans can support their favorite athletes.
