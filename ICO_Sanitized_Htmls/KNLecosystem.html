What business cases can be carried out on KNL platform?

CASE 1

A farmer can sell his harvest on our platform. KNL provides him with insurance and an contributor can buy it now at a favorable price.
The result:
  • A farmer gets profitable investment (working capital for increase in cultivation)
  • An contributor gets a tokenized asset (in the form of a real amount of grain in an elevator, accredited on the platform). The tokenized asset can be sold on the exchange any time.
KNL Token?


  • KNL is a token providing access to investment projects on our platform, released by Kernel-Trade Platform. The KNL retention requirement is mandatory for the Kernel-Trade platform, as it guarantees all payments.
  • KNL's good liquidity, which stimulates demand for tokens and protects both investors and the platform from market volatility, allows investors to plan investments more thoroughly. Using KNL to cover all transaction and commission costs is a fast, safe and reliable way.

FAQ

QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS

  • General questions
  • Selling Tokens

  • What is the KNL platform?

    KNL – is a platform that unites and considers the needs of farmers, traders, contributors, exporters. We provide each participant with the opportunities and instruments that make up a whole chain of efficient processes for maintaining the operational activities of the agricultural industry

  • Where is the company registered?

    The project is registered and carries out its operating activities in Estonia.

  • What is contributor interest in KNL?

    Contributors and traders will be able to receive guaranteed returns on transactions. To do this, you need to purchase a KNL token, through which transactions are secured in the system.

  • What is a digitized asset?

    A digitized asset is an asset either entrusted for safekeeping or temporary possession: whether it be crop volumes, an office center or an apartment. The KNL platform offers to advantageously trade these assets on our own stock exchange, as well as obtain a security loan, investing in such loans.

  • Can I make money by buying a KNL token?

    According to the KNL token, the yield is assumed as part of transactions with this type of digital assets. You can enter into a loan contract and get profit in a few months. Write to us in the section "Contacts" - we will contact you.

  • Can I buy a KNL token for cryptocurrency or using a bank card?

    Yes, you can buy a token using BTC, ETH, as well as using Yandex Money, a Visa / MasterCard bank card in your account.
