About MultiplyCoin – MYC

MYC is a token that will power MultiplySkill. An emerging markets focused mobile education platform with the mission to empower over 100 million students and young professionals. To acquire the digital skills necessary to earn freelance income, become entrepreneurs. Or transition to a new digital career in the face of AI and automation.

Similar to Binance, a world leading crypto exchange, we will burn our tokens once a quarter. Supply will be reduced over time, profit generated from our Edtech platform will be used to buyback and burn MYC tokens.

Our solution is a mobile first digital platform that connects students, professional and mainstream communities to leading opportunities, job and skills.

Exponential growth

Our strategy consists of working with the world's largest, and most innovative investors, strategic partners and exchanges to facilitate exponential growth with our platform serving no short of 100 million students over the next 5-7 years

For professionals

University students
