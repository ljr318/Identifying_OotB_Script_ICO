These platforms are the front-runner in the development, distribution, and monetization of HTML5 games that work on any platform and any device. They are also two of the GameX’s official partners.

How GX Playground keeps game progression data?

How much GX can I earn by playing these games?

About GX Marketplace

What is GX Marketplace?

GX Marketplace is one of the major projects of GameX which lets its users buy, sell and trade video games, in-game items and such.

Is everything functional?

The current version of GX Marketplace is something we call as Lite-Beta. Peer-to-peer trade is not implemented yet. Base supplier of games on sale is directly GameX.

So what can I do on GX Marketplace at the moment?

You can buy video game key codes by using more than 25 cryptocurrencies as payment options.

What’s the role of GX tokens here?

You can use your tokens to apply some discounts on your order. In future, when liquidity is stabilized, it’d be possible to use GX as the main currency for purchases.

How much discount can I apply?

GX’s value on GX Marketplace will be always more than the average exchange value. So when GX increase its value on the market, it’ll be also more valuable here. The current rate is around x3 of average market value.

What if I have thousands of GX and it’s accumulated value is higher than the game price? Will it be free?

No. Each game has a floor price limit. You’ll be able to use only allowed amount of GX tokens for discounts.

Which game platforms does GameX support?

Majority of games are from Steam. But we’ll also have games from other platforms like Origin and uPlay.

Why should I buy it from here? What’s the advantage for me?

First of all, our prices will be always lower than base Steam prices that are set for the United States and USD. If you have enough GX and apply some discounts to your orders, prices will be dramatically low.

Second, we only use cryptocurrencies to support the blockchain industry, so it’s not possible to use fiat currencies like USD, EUR, GBP etc. Each purchase you make also would support GameX for further development of projects, therefore strengthening your investment here.

What is the process of a purchase?

1. Choose the game you want and hover over its cover image then click View Details .

2. Details page shows you information about the game, a few screenshots and a video. Important things to check here is;

– Platform: It’d be Windows, Mac OS, and Linux. Make sure that you see the name of the platform you will install the game on.

– Region (Activation): We intend to add games that has no activation limitation but sometimes we may list some games for Europe and Russian regions only. If you see “Worldwide” there, you can activate that game wherever you live.

If everything is OK, click on Proceed to Checkout button on the upper panel to navigate to Check out page.

3. Check out page shows;

– The platform price of the game (like how much is it on Steam),

– Price of the game on GameX,

– Price with max. applicable discount and,

– How much discount you may apply if you use all of your GX on your GX Alliance balance.

Below that information, you’ll see the Payment Preferences section. There, you may select Cryptocurrencies Only option to purchase the game directly, without applying any discount and keeping your GX or you may also select Cryptocurrencies + GX (Discount) option and use your GX to decrease the base price of the game.

After you completed that process, you can click on Confirm and Continue button.

4. You will be landing on the pre-gateway page, there you will see the final order details like base price, applied discount, payment fee, and VAT costs. After checking that everything is like it should be, you can click CoinPayments image button. Then you will be redirected to the CoinPayments gate-way interface.

5. CoinPayments interface is simple enough. Just select the cryptocurrency you want to use for payment and click on the Complete Checkout button.

6. Next process will be about your payment. CoinPayments will give you a wallet address for the cryptocurrency you have selected and will wait for you to send the required amount to that wallet. If you somehow send the amount that is lower than the expected, you can send a second one to the same address and they will be accumulated. When it reaches the required amount, CoinPayments will verify your purchase and will let GameX know about it. If you don’t send the required amount in a given time, the order will be canceled on CoinPayments and GameX.

7. After that we will verify the information forwarded to us which is normally would take 15 min. and then we will send your game code via your registered email on the GameX.

Can I get a refund after I purchase the game?

Digital goods are not subject to refund policies since they are not possible to recover after being sold. So, if we already delivered the game code to you, you can’t request a refund.

What if the key code is already used or invalid?

In that case, please take a screenshot of the moment of error. Make sure that you show the current date and hour on your desktop while taking the screenshot. Then contact us and we’ll send you a new key immediately.

How long would it take the complete that purchase process and get my game?

That long 7 steps normally take 5 min. or even less. It looks complicated when you write it step by step :). After that, if we think that you have sent the payment immediately, it’d just take another 15 min. for us to check it and send your game code. It may be extended only if there is a problem with verification.

I’ve created an order but didn’t complete the payment process on CoinPayments interface, can I do it later?

Your created orders will be listed on My Orders page. There you can see your incomplete order with the status of “Waiting for Payment”. If you check the details of that order, you can use the CoinPayments button to access their interface and complete the process.

Where can I see my game code other than the email that is sent?

For security purposes, we don’t store your codes on our servers. If you somehow lost your code, just contact us with sending your Order ID and we’ll send it to you.

There are bugs in the system!

Please report it to us via contact us page and let us reward you for not trying to exploiting it.

About GXRS

Last Update: 11.19.2018

What is GXRS (GameX Raffle Store)?

It’s one of the GameX’s Internal Services program that aims to let GX holders use their tokens for different kind of services or products. With GXRS , you can use your GX to join a game raffle!

How can I join?

If you have enough GX on your balance then you can buy your tickets to join the raffle right away.

How many tickets can I buy?

As much as you want, as long as you have the needed GX on your balance.

Can I get my GX back with returning that tickets?

No. Once you join, you are in the pool.

If I’m not the winner, will I get my GX back?

No.

How will you deliver the game to the winner?

Key code of the game will be sent to the winner via email.

How will you determine the winner?

It’s simply will be determined by a random number selection. Each ticket for each user resembles a number. Having more tickets (so, more numbers) in the pool will increase your chance to be picked up. We use Google Number Generator in this process.

How many people can win?

Only 1 person can win the game. 1 winner and 1 backup user will be selected. If the winner can’t pass the security and identity check of his/her account (multiple accounts, fake referrals etc.) or if we can’t reach he/she somehow, the backup will take his/her place.

How can I be sure that you don’t cheat on raffles?

You can’t be. But the requirement for the winner is to reveal his/her full identity to GameX, including name, gender, age, city, country, and a photograph. So, we’ll see that winner altogether.

Can I give or sell the game after I win?

You can do anything you want with it.

Will you add new games?

Certainly! New games will be added frequently.

I have GX on my ERCO20 wallet, can I use them to join?

You can use our GX Deposit feature to send your tokens from your wallet to here, GX Alliance.

Can I see the raffle participants list?

Sure, please check our GX Raffle Store Participant List .

What should I do if I win?

First, make sure that you are the winner. Check the website’s new section regularly. Especially when you see a news about GXRS. Then simply follow the given instructions on that news post.

I’ve won but couldn’t notice that I’m the winner and 3 days passed, can I still get my game?

Unfortunately no. Another backup probably took your place already.

I’m an amateur/indie game developer and have a game ready as a product. Can I list it here?

Yes! If your game is accepted by GameX and listed in our store, you’ll have all of the ticket earnings for your game without paying a commission . We’ll always walk together with indie developers! Contact Us to talk about your game.
