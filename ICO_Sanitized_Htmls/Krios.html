Post your job offerings or hire talented freelancers from around the world. Allow Krios to present your job needs to 10s of thousands of freelancers.

Our Vision

Krios aims to assist businesses in realizing their potential with the simplest and most effective & efficient way to hire freelancers. To be the world’s leading freelancer and influencer platform. By 2021, we aim to make up over 15% of the industry.

How does
Krios work?

Search

The client uses Krios to search for their required industry specialist, be it an Ad Manager, Influencer, Graphic Designer, Content Writer, Developer or any other skilled individual.

Requisition

The Client sends the description to the selected individuals, on getting approved they deposit their funds for the particular project.

Payment

After the client completes payment over Krios platform, the freelancer starts their work while Krios holds the payment safely in escrow.

Monitor

Krios enables the Client to have complete control over the project, while monitoring it’s progress.

Feedback

On the successful completion of the project, the client can review and comment on the team performance.
