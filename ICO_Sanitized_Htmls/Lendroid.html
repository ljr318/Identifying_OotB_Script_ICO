The new
financial ecosystem

VIABLE

Pick from a range of business models. Lend risk free, run ‘Harbour’ or ‘High Water’ liquidity pools, underwrite loans for a fee.

OPEN SOURCE

The entire ecosystem, including the UI, is open source. Launch your own lending dApp with ease.

SECURE

The devs get fully audited smart contracts. Users experience the most transparent, sustainable distribution of risk, ever.

Better, or for
the first time ever

  • Lending
    Zero risk by design. Have digital asset?
    Contribute to a risk-free Harbour pool, hold
    poolshare tokens, redeem poolshare tokens.
  • Borrowing
    Low maintenance. Set your expiry date,
    pay for loan upfront, based on collateral risk,
    get loan, forget about loan until expiry.
  • Underwriting
    Reward for skin-in-the-game. Borrowers pay
    you to take on the risk of their collateral.
    Without you, a loan can’t be issued.
  • Harbour Pools
    Risk free liquidity pools. Anybody can stake
    some LST and set one up to retail interest tokens.
    Both lenders and borrowers contribute liquidity.
  • High Water Pools
    Liquidity pools that take up risk. Anybody can
    set one up to retail interest tokens and shield tokens.
    Underwriters and borrowers contribute liquidity.
  • SUFI Tokens
    Fungible Entangled Tokens that ensure the most
    transparent, sustainable flow of risk, ever. This is some
    next level token engineering.
