Introduction

The Playcoin team has been rebranded into the PlayCoin Crypto Alliance to enable continuous eco-system expansion and realize the revenue model.

The new business model, Crypto Alliance and incubating structure, will select promising projects and execute joint funds or cooperation by Playcoin.

We will continue to build various revenue models for investors and holders.

Our Service

B2B Wallet

Based on accumulated technical skills and know-how of existing PlayX developers,

this project is a B2B project that produces wallets of various Crypto businesses through White Labeling method.

It can be applied to variety of businesses such as blockchain media, community, and games.

CoinFarm

It was launched as a favorite dashboard information service for margin traders;

it is a platform that has grown to hold the highest global ranking of 26,808th and 25,000 active users based on Amazon Alexa.

Based on these user bases, it is becoming a popular platform for margin trading

with platforms that are preparing for direct intra-platform trading and various services.

Crypto e-Commerce

This is a platform that will be operated by adding crypto payment and simple transaction features to existing e-Commerce-style shopping malls, and LifePharm, which has a large number of members worldwide, has been selected as a business partner and will be launched based on that.

It will be structured to accommodate both wholesale and retail sellers; it will have a shopping mall structure that will benefit from all transactions being recorded on the blockchain.

MCN Platform

This was a professional MCN that deals with beauty and diet materials, launched in cooperation with influencers who are active not only in blockchain but also in non-blockchain fields. With a platform to tap into the influencers and awareness of the Infusion, we will run a sophisticated token economy and platform operation that addresses many issues to complement the advantages and disadvantages of existing projects.

Copyright Platform

This is planning a copyright-related platform, one of the areas where blockchain-based technology can be very useful, with renowned composers at the forefront.

Many projects have already tried copyright-related blockchain models; success stories are hard to find. These cases were carefully analyzed to design a realistic blockchain convergence copyright platform with potential for development.

PLX Announcement

(Regarding the rebranding of PlayCoin and Re-listing)

Greetings from team PlayCoin. It’s been two years since PlayCoin came into the world. We have received a lot of attention for its unique business model of blockchain game platform and social mining for indie developers and have been focusing on blockchain games and many other businesses with PlayWallet as the hub for the past two years.Recently, many other ICO projects have failed to achieve their goals, or there were many “scam” projects only focused on ICO funding, causing social controversy.

PlayCoin is not a scam project or a broken project because it fails to achieve its goal. However, from the perspective of the project’s objectives and profitability,
we concluded that it would be better to expand and improve the current business and continue to expand the ecosystem and make substantial profits rather than to maintain the initial business model of PlayCoin.

Therefore, we have decided to pivot our business model.The new business model supports the crypto projects of partners and subsidiaries and is designed to create a flexible revenue structure as holding companies.

PlayCoin has been collaborating on various projects with many partners and cooperating companies. Some of these projects didn’t match up with our new business model, so we only provided technology, but we invested in others with high growth potential. Among them, we selected high-performing and high-growth projects and included them in the PlayCoin Crypto Alliance.
If a collaborating project’s growth is not matching the projected rate, we will spin-off them, and one with a higher growth rate, a higher chance of inclusion they will have.
The PlayCoin Crypto Alliance (PLX) buys back 20% of the profits of the included projects, gradually reducing the amount of PLX in the market, and supports PLX to participate in token sales of new projects or one-way swaps of PLX to new project tokens.

This is a new business model for existing PlayCoin and new PLX investors and holders.PlayCoin is rebranding as PlayX (PlayCoin Crypto Alliance) and will be re-listed on exchange. At the beginning, the amount of the reward may not satisfy the holders,
but we will continue to reward the existing PlayCoin holders, and the rebranded PLX (PlayX) will satisfy investors and holders by applying a carefully calculated compensation model to increase business continuity and profitability.
We start with the Crypto Alliance business model for several projects. As the reflection of holders’ desire, we will always try hard and reward holders with sincerity.

Thank you.
