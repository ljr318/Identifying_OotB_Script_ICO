Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bethereum?

Bethereum is a decentralised, blockchain-powered social betting platform where you bet with friends and other players, not against a bookmaker. Built on Ethereum technology and Smart Contracts, Bethereum tackles the key challenges of conventional betting while introducing a host of innovative features. All bets on the platform are carried out via our own Ethereum-based token: Bether.

What is the Bether token?

Bether is a utility token based on the ERC223 standard. All transactions on the Bethereum platform will be carried out in Bether. The Bether token will be freely tradable on major exchanges and is fully compatible with Ether wallets such as MyEtherWallet.

Where can I store my Bether tokens?

You’ll be able to receive and store your Bether tokens in any Ethereum wallet supporting ERC223 tokens. You cannot store the tokens on any exchange-based wallet (e.g., Coinbase). Here is a list of the most popular compatible wallets:

  • MyEtherWallet (no download needed)
  • MetaMask (Firefox and Chrome browser add-on)
  • Mist (Desktop)
  • Parity (Desktop)
  • imToken (iOS/Android)
  • Trust (iOS)
  • Trust (Android)
  • Cipher (iOS)
  • Cipher (Android)
Will the Bether token trade on exchanges?

We’ve been listed on Coinbene , CoinTiger , IDEX and Eterbase , both top-tier cryptocurrency exchanges supporting BETHER/BTC, BETHER/USDT and BETHER/ETH trading pairs!

Is there a lockup period for founders?

Yes. Bether tokens allocated to founders will vest over the period of 72 weeks.

How can I see Bether tokens in my ETH wallet?

If you cannot see your Bether tokens in your wallet, please add a custom token to your wallet, using the following parameters:
Token Contract Address: 0x14C926F2290044B647e1Bf2072e67B495eff1905
Token Symbol: BETHER
Decimals: 18
