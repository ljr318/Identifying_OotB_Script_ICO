BUY & SELL ON THE GLOBAL GREEN ENERGY PLATFORM

WPP W2H2™ CONVERSION

WPP’s W2H2™ Power Plant Conversion Technology focuses on retrofitting existing polluting fossil fuel power plants operating on Coal, Oil, Natural Gas, and Gasoline into Green Energy Hydrogen-Fired Power Plants. WPP provides a highly innovative Water to Hydrogen Gas fuel system that eliminates pollution and lowers fuel costs by approximately 50%.
