Projects

Project Accelerate

Across many parts of Asia, Tuk-Tuk’s are a popular method of low-cost transport but suffer from fuel efficiency and contribute to the local air pollution.
Together with our innovative partner (DSA) we have identified battery and charge modifications that could provide a solar powered Tuk-Tuk with several hours between charge due to improved self-charging capabilities.
To be located in six cities across Asia and the EU
The Tuk-Tuk’s will be given to the driver in exchange for weekly video blogs
This promotes greener transportation methods and increases our brand exposure

Projects

EV Charge Stations

Project Energise will focus on Electric Vehicle (EV) charging stations. The EV charging station market is currently emerging across the world and while the large corporates invest in main infrastructures, there is a need for smaller independants to fulfill niche segments. Should we be successful in raising our SoftCap, we will aim to implement EV charging stations to meet a growing demand of consumer needs now and for the future

The global electric vehicle infrastructure market is forecast to be worth $63 billion by 2025 and FRED Energy is looking to be part of this new ecosystem
