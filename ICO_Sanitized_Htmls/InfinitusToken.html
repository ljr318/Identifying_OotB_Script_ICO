WHAT IS INFINITUS?

Infinitus is a blockchain based decentralised mobile app (dApp). It allows you to safekeep encrypted digital data such as private keys, passwords, recovery seeds, pincodes or any alphanumeric data on a decentralised network. Upon a pre-set time trigger, your private data will be automatically transferred to a designated receiver.

Functional To Use

The INF mobile app is a simple, user friendly and accessible dApp that is available on both iOS and Android smartphones.

Set up your INF dVault in 3 simple steps on the mobile phone.

  1. Key in your private data
  2. Input your data retrieval preference:
    • Option 1 - Hardware Delivery by Code Trigger
    • Option 2 - Electronic Delivery by Time Trigger (eg. Email, Wechat, Whatsapp, SMS)
  3. Scan the Infinitus Magic Card for Code Trigger or Set the countdown timer for Time Trigger.

1 INF Token can set up 1 INF dVault (1MB).

There is no limit to the number of vaults a user can set up on the INF mobile app.

Valuable To Own

The INF Token is affordably priced for the mass market consumer. Its low cost-to-value makes this mobile solution an attractive and viable alternative to the typical password safekeeping methods such as pen & paper, USB drive and PC/phone storage.

The INF Token is a valuable digital asset to own because the INF Token is not only an utility token that is used to create a smart contract decentralised vault on the app, it is also a tradable digital asset that increases in value as the demand of the Infinitus App services increases with mass adoption.

Effortless To Earn

Download the INF app to become an INF Network Node. Every app will be given a unique 16 digit network node number which entitles the user to monthly INF Rewards Tokens. There are different types of monthly rewards campaigns and incentives for our users. Hence every INF users can “earn” INF Tokens through the app simply just by having the app on the phone. Early adopters “earn” more reward tokens and get the most advantage! Find out more about INF Rewards on the app today.

FAQ

General

  • What is Infinitus?

    Infinitus is a blockchain based decentralised mobile application (dApp) that allows you to safekeep encrypted digital data such as private keys, passwords, recovery seeds, pincodes or any alphanumeric data, on a decentralised network.

  • Who needs Infinitus?

    Any crypto wallet users can use Infinitus to safekeep their wallet private keys and passwords. Infinitus is compatible with any type of crypto wallets – hardware, web-based, mobile and paper wallets.

  • How does Infinitus work?

    Users may safekeep any alpha-numeric data on Infintus.

    Infinitus encrypts, fragments and distribute the private data in small bytes across the INF decentralized network nodes for safekeeping. Upon activation, the Smart Contract gathers back and delivers the data to the user via electronic methods (SMS) or the hardware method, with the Infinitus Card.

  • Can I reset my INF mobile app password?

    Yes. The password on the INF app may be reset.

  • What happens if I delete the INF app?

    You will not be able to receive the Monthly INF Rewards Credits should our system finds that your Network Node is in inactive mode.

  • I can store my private keys on my USB drive, laptop or on a piece of paper. Why do I need Infinitus?

    Traditional data storage uses a store & retrieval protocol where users could only manually retrieve the stored information, typically with a password. However, Infinitus utilizes Smart Contract to automatically transfer your private data to a third party via the SMS delivery. The INF card may be used to create a simple multi-sig for different parties to hold a small part of the private key. INF complements all existing data storage method and provides additional protection for crypto users.

  • What safety measures can I take to ensure my private data is kept safely and securely?

    We advise that all users practice personal safety when using the INF app.

    Please refer to our Dos and Don’ts on the INF Card page.

INF Network Node

  • What is Infinitus Network Node?

    The INF Network Node is a unique 16 digit number that is exclusively allocated to each mobile app download. When a user downloads the INF mobile app onto his phone, the app immediately becomes a node on the decentralized INF network.

    This mobile phone node contributes to the community by providing storage (approx. 500MB max.) for private data in the INF dVault.

  • Does the INF network consume mobile data constantly?

    No. The INF network does not consume data unless the user is performing an activity or transaction on it.

  • What does the 16 digit number represent?

    The 16 digit number is your unique INF Network Node Number.

    The Network Node is an account number that is used to receive and send INF Credits from app to app and to receive INF Rewards Credits on the app.

  • What do I have to do to keep the INF Network Node live?

    There is nothing a user needs to do to keep the Network Node live. Simply keep the app on their mobile phone. The Network Node will be automatically “activated” when the network recalls the data.

  • Does the mobile app/network node consume data usage?

    No, the mobile app if left idle, does not consume mobile data. The app only consumes data when the user has in-app activity running, for instance: setting up an INF dVault, converting credits or transferring tokens.

  • How much storage does the network node needed on my mobile phone?

    The amount of storage of being an INF network node ranges from 5MB to a maximum of 500MB.

  • What do I need to do to become a INF Network Node?

    Download the INF app from Google Play or Apple Store and register an account on the app.

    Simply keep the app on the phone and it will automatically be recognized as an INF Network Node.

  • Is INF mobile app = INF Network Node?

    Yes. The mobile app is the INF network node. Every INF mobile app comes with a unique 16 digit network node number.

  • What does the INF Network Node/ INF app do?

    Every INF mobile app contributes to the INF network by being a node within the INF network.

    The node randomly safekeep small fragments of other user’s private data on the app (max 500MB). The INF app uses a small amount of storage on the user’s mobile phone. In exchange for this mobile phone storage contribution to the network, the user receives INF Credits as reward each month. Please refer to the INF Rewards for more details.

  • What if there is not enough Infinitus network nodes?

    INF has backup reserve network nodes on INF servers to run the network should all our mobile app network nodes fail at the same time.

  • What do I get as an INF Network Node?

    You can contribute to the Infinitus decentralized network by becoming one of our network nodes that offers storage to our community.

    There are many incentives and rewards for all our network nodes such as bonus credits and other perks each month.

    We believe in creating a self sustaining ecosystem where our users benefit from using the INF dVault to safekeep their private data and contribute as well by offering other users the storage for their private data. Please join us and help us build a truly self sustaining and scalable decentralized network for the crypto community.

  • What is INF dVault?

    INF dVault is a decentralized “vault” network system where every mobile app is a network node that safekeeps a fragmented piece of encrypted data. It is activated by the INF Card or SMS.

  • How do I get back my private data that is “stored” on INF decentralized network?

    There are 2 ways to retrieve your private data.

    Option 1- SMS Delivery- via SMS

    Option 2- Hardware Delivery- via The Infinitus Card. Simply scan the card on the app to retrieve the stored data.

  • What is the Infinitus Card?

    The Infinitus Card is a hardware card that safekeeps your data. The information stored on the INF decentralized network may be retrieved by scanning the card on the INF mobile app.

  • Is my private data stored on the INF servers?

    No, INF servers do not store any user’s private data. The user’s data is encrypted, scrambled, fragmented and disseminated across all the INF network nodes (mobile apps) where each node stores a small random piece of information.

  • Can I edit or change the private data after I have submitted on the app?

    No, you may not edit or change the content of the private data after you have submitted because the data is recorded on the main network and it is immutable.

  • What can I do if I wish to edit the stored data?

    You may set up a new INF dVault with the new information. The previous vault data cannot be edited or deleted.

  • When will the INF dVault services be available on the app?

    The INF dVault services will be ready in Q3 2019.

  • What safety measures can I take to ensure my private data is kept safely and securely?

    We advise that all users practice personal safety when using the INF app. Please refer to our Do’s & Don’ts on the INF Card page.

INF Card

  • What is INF Card?

    The Infinitus Card is a crypto wallet backup card that enables a user to protect and manage confidential crypto wallet data such as private keys, mnemonic phrases, passwords, keystore data or any alphanumeric data, on the Infinitus decentralized network.

  • Is the INF Card for one-time use?

    The INF Card Blue is a Single-use card. Once the data is retrieved, the card will be wiped clean and has to be discarded.

    We will be introducing the INF Card Black- a Reusable card, which can be reused unlimited times in Q4 2019.

  • How do I use the INF Card?

    The INF Card needs to be used with the INF mobile app. Simply scan the card on your mobile phone to activate the dVault to safekeep your private keys.

    Please refer to the INF Card page on our website for more details.

  • Why do I need the INF Card?

    The INF card is an affordable and user-friendly way of protecting and managing your private keys. It is especially convenient for mobile crypto wallet users. INF card is 97% cheaper than the current competitor products in the market.

  • Where can I purchase the INF Card?

    You may purchase the INF card on the Infinitus mobile app. Our website e-store will be launch soon.

    INF card will also be made available through authorized resellers and major e-commerce sites.

  • Can I add an extra pin to lock the INF Card for security?

    INF card is designed for easy access by a third party in the event of emergency, hence the pincode is currently not a feature we focused on. We may include this feature in the future.

  • Is my private data stored on the INF Card?

    No, your private data is not stored directly in the INF Card. The data is stored on INF decentralized network and the INF Card acts as a key to unlock the “vault” on the decentralized network.

  • What safety measures can I take to ensure my private data is kept safely and securely?

    We advise that all users practice personal safety when using the INF app.

    Dos and Donts’:

    1. Split your private key into 2 or more cards.
    2. Create an extra backup and give it to someone you trust for safekeeping.
    3. Use multiple backup methods together, such as pen & paper, USB and INF.
    4. Split your private key into 3 parts and give it to 3 different people creating a simple “multi-sig”.
    5. Store 11 recovery words on INF and keep 1 word separately.
    6. Do use the stickers provided to differentiate your INF cards.
    7. Do not write anything on the INF card such as 30 Bitcoins in Coinbase wallet.
    8. Do not save your public key and private key data on the same card.
