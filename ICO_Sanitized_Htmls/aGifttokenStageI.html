Our Token

What is aGifttoken?
aGifttoken is a token based on the Ethereum blockchain technology. It can be received as a gift or offered on special occasions.

a aGifttoken is implemented and managed entirely as a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain (ERC20 smart contract). This implies that when this smart contract is deployed, users can have the assurance that all their transactions are decentralized and occur without any interference from services or third-party entities, including for aGifttoken itself.

Smart contracts will guarantee the token holders' anonymity and safety. This ensures top-notch levels of compliance, stability and security. aGifttoken will be the leading application that will successfully deploy this technology in the segment. aGifttoken will maximize returns on this business model and technology by positioning itself as a blockchain powered contributionplatform. It is perhaps the biggest hope for the future of the industry.
