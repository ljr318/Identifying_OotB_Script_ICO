Who we are! Media

A decentralized News Media and Financial News platform powered by the people.

The news reporting industry is stagnant in its ability to provide consumers with unbiased, uninfluenced, fact driven reporting. Here at Origin Reporting we are striving to change that, to shake up the news industry and provide an innovative way to approach worldwide news.

What we offer

Earn Origins by simply participating in our platform

Write, Review, Comment and Share

Every action within the platform will result in users earning Origins! Content Creators will be rewarded Origins to produce unbiased, factual news. Content Consumers will earn Origins to rate and comment on the articles. To ensure that platforms content is in alignment with the mission, Independent Reviewers will verify uploaded news content.

Pay-Per-Bounty, Pay-Per-Question

A Content Creator can provide a bounty to incentivize sharing of their articles to increase their viewership and therefore earnings. Authors that are part of the Origin Reporting community that are subject matter experts in the financial industry will be rewarded Origins for answering questions asked by novices within the area of expertise.

Crypto Currency Mining

Just by using our platform your device will mine cryptocurrency and you will earn Origins as a reward for participating in this feature

What is an ICO ?

An ICO is a form of fundraiser that typically acts to fund a project. The term “ICO” stands for Initial Coin Offering and is similar to an IPO in the stock market. It’s essentially an opportunity to participate in a project that you would like to see come to life, while at the same time potentially having the opportunity to profit from a rise in the demand for the coin

Is it safe to invest in ORIGIN REPORTING ICO ?

Yes, it is. We are team of professionals with experience, who are supported by group of expert advisers and lawyers.

Are ICOs legal ?

We are a non-misleading ICO, which raises funds for a project that is perfectly legal, provided all documentation is in place and proper legal counsel has approved the ICO terms.

What do I do with ICO tokens ?

You can use them as utility tokens for in-platform services or store your tokens in a digital wallet just the same as you would store a cryptocurrency like Ethereum. Which wallets you can use is dependent on which blockchain the tokens are issued on. Origin is powered by Ethereum. This would enable you to use any Ethereum wallet.
