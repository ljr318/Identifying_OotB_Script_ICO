Secure

Digital smart contracts mitigate payment default risk and enable secure transactions under one payment system free of currency exchange rate volatility and bank transaction fees, opening sponsorship trade globally.

Efficient

Automated process saves time and resources - faster acquisition and significantly lower costs vs. the traditional process. Unbundling complex legacy system into simple one-click optionality.

Scalable

One-stop-shop for sponsors to browse and buy real-time opportunities in targeted shorter-term packages. Campaign Consultant tool allows building customized campaigns across geographies, categories and demographics.

Value-add

A value-add SAAS solution for the rights holders acting as an extension of their sales department - sales lead generation tools, targeted sponsor outreach, inventory management and data-driven sales reports.
