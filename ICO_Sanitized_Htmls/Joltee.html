About Joltee

Joltee is able to reward its drivers for their good driving at the end of each trip. The better you drive, the more you earn. This reward is a crypto money called the JOLT.

What is Joltee ?

Digital renting experience

Download the Joltee App, select your vehicle in the catalogue and monitor your performances at the end of each trip !
Rent a vehicle within a group of friends, family or neighbors, share a common wallet and increase your reward.

Challenge Joltee drivers around the world

Check out your profile, send battles to your friends to prove them you are the best driver ! If they lose, you enjoy their JOLT.
You are ranked among your friends, you can also add new ones through social media platform and challenge all drivers in the world.
