Phantasma Chain

0.1

Ecosystem

Phantasma is a fast, secure and scalable blockchain solution powered by the governance token SOUL and the energy token KCAL that allows for interoperability with other blockchains while maintaining a decentralized governance system. With its innovative staking mechanism, dual token system and advanced non fungible tokens, it allows users to access digital goods & services such as communication, entertainment, marketplace and storage.

0.2

Phantasma app

The core of the Phantasma blockchain is the Phantasma app. The Phantasma app is one of the most advanced and user-friendly ways for people to connect their daily used applications with blockchain.

Phantom Force

The Phantom Force is an independent group of open source developers and other contributors who wish to contribute and build the best user experience & ecosystem on Phantasma Chain and within the Phantasma app. The Phantom Force team members and dApp development is funded by Phantasma.
