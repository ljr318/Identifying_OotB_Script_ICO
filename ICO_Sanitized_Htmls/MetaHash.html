How did we attain such swiftness?
1
Transactions begin at the outer radius and then run into the core network using the fastest route.
2
Each stage approves a transaction on its way to the core.
3
The fastest nodes of the core network synchronize transactions between themselves.
4
Core network returns the balance of the wallets to the network, which verifies the correctness of the calculations.
5
Backup data goes into the classic Blockchain, verifying network integrity.
6
Anchors (hash from the longest chain of blocks) are stored on the Bitcoin, Ethereum and other networks.

FAQ
About #MetaHash
Are you a legal entity?
The process of registering #MetaHash in Switzerland has been finalized and we’re officially a Swiss-based legal entity.
Where can I examine the MVP and Open source code of #MetaHash?
Decentralized network is currently active with a multi-asset wallet. The source codes of #MetaGate protocol will be released after a public security bounty program for hackers is completed and the patent is obtained.
What is the general aim of the product itself and the token?
#MetaHash is a next-generation network based on the Blockchain 4.0 technology for sharing digital assets and a platform for creating and managing decentralized apps and services in real-time.

MetaHashCoin is exchangeable for transactions, data storage and app functions. You can also spend #MHC to add a public address, list your app in the #MetaApp directory, purchase ads in the #MetaGate adnetwork and much more.
What are the speed capacities of your Blockchain? How can you reach such a result?
#MetaHash is capable of up to 80 000 TPS with under 3 seconds validation. Please check our White Paper to find out how we’re able to achieve such swiftness.
Is your coin mineable? Are there any benefits for the miners?
We’re using a brand-new form of mining called “Forging.” You can forge our coin anywhere, anytime, on basically any PC (or a smartphone). 50% of all rewards for forging are distributed among coin owners. Read the White Paper for more details about our form of mining.
What proof system do you use, POW or POS?
#MetaHash uses a hybrid consensus algorithm. This is a multiPOS system including the vote results from separate network nodes.
#MetaHashCoin and Prospects
What are the general differences between #MetaHash and any other Blockchain provider?
Our decentralization allows us to achieve TPS of up to 80 000 and validation time of under 3 seconds.

As a next-generation Blockchain, #MetaHash is swift, absolutely decentralized and uses low-cost transactions (down to 0 commissions). No other modern Blockchain project answers all of these requirements.
Is #MHC ERC20 emited though the ETH Smart Contract?
#MHC is a separate independent coin that operates in a separate chain. Nevertheless, some mirror contracts in Ethereum and #MetaHash exist. These mirror contracts allow for the tokenizing procedure of ERC20 tokens in both directions.
What is the total supply in tokens?
9,200,000,000 #MHC will be released in the span of 10 years.
What exchange platforms will trade the #MetaHashCoins?
Coin availability on exchange platforms will be announced through all official #MetaHash public channels. We aren’t allowed to share such information in advance.
What will the initial price of the #MHC be after the ICO once it reaches the open market? And after one year?
The cost of #MetaHashCoin is determined by market necessities. #MHC is used to pay for the following services: transactions, data storage, app functioning and so on. These factors will influence the cost of #MHC.
When does the token sale start/end?
ICO is completed and the project awaits the buyout of allocated #MHC.
Other Questions
So in a nutshell, what is #TraceChain?
#TraceChain is the next generation of Blockchain systems. The solution is based on an autonomous self-studying routing algorithm. It starts its function at a speed of 50 000 to 80 000 transactions per second (basic speed of a 100 Mb/s channel) and increases the speed by adding reasonable numbers of high-speed channels. These channels form the core of #MetaHash network and make its work stable.
Hard cap / soft cap?
Hard cap / soft cap of ICO is set to USD 36,000,000.
Is #MetaHash a BTC or ETH fork ?
#MetaHash is not a fork. This is an independent blockchain provider that is much faster and more secure.
