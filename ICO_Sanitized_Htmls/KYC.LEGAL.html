Provably Fair

The reason why many players choose to play in crypto casinos rather than FIAT ones is a system called simply ‘provably fair’.

Each game labeled ‘provably fair’ uses a blockchain technology to store each roll details so that the player can independently verify if it was truly random.

Most commonly provably fair game will provide you with three variables: serverseed, clientseed and nonce. You can copy and paste those variables into an independent verifier which will let you know if the roll was truly random or not.

Provably Fair FAQ

Are all games in bitcoin casinos provably fair?
