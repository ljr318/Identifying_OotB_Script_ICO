  • Our Vision

    Supporting the Fintech Ecosystem

    We are building a digital financial institution designed to support the Fintech ecosystem.

    More Efficient Payments

    This institution will enable domestic and international payments and provide products and services aimed at enabling more efficient trade and transactions.

    Cutting Edge Technology

    We want to design a financial institution from scratch with cutting edge technology to grow and enable value transfer.

    Secure Access

    We take security seriously and having no legacy system means cybersecurity is built-in rather than bolted-on.

    Innovation

    New products and services improve traditional payments.

    Transparency

    We want to help drive competition, transparency and innovation in the financial services sector.

  • What we plan to do

    SUPPORTING THE FUTURE

    We want to support fintech companies with corporate and investment banking.

    INTERNATIONAL PAYMENTS

    We want to provide international payments at low cost, with almost instant settlement (T+0).

    PLATFORM TO ENABLE INNOVATION

    We aim to launch a marketplace that allows innovation.
