KOZJIN Fintech Services

KOZJIN WALLET

Enables users to manage and store their crypto assets securely, which can get traded on the exchange or spent via Kozjin card directly.

KOZJIN CARD

Enables users to withdraw fiat currencies directly from the wallet any time, anywhere after the exchange from cryptocurrencies.

KOZJIN QUICK EXCHANGE

Enables users to convert their crypto assets between multiple currencies stored in the wallet easily.

Chat Features

Instant Peer-to-Peer
Messaging

End-to-end encrypted messenger built into the application with abilities to create groups, broadcast, messages, and create secret chats with timers on messages. The chat feature allows users to send photos, files, audio messages and much more.

Crypto Transfer

Users are able to transfer BTC/ETH/KOZ and other upcoming supported cryptocurrencies. Transfers to other Kozjin users can be sent directly from a chat window or just by entering your contact's name.

  • Individual Chat

    Individual users can create, share and produce unique content via individual chat room with other users.

  • Group Chat

    Users can leverage on the chat by connecting with larger communities and influencers.

  • Channels

    All type of users or companies can create, share, and actively promote content to build different interest channels for both personal and business use.

KOZJIN Property Platform

Real estate on the blockchain:

Kozjin has a visionary attempt in helping to tie real estate with tokens. Kozjin Property Platform will allow property owners to list their property for sale for Kozjin tokens and property buyers to buy properties that are professionally managed by experienced property managers or custodians.
