About

Commit Good is a platform based on the Ethereum blockchain and powered by the $GOOD token. The platform provides an ecosystem of services through an easy-to-use interface, while allowing full transparency and traceability.

Commit Good allows Charitable Organizations to create projects that serve their core mission. Charities around the world will be able to post projects that can now be funded with the $GOOD token allowing donors to give with confidence.
