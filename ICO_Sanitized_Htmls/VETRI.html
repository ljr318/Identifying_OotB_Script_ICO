Personal data - A very valuable resource.

Did you know that your personal data is a very valuable asset? Just by being your true self, organizations and businesses around the world are eager to get to know you… and willing to reward you for learning from your personal data. Until today, businesses traded your information, sometimes without your consent. Now, you have the power to remove the unwanted middlemen and share your data yourself – for a fair price.

Getting rewarded for your own data is simple, safe, and fun.

You do not have to change any of your habits or act in any specific way. Just be who you are and share the information you are willing to share in a completely anonymized way. You will be surprised how important you actually are and what value your data can bring to businesses, researchers, marketers and eventually yourself.

Monetization and the VETRI ecosystem.

VETRI consists of a data wallet and a marketplace. The data wallet allows you to store and manage all your personal data. The marketplace enables you to sell parts of your data to researchers, businesses, or marketers in an anonymized way. As the sole owner of your data, you get to decide who can use it and for which purpose.

Fueled by the VLD token.

Our entire ecosystem is fueled by the VLD token. VETRI uses blockchain technology, ensuring trust in the marketplace and guaranteeing smooth transactions between data owners and data buyers.
