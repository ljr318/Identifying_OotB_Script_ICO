VISION & MISSION
VISION: We envision a future where (autonomous) vehicles communicate securely:
1) with each-other, through vehicle-to-vehicle interaction
2) with the road infrastructure and the Smart City grid
3) with our smartphones through the mobile applications we already developed.
Imagine requesting an autonomous vehicle from a mobile app, sending to do a pickup for you, tracking it and controlling it securely at all times.

MISSION: Build the Autonomy Chain Blockchain Cloud to enable communication and exchange of data and services between connected and future autonomous vehicles and third party applications available on the Autonomy Chain App Store.
BUILDING THE SECURE INTERNET FOR SELF-DRIVING VEHICLES
Scalable
We used blockchain technology that is scalable and has a very low latency in confirming access and transactions.
Real Time
Real Time communication between vehicle and end user with ultra fast response times and notifications.
Flexible
Other developers can build on top of Autonomy Chain through APIs and SDKs. Their apps will be available on the Autonomy Chain App Store.
Native
The fully native iOS and Android we have already developed, give us access to 99% of the smartphone users.
Fast & Secure
Blockchain is the only choice for the security need when controlling access to a connected vehicle. We rely on it for private data and the security of the transactions.
Live Tracking
Ability to track a connected vehicle in real time, control where it goes and what or who it picks up and what the destination is. The vehicle can be owned by you or someone else.
Advanced Reporting
Advanced reporting tools will allow you to tailor your interaction with a connected vehicle with a clear, secure history of payments and other data.
Transparency & Detailed History
Transparency for the prices everyone pays is very important. You control your private data, but public prices will prevent predatory pricing strategies that are used today by ride-share apps.
