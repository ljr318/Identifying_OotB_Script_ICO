One.Platform

NWS.One

Presented in the form of timelines, NWS.One service will show information received from One.Platform in an easy and understandable form.
Using Machine Learning, the platform will be capable of self-learning and analyzing information flows, which will help to show hidden connections between obtained facts and historical background for their change. Also, based on that analysis, the platform will be able to create predictions for interesting events and data.
Those algorithms, joined with modern technologies, will allow to transparently filter obtained information from false facts, thus becoming a base to create an everyday tool for reading news, understand them, discuss and gain access for expert analysis and independent observers opinion.

NWS.One use cases

Earnings

We expect NWS.One to be the service of many benefits. One of it – there will be several possibilities to earn money, making the work on the platform your stable source of income.
The first way to earn money on the platform is by gaining “Oracle” status. Oracles are trustable professionals in their field, which provide analysis of different topics within their expertise for the fixed price.
The second way is to create timelines filled with unique and interesting information, making those timelines available by subscription.
And the third way is to remain open to the community, but being rewarded for your work by showing traditional advertising in your timeline.

Analytics

One of the most important roles in the functionality of One.Platform is the ability to analyze, compare and show you analytics of given subjects. We can achieve that by using Machine Learning and Neural Networks. These two technologies are heavily adoptable and you will be able to analyze not only numbers but the event itself and events that led to it, thus having a full analysis at a glance.

Corporate

With the additional layer of security and encryption algorithms NWS.One service can be used by corporate clients. This service will become easy to use tool to track market changes and thus will help to raise awareness about brand, services or product in the most efficient way. Media corporations will gain convenient and easy to use tool to track world events and opinions of not only experts but also bloggers and other platform users, thus receiving true and reliable information from different sources, which were checked by reliable platform algorithms.

Knowledge platform

Because of the information flow amount NWS.One can be used as a great knowledge platform with proven, checked information. Using timeline system professionals in different fields can track updates and changes in their activity fields. Students, professors and teachers can read, compare and analyze scientific articles of their interest, jurists can easily track legislation changes in real time. Medical workers will have a convenient way to check new researches and technologies in medicine, programmers will be up to date with latest developments and changes in programming languages and technologies.

Bloggers and analytics

The power of Machine Learning and Neural Networks will be a must-to-use tool for bloggers. With the help of One.Platform you will be able to gather information, create analytics within minutes, representing it in a convenient and understandable way. Using platforms fact-checking algorithms you will be able to create proven unique articles, which will attract readers and raise your awareness.

F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I participate ?

We believe that information is the most important resource today. Looking at the most memorable moments of our history we can see that control over information sources is control over whole countries and nations.

In the modern world, there are millions of sources of information and there is no way for a single person, or even for a group of persons to check them all.

As you may have seen – the best and interesting investigations and analytics are done by the crowd – sometimes even thousands of people, which were carefully checking and comparing all the information about specific event or news – finding small pieces of truth and trying to get the whole picture together.

That’s where One.Platform will shine. Modern algorithms designed especially for checking and comparing facts will do the job of thousands of people. Carefully extracting true hidden facts in the massive information flow, this service will be able to show you true, clean facts, numbers, pictures and videos.

Nws.One backed by One.Platform will be the long-awaited revolution in News, Mass Media and Analysis, and we want you to be a part of that revolution!

Why do you need Blockchain ?

We are often asked why do we need Blockchain, instead of using the usual database?
The answer lies in the principles of the blockchain itself. The main principle of blockchain technology is its distributive nature. No centralized version of information stored in Blockchain exists for a hacker to corrupt. Hosted by millions of computers simultaneously, its data is accessible to anyone on the internet and once stored it cannot be changed.

Please read more about how exactly blockchain technology will be used in our whitepaper chapter, called “One.Platform Use Cases”.

So this is another news aggregator?

The short answer is No. The news aggregator is a simple script, which searches for news and shows them to you, sometimes sorting them by the source. As we call it “Information Crawling” is only a small part of planned functions to implement. Most important is that NWS.One service will help you understand and sort the news, filtering fake sources, propaganda articles, fake news and false investigations. All the sorting is transparent and in a simple form is just an advice – the platform will not restrict access to any information sources, it will just advice you the trust level of source you are reading.

For more information, please check out our first website section NWS.One and our whitepaper.

Which Blockchain you will use?

We plan to use Ethereum blockchain for our project because currently, it is the fastest and stable Blockchain network. Though, as you see in our roadmap – in the future we plan to develop our own blockchain with even faster transactions and zero fees for internal platform usage.

What is your plan, if fees for Ethereum transactions will rise?

Current Ethereum fees are quite low and transactions are still quite fast, though in the future we plan to create our own blockchain for internal usage to eliminate such problems. Until we do that – NWST token amount will be more than enough to keep platform working with even higher Ethereum transaction fees, that is why we will keep all the tokens left  after the crowdsale campaign.

How will you manage nodes of your Blockchain and guarantee that major part of them will not be under your control?

That is a good question. Since we are creating open and fully transparent service – we will create a special client, which will act as usual “wallet”, but with additional functionality. You will be able to download blockchain and become one of our so-called “retranslation” nodes, thus helping other people to connect to One.Platform, gaining reward for keeping it up and running.

Please read more about One.P2P in our whitepaper.
