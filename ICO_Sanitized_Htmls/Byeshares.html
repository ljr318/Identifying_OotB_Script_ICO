HOW IT WORKS

THE VERY FIRST LEGAL TOKENIZATION OF CORPORATE EQUITY

The rationale for ByeleX to develop a blockchain-based equity token to represent corporate shares was to prove that legal barriers for businesses seeking to launch this type of tokens, can be demolished.

We are proud to be the first company in Europe that has tokenized its corporate shares on the Ethereum Blockchain. All in line with existing Dutch legislation, thus enabling a completely new, novel and legal way of business financing.

We refer to this type of Initial Coin Offering as STO (Security Token Offering), the new and legal ICO 2.0 SEE VIDEO

WHY PARTICIPATE IN OUR STO?

By joining our STO (Security Token Offering) you will become a true shareholder of ByeleX, the Next Innovation Company , with all the associated benefits such as equity premium, voting rights, and dividend payouts.

HOW DO I PARTICIPATE?

You can obtain  BYESHARES with Ethereum, Bitcoin or Euro. Check out our PROCESS!

BENEFITS OF BUYING BYESHARES

Once you have obtained your shares you are a true shareholder of ByeleX.

  • Shareholders can trade BYESHARES tokens as soon these are registered with one or more exchanges.
  • Registered shareholders are entitled to attend periodical shareholder meetings and have voting rights.
  • Registered shareholders are entitled to dividend payouts.
