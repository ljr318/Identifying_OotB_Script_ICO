DUAL INTERFACE

Our simple User Interface design will provide an intuitive and easy interface to trade. We will also provide a more complex interface for experienced traders with access to specialized tools and analytic facilities.

PRICE ALERTS

With the XERA platform, traders will be able to set SMS and email alerts for a variety of indicators including, but not limited to: MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands, Stochastics, Momentum Oscillators, Moving Averages.

BUILT-IN TRADING AUTOMATION

A scripting environment for trade automation that allows traders to implement their strategies via direct coding so that the trades get executed automatically based on user implemented logic.

TRADING INFORMATION & GUIDES

Be it the UI, withdrawals or expert level trades, you will have access to understand, learn, implement and improve your trading with the help of visuals, voice & AI.
