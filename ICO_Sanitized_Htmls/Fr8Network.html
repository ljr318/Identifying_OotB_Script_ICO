About Us

Safety & Compliance Has Never Been Easier

Fr8 Network was co-founded at the intersection of
logistics and technology, born from a pre-existing
distribution business that has shipped over $1 billion
in freight over the past 15 years. Our co-founders
learned the nuanced struggles that plague the
logistics industry: limited data transparency, poor
incentive alignment, price manipulation, high
coordination costs, theft & fraud, high cost of capital,
shrinking driver supply, poor capacity planning, and
environmental impact.
