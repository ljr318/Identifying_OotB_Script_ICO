Social Responsibility

We are looking to be a legitimate organization with a purpose of serving people who are in need of support and who are looking to develop themselves.

We want to put aside twenty percent of the commissions from platform profits, mainly for the development and education of fundamentals of blockchain technology and cryptocurrency markets.

FAQ

We are working to integrate the system used at 9 Ivy League universities called Canvas into KryptEd.We aim to create a platform that works with BlockCain, using the Swarm and Whisper technologies that the Ethereum community offers.
30,000,000 $KED set aside for content creators and contributors to KryptEd Platform.
Users can collect $KED rewards by completing tasks, creating courses, interaction and by completing a course.
For the first quarter, all courses on Krypted were free to attract as many students as possible. User growth pool was used to incentivize this traction. At this moment, basic courses are offered for free. However, premium & advanced courses are paid.
Students are incentivized to perform the peer-review process for assignments. KED tokens are received for each peer-review that students submit. Proof of effort verification process is defined in our whitepaper. We’re also exploring payment models where students would do quizzes & additional assignments to get some amount of KED tokens back from their initial purchase.
The actual amount of token received for course completion is specified depending on the sponsor. The amount may vary on geolocation, language, and tutors.
Krypted will be offering KED tokens payments to users that begin courses. It’s our mission to provide affordable & high-quality education to as many people as we can.
We believe that knowledge and skills are more important than certification and at the moment Krypted doesn’t offer certificates, but we’ll be looking into certified courses for the future.
We'll start out with IT/cryptocurrency/blockchain related courses at first. These courses will be in English. We feel that it's easiest to learn IT subjects through an online education platform. However, we're open to multilanguage and multisubject support in the future.
