TECHNOLOGY

Coaxial scheme - a scheme in which a pair of parallel mounted screws rotates in opposite directions around a common geometric axis. On rotorcraft, it allows you to mutually compensate for the reactive moments of a pair of rotors, while maintaining the most dense layout of the drives. This type of model has much greater stability compared to the classical scheme, which makes the model ideal for beginners and / or flights in a limited space.
The helicopter with coaxial rotors has a relatively small size, compact enough, which simplifies its maintenance, storage, transportation, expands the scope. Small dimensions, reducing the mass separation, create small moments of inertia, so the helicopter has large angular rotational speeds and high maneuverability.
The symmetrical layout with a minimum spacing of screws simplifies aerobatics in gusty wind conditions, which is especially valuable when working in ship-based or in
mountainous areas. The absence of a bulky tail boom facilitates piloting at low altitudes, improves flight safety over rough terrain, and simplifies the implementation of emergency landings.

Anti-Dron (UAV) System

IAM Anti-UAV Defense System (AUDS) is consist of detection unit, UAV tracking unit and jammer device. It integrates target detection, tracking and recognition, command & control and jamming multi functions in one.
Based on different application scenario, it can be flexibly deployed into an optimal solution by choosing different detection unit and jammer device.
AUDS can be used by fixed installation, portable type and vehicle mounted type.
By fixed installation type, AUDS is widely used in high level security protection site and portable type is used a lot for temporary prevention & control in key conference, sport events, concert etc., vehicle mounted type is normally used for routine patrol or more.
