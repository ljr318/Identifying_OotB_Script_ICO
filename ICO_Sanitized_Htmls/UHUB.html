What is our philosophy?

In one word: Simplification

In a world marked by volatility, continuous evolution and complexity, we need tools that give us peace of mind and visibility. This enables us to focus on the essentials in our work.
So far, the digital possibilities have “only” increased our efficiency in partial areas. It is already possible for us to serve a much larger number of channels in communication. However, the effectiveness and consistency of communication have suffered from this so far.

uhub increases this sustainably by supporting the focus on the relevant tasks of each step.

Who is behind uhub.io?

uhub.io is a spin-off of BKW, an internationally active energy and infrastructure company based in Berne. BKW recognised the current changes in the energy business at an early stage and diversified its activities. In addition to the energy business, a rapidly growing service group has emerged.

The communications team was required to coordinate its work with an increasing number of companies with different headquarters and to quickly increase the efficiency and effectiveness of its communications measures. As supporting software was not available on the market, it was developed by the company itself in a typical engineering spirit and in close cooperation with the practitioners of the communications team.
