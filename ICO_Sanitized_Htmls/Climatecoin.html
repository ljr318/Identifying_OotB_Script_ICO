FORWARD THINKING BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY

CLIMATETRADE is based on BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY, a decentralised database (Distributed Ledger Technology or DLT) that stores a registry of assets, data and transactions across a peer to peer network.

TRANSPARENT

Every transaction and its associated value are visible to anyone with access to the system.

FAST

Blockchain guarantees a faster implementation of programmed tasks and actions, and quick reclaim of the information and assets in the ledger.

EFFICIENT

Blockchain reduces the costs involved in verifying transactions, by removing the need for trusted “third-parties”.
