Check faster. Check more often. Prove it.

Since 2013 there have been several Royal Commissions into how the most vulnerable Australians, in particular children, have been subjected to harm and cover ups in institutions where they should have been cared for and protected.

At duty of care we imagined a safer world for children and other vulnerable people. Our platform strengthens the Australian Working with Children Check framework and helps organisations to easily manage these checks and other professional accreditations.

A better way to manage checks and make children safer.

With DutyofCare you can;

  • Check the current status of Working with Children Checks for your entire workforce in less than 5 minutes.
  • Automatically check everyone, every week, with absolutely no increase to your workload.
  • Run real-time checks on the ground, so you can be assured that last minute substitute referee/coach/carer is safe to work with children.
  • Be notified when something needs your attention, like a revoked or expired card.
  • Have access to a permanent, accurate, tamper-proof audit trail.

We know that keeping on top of Working with Children Checks is just a small part of what you need to do to protect the children in your care, but every little bit of rigour makes a difference.
