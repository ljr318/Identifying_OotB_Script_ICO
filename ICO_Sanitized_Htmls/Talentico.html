Mission

TALENTICO project mission lies in the creation of the ecosystem where talented token holders will be investing their means in order to gain maximum profit, thereby offering equal opportunities to unlock their potential to the gifted people irrespective of their age, social status and location.

Market problems

  • Lack of developed infrastructure for expert consultations and support from professionals (influential people in business, sports, fashion, art, politics, tech community etc.)
  • Lack of transparency in communication among aspiring talents, their agents and intermediaries
  • Large number of intermediaries

Global

  • Modern science, economics and culture are in need of talented people. The humanity is carrying the weight of unresolved problems ranging from the incurable diseases, global warming, racism, intolerance to the issues of balanced development on the whole planet

  • TALENTICO platform will help talents to gain recognition and achieve their fullest potential so that they could bring good to society and the world. Gifted people will be able to find sponsor support and mentorship.

  • Business will gain access to a wide and segmented audience of potential clients and employees.
