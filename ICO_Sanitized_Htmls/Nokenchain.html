Explanations and disadvantages of “normal” masternodes

A masternode is a means of investing and securing a blockchain. It is also one of the 3 main ways to validate blocks on a blockchain with cryptomining and proof of stake (staking), and thus be rewarded for this work.

It is generally composed of 2 elements : the wallet of the concerned cryptocurrency and a Linux Virtual Private (Internet) Server (VPS). A computer program is started on the VPS and exchanges data with the entire network of the concerned cryptocurrency. The VPS must be permanently connected to the Internet.

To function, you first need a “collateral” (starting capital / deposit), ie a certain number of units of a cryptocurrency (for example 1000 cryptocoins). Once you have assembled the collateral, you can create a masternode. The collateral helps reduce price volatility and makes Sybil attacks almost impossible. All required cryptocoins for the collateral must be credited in 1 single transaction.

The masternode concept, invented by Dash in 2015, has been adopted by more than 370 other cryptocurrencies.

By creating a masternode, you secure the concerned blockchain, which in turn allows you to earn rewards (block rewards), paid in the cryptocurrency linked to the masternode (for example in DASH, if you have a DASH masternode).
You receive this reward in cryptocurrency at a regular interval (generally 1 to 20 days, depending on the cryptocurrencies).

Advantages : No machine to have at home, regular compensation. The collateral can be recovered in cryptocurrency at any time.
Disadvantages : Requires collateral in the concerned cryptocurrency, rent a virtual server (VPS) and install the program on Linux. The value of the cryptocurrency may drop.

The Nokenchain solution : stablenodes

Stablenodes correct several drawbacks :

  • It is not necessary to have all of the collateral.
  • It is not necessary to have the concerned cryptocurrency to acquire the stablenode (you can get it by paying in euros).
  • No more renting (and therefore paying monthly) a virtual server (VPS).
  • No need to know how to install a program under Linux.
  • The collateral is guaranteed against the euro. Even if the value of the cryptocurrency drops, you can recover the amount invested in the collateral at any time without loss of value !

Technical explanations

  • Stablenodes are a secure system, invented by Nokenchain, whereby cryptocurrency owners can generate stable passive income.
  • The algorithm developed by Nokenchain secures the original collateral against the US dollar on the day of creation of the stablenode, which avoids significant variations in the value of the collateral. It is linked to the Nokenchain Stable Protocol (NSP) allowing you to receive rewards generated by the growth of the Waves blockchain activity and the growth of the Nokenchain ecosystem.
  • The earnings generated through these economic activities (Waves transaction fees, validation of new blocks, sales of Nokenchain services, …) are converted into the chosen cryptocurrency and paid to you on your wallet in the form of masternode rewards.
  • The advantages are that the rate of pay is not managed by a bank and is higher than in banks. The expected yield is 5 to 12.50% per year.
  • Rewards are sent weekly. You can check yourself in your wallet that the rewards have arrived and withdraw your collateral at any time on request. You can never lose the gains already accumulated.
