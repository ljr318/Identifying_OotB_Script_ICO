Effortless Investment

Bamboo is a microinvestments platform that allows anyone to seamlessly purchase digital assets, like Gold and Bitcoin. It's an easy, secure, and effective way to participate in the blockchain revolution. All you need is a mobile phone and a bank account to get started.

Put your spare change to work

Bamboo monitors your selected bank accounts for transactions. We then round to the nearest full value and gracefully invest the spare change from your daily expenses into a diverse portfolio of assets.
