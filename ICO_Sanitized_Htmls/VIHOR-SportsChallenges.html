Introduction

VIHOR is a blockchain database that supports community-building and social interaction with cryptocurrency rewards in addictive game with real-time online photo competition system for action sports.

VIHOR combine concepts from social media in community building and social interaction with lessons from building cryptocurrencies and their communities.

AS FEATURED ON:

Vihor Opportunity

Extreme sports athletes that don't have social media platform that could make them feel comfortable enjoying in the environment that is tailored to fulfill all their needs.

Lack of one universal app for extreme & active sports

Absence of social networks that pay users for creating and curating sport content

No specialized social network to track and promote achievements of extreme sports athletes

No easy way to identify and contact influencers in extreme & active sports

Lack of opportunity to engage with sponsors and brands

Solution

lncentivized Blockchain Social Media Platform for extreme and active sports athletes

New global community connecting athletes & sports fans worldwide

Creating and curating sport content & achievements with built-in rewards

Addictive gaming with rewards through daily challenges and interaction

Opportunity for great, talented and passion driven athletes to seek sponsorships from a pool of innovative and globally recognized brands

Partnering innovative and globally recognized brands with VIHOR ambassadors

FAQ

What is unique about the company?

VIHOR is the only social network and global community for extreme and active sports athletes in the world with comprehensive set of features and services that this social group need and want to have.
Beside this, VIHOR is the only social network that pays users to publish or curate content, sharing images and videos with their friends with addictive gaming with rewards through daily challenges and interaction.
VIHOR provides opportunity for great, talented and passion driven athletes to seek sponsorship from a pool of innovative and globally recognized brands.

How can I follow more news and announcements from Vihor?

Vihor has a growing community and social media presence. You can find us on the official @vihorapp Twitter, Telegram, Instagram, Facebook and LinkedIn.

What will the native VIHOR token be called?

Token name: VIHOR token | Sticker Symbol: VHX

What is the VHX token used for?

VIHOR is social media platform with highly targeted audience that is rewarding its users with cryptocurrency rewards that can be used to purchase items from VIHOR online store, get discounts on items or various services on VIHOR platform.
VIHOR is a blockchain database that supports community-building and social interaction with cryptocurrency rewards in addictive game with real-time online photo competition system for action sports.
VIHOR combine concepts from social media in community building and social interaction with lessons from building cryptocurrencies and their communities.
VIHOR will inspire participation in the community through fair accounting system that will accurately and transparently reward our users who make subjective contributions to the community.

Where can I store my tokens?

Tokens can be stored on ERC20 supported wallets. Vihor will have the VHX exchange wallet available soon after the end of token sale.

Who can participate?

Individuals and entities from People’s Republic of China, United States of America, Canada, Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, and the Crimea Region would not be eligible to participate in the sale of BCT tokens.

What Cryptocurrencies do we accept for the BCT Pre-Sale?

We accept BTC, Steem, Bitshares and ETH.

What is the minimum contribution amount for the Vihor private sale?

Minimum contribution is 10k USD in BTC or ETH.

Will there be a discount for the Pre-Sale?

Where will I be able to buy BCT Token?

What happens if the funds raised are between the soft cap and the hard cap?
