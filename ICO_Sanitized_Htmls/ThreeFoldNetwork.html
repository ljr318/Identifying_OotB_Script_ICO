THREE LAYERS | for a truly efficient internet

ThreeFold had to revise the whole Internet infrastructure to enable a truly equal, autonomous and sustainable Internet. Our Autonomous Technology replaces 4 of the current 7 layers of the Internet infrastructure, resulting in a much simpler system that is truly efficient, scalable, sustainable, affordable and peer-to-peer.

explainer video

FOUNDATION

THE CAPACITY
LAYER

Nodes distributed around the world by independenet Farmers form the ThreeFold Grid - a peer-to-peer network of compute, network and storage capacity. It provides the physical architecture needed to make all our digital experiences possible.

Learn more

BRIDGE

THE AUTONOMOUS LAYER

Autonomous technology deploys and operates the IT infrastructure and its services. This layer is home to 3Bots - our digital assistants, that ensure autonomy by managing our processes and workloads - no middlemen involved.

learn more

LIFE

THE EXPERIENCE
LAYER

Where users can access peer-to-peer applications (social, office, productivity, finances, and more) and where creators can build new ones. Any programming language, application or workload that works on Linux is compatible with our tech.

learn more

TFT| THE TOKEN OF THE NEW INTERNET

ThreeFold Token (TFT) is a decentralized digital currency used to buy autonomous and decentralized Internet services (compute, storage, and application) on the ThreeFold Grid. TFT synergistically connects the world of cloud and blockchain markets to the most scalable and advanced peer-to-peer infrastructure to provide true performance, security and privacy.
