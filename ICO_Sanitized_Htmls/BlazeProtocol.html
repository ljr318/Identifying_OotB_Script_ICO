New-Age
Multi-Dimensional Ledger

Blaze is a block-lattice that can spawn in a multidimensional space, designed as a robust platform for dApps.

Blaze is a first of its kind block-lattice protocol designed for the ever-expanding needs of the digital industry. Its unique infrastructure offers three advantages: making the governance effortless, improving the transaction processing speed and reducing blockchain bloat. The protocol has been designed to be robust and secure to support mission-critical applications. At the same time, it is flexible and resilient to keep up with the ever-changing needs of the industry.

Moreover, Blaze is a blockchain-as-a-service-platform where businesses can set up their own isolated ecosystem without worrying about the security or a need to code. With features like lightweight smart contracts, smart transactions, built-in voting, messaging and more - Blaze ensures that the businesses transition fluently into the next era of technology.

Structure

Blaze is the first DLT that can spawn in multiple dimensions, when desired. This feature allows the dApp owners to build their products and services in isolated sub Dimensions while relying on the security provided by the main Dimension.

Consensus

The WPOS consensus mechanism takes into account the credibility of candidates that wish to run the system in a decentralized manner. The integrity of electoral contenders is measured using the Prestige score.

Governance

Blaze incorporates a real-world-like governance model right at the protocol level, thereby solving the shortcomings that couldn’t be addressed by Consensus alone.

Scalability

The unique block-lattice structure allows a sub Dimension to originate, whenever the load on the parent Dimension gets overwhelming. This makes the system’s scalability theoretically limitless.

Bloating

The transactions on sub Dimensions are pruned once they are confirmed. This keeps the actual Dimension lightweight and efficient.

Reliability

Blaze has evolved from the time-tested DLTs with years of production experience. Also, it is developed in C++, the most powerful & reliable language for development of mission-critical and low-latency applications.
