ABOUT

While governments put all communication channels and financial flows under total control to fight against terrorism and corruption, people private data and user behavior are permanently traced in the internet… personal comfortable space of individual protected information decreased dramatically. Toxic new cryptography which based on KCG system with GVEM will be implemented  to guarantee that no third party can get any private information. To reach this we are developing a set of software solutions (Toxiс Ecosystem).
At the moment Toxic messenger MVP version is already available to the public (based on TLS 1.2, DTLS till new KCG & GVEM encryption is developed).

Keyless

Keyless encryption technology eliminates the human factor, the possibility of theft, bribery or voluntary intent to transfer keys to other persons.

GVEM

Completely eliminates the possibility of hacking, including the brute-force attack due to Geometric Vector Encryption Method

multi-level

Multi-level (up to 6 levels) encryption makes the system completely protected against the third party interference.

Freedom
