Diamonds Vs. Gold

  • THE COST OF INVESTING IN DIAMONDS IS LOWER THAN INVESTING IN GOLD, DUE TO THEIR SIZE. AS SUCH, STORAGE AND CUSTODIAL COSTS ARE LOWER.
  • Diamonds have a variety of categories, reflecting different risk/reward profiles. Gold has just one price to cover all products (ETF/ physical or futures).
  • The public currently holds more than $500 billion of diamonds without profitable liquidation channels.
  • Diamonds are a market with huge expansion potential. Gold is a saturated market.
  • Gold = 20% financial product vs. 80% jewelry & industrial
  • Diamonds = 1% financial product vs 99% jewelry & industrial
