    A BRIEF INSIGHT INTO EXTRACREDIT AND BITCOIN HOMEWORK

    Extra Credit
    Extra Credit was created with the average working-class person in-mind; people who have no, or very little knowledge of CryptoCurrency. Our vision is to empower everyone with the knowledge required to invest and manage their CryptoCurrencies with ease. This means that anyone can participate in ExtraCredit, with or without past CryptoCurrency experience.

    Our Team
    Our Team Extra Credit brings together a team of 10 members, dedicated to teaching our users all they need to know about CryptoCurrencies.     FAQ's

    Have any questions? Before contacting one of our team members take a look at our Frequently asked Questions below

    How do I Purchase tokens?
    You can easily purchase Extra Credit (XTRA) through one of our Exchanges we are listed on, click here to view our "Exchange Listings" section, simply create an account with the Exchange and purchase our token, the process will usually take around ten minutes. Feel free to contact us for assistance if you have any questions or issues whilst doing so.
    Who is ExtraCredit targeted at?
    Extra Credit is targeted at people looking to advance or start their path in the Cryptocurrency world. We feel that it's moving at such a speed, a speed that is truly unbelivable and it now needs an Educational Cryptocurrency and learning platform to support its progress.
    Where do I store my Extra Credit after I purchase XTRA from an Exchange?
    You can easily store your Extra Credit tokens on a wallet that supports our ERC-20 token, please head over to our "Wallets Section" and download your preferred wallet. You can then generate a sending address on which you can send to from the Exchange where you purchased the XTRA tokens from. Contact our support if you have any doubts / issues whilst doing so and we will happily guide you through the process.
    How do I stay up to date with Extra Credit's & BitcoinHomework's latest news?
    You can be kept up to date by joining our newsletter, by signing up to our newsletter you will receive news regarding upgrades and updates including company changes such as new staff or partnerships that relate to Extra Credit & Bitcoin Homework. We do not send spam or distribute our subscribers emails to any third party company, period.
    What is Bitcoin Homework?
    Bitcoin Homework is our Learning platform, which is here to aid people looking to learn about Cryptocurrency, this includes the positive and negative points, the invesment opportunities and the technical sides of Cryptocurrency, we are looking to educate the public and to also educate business' about how they can implement Bitcoin and other Cryptocurrencies to their platform maximising profits and minimizing risk taking away the fear of the unknown!
    How do I earn XTRA tokens?
    Once our mobile app is up and running the learn and earn will come to life, BitcoinHomework desktop version will be there for anyone who wishes to take FREE high quality courses that teach the individual key information about the particular topic / subject they wish to learn in the world of cryptocurrency. Whereas the mobile app will be on a monthly basis and the user will be paid for completing actions, the more work completed, the more the student earns.
