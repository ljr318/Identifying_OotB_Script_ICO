Flagship Project

Solar-wind Technology

USAT Inc.âs flagship project of 2018 is the solar-wind farm: a new renewable energy technology developed through the USAT Inc. platform. Solar-wind farms provide clean energy more affordable than any other energy system on the market, renewable or otherwise.
Solar-wind technology uses the sun to heat air within a tube. As the hot air rises, it travels along the tube and must pass a turbine to escape, turning it and generating power. It can also utilise the waste-heat generated by factories and industry to produce power. This simple mechanism has few moving parts and uses low cost materials.
Itâs incredibly simple modular design is sturdy and low maintenance, making it highly cost-effective to build, run, and maintain. It can also be built out of entirely recycled materials, which reduces its carbon footprint to zero.
