About

By utilizing cutting-edge, crowd-sourced technology, Spectre.ai matches traders against an embedded liquidity pool or other traders on the Spectre trading platform. 24 hour liquidity and zero-opportunity for fraud is ensured in this unique model!

No broker*

STATE OF THE ART VERIFICATION

In a traditional trading scenario, the settlement happens behind the curtains and at times, doesn't even happen. At Spectre.ai, every trade transaction on our online trading platform is mined and settled on a transparent digital ledger or aggregated every 24 hours and sent to a public digital ledger for verification. A growing network of processing nodes (over 20,000) around the world will confirm the trade entries and exits (using independent 3rd party verification of entry and exit prices) along with debits and credits to trader accounts. This is the first trading platform in the world that offers a globally based audit trail. *

Spectre.ai is the first platform to allow retail traders to trade Digital Contracts, a new trading contract-class with a verified outcome resulting in payouts of up to 400% (in some cases), within one second. Digital Contracts can be traded on a range of assets including traditional currency pairs, and other assets too. Last but not least, Spectre.ai also offers traditional A-Book FX trading. Constant and Live Crowdsourced Liquidity

Spectre.ai, unlike other brokers, shows its live liquidity pool that is crowdsourced and acts as counterparty to trades on the platform. This means that even if there is 1 trader or millions trading, Spectre.ai always has liquidity. This liquidity is protected and secured giving traders long term comfort. Traders trade either against each other (p2p) or against the liquidity pool. This means no trader is trading against a shady broker's balance sheet who may choose to pay out or not. Neither Spectre.ai nor the traders have access to the crowdsourced liquidity pool. Only next generation immutable contracts can extract or send funds from and to the crowdsourced liquidity pool, respectively.

Broadest Asset Range

Spectre.ai allows for the synthetic (no underlying ownership) of a range of asset classes, whether its currencies, stocks, bonds, ETFs, indices, Epic and many more. Digital Contracts and CFDs, the unique contract types in the platform, can be taken by traders on a broad range of assets.
