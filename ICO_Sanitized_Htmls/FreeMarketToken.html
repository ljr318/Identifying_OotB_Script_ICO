About Us

Using the powerful concept of Incentivised Gift Marketing, Freemarket Token encourages consumers to engage with brand marketing and advertising by incentivising consumers to directly interact with the marketing message.

The concept is to redirect a portion of the advertising spend towards incentives that are provided to consumers as a reward in the form of FMT tokens which can be used to tip others or redeemed through various platforms and participating merchants on Ecryptostore.com
