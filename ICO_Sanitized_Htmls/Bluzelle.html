What is Bluzelle?

Bluzelle enables Dapps to store their data in a place that is both decentralized and mutable, getting the best of everything. Keep your data fresh but safe from hackers and censorship-resistant.

  • Instant Global Coverage. Developers get multi-region coverage for the same price. Data is stored across North America, Europe and Asia. Expansion soon to Africa, South America and Australia.
  • Community Network. Data servers are crowd-sourced, reducing costs for developers and passive income for providers. Enhances network security, scalability and lowers global impact.
  • Wide-spread Availability. Through the inter-blockchain communication protocol (IBC), Bluzelle is ready to be used by any blockchain. Javascript support opens availability to millions of developers
