OUR STORY

Hatchworks is a venture firm with an in-house incubator. The company thoroughly vets, invests in and monitors its portfolio investments through an Investment Committee (IC) and Operations Committee (OC). Hatchnet , which is its proprietary algorithmic investment insight platform is now open to all HATCHTOKEN holders, giving early access and insight on industry leading VC deals that have eluded the masses for decades. While VCs keep the best deals to themselves, we share, through our upcoming series of Hatchfunds.

OUR TEAM

Our highly experienced Investment Committee (IC) and Operations Committee (OC) is comprised of veteran investment bankers from JP Morgan, Goldman Sachs and successful entrepreneurs who have had successful exits. All investments, whether or not they are early-stage seed investments or later stage growth capital, go through a strict quantitative (Hatchscore) and qualitative assessment process before which term sheets are studied from a deal mechanics and legal perspective. Positions are then entered and portfolio construction is conducted carefully , taking into account asset-specific valuations and correlation on a sector and cross-portfolio basis, as well.
