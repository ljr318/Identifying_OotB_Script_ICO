Kepler Ecosystem

Kepler Ecosystem is an innovative platform that will commission high-tech initiatives for developing breakthroughs through global teamwork. The platform will allow talented and gifted minds to participate in the development of technological solutions or link up with the right resources and personnel to bring their ideas to life. Not only does Kepler network act as a direct gateway for disintermediated connections, but also it provides investors with unique investment opportunities in high-techâs most disruptive branches: Robotics &AI.


Kepler Ecosystem will work as a social network where idea generators(founders) can initiate global team-building process, adding missing pieces and even assembling their teams from ground-up. Secure Funding
Kepler Accelerator funding model taps into a larger and a more diverse participant market, supported by blockchain technology. Any individual, not just traditional institutional investors or high net worth individuals, can participate in all financing rounds. Kepler Accelerator provides its participants a liquid marketplace, where Startup founders benefit from a relatively quick and secure method to raise capital for their project without ceding strategic control.
Accelerator

The decentralized Venture crowdfunding program will be the main tool of operation for start-ups who have successfully passed the screening and start project development for consumer market entry. Kepler Accelerator is a medium for Individual investors, investor networks and centralized venture funds who seek to participate in Seed, First and Second rounds of investment.


Kepler Accelerator presents the opportunity to disrupt the early stage financing ecosystem by eliminating geographical barriers between investors and startups seeking funding. The potential of a decentralized venture fund opens new opportunities for various stakeholders, where investors can select opportunities based on merit rather than solely on geographic location or professional network.
