Our Solutions

Distributed oracle tools and services.

  • Φ
    Token
  • Frameworks
  • Applications

With our oracle coin (PHI) we introduce the compound token architecture, fusing minimal and signal components into one.
This currently represents the most sophisticated token construct on Ethereum.

Get PHI from our Autobid Contract , and convert between PHI and sPHI with our Token Contract .
Our weighted signature oracle framework Pythia provides a host of benefits over other oracle solutions.
Our prediction market framework Omphalos was designed to capitalize on the same advantages.

We consider Pythia to be the most promising distributed oracle solution envisioned to date, and Omphalos to be its killer app.
The existence of high-quality distributed oracle services enables a wide variety of new use cases for smart contracts and blockchains.
Oracles make event-dependent contracts (like price pegs, IoT functions, DAO governance, and prediction markets) possible.

Experiment with a beta version of Omphalos to begin exploring the possibilities.
Agora represents the culmination of all the components of the Delphi platform into a decentralized oracle marketplace.
Our flexible, framework-based approach and the signaling function of our compound token allow a true oracle reputation network.

The Oracles of Agora will power the smart contract economy of tomorrow.
