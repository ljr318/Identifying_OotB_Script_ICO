FAQ

What is Krosscoin?

Krosscoin is a very elegant concept: we build innovative apps that have a large customer base potential. We also allow third-party developer apps into our ecosystem. All apps are monetized with KSS tokens using a ‘’PAY-AS-YOU-USE’’ framework.

This allows app developers to make interesting and highly functional apps, and lets the end-consumer pay for only the functions accessed, when they access or use it.

Our first apps utilizing the Krosscoin Platform is the patent pending Pipdroid app family. Pipdroid apps lets forex and crypto traders launch and manage trading bots right from their smartphones. Imagine being able to access and launch bots to both forex broker platforms and crypto exchanges, right from your hand held device, anywhere you are!!. Join us as we revolutionize one industry after the other with our amazing applications

How many products will Krosscoin have?

Krosscoin will have both own developed dapps and third-party dapps using the Kross Monetization Platform. Krosscoin ecosystem will have at least 100 dapps by Q4 2019.

What is KSS-ETH?

Krosscoin is a multi-blockchain project. The reason for this is so our dapps can be relevant to specific blockchain communities who are used to that blockchain native asset. Krosscoin thinks business and revenue first. KSS-ETH is our ethereum token. KSS-ETH has a 100 million max supply, has over $1m in token swap deals with several great projects, and will be listed in top 20 exchanges.

What exchanges will Krosscoin be listed in?

Krosscoin has been confirmed for listing, is trading or is in final talks with the following exchanges: IDEX, Forkdelta, Etherdelta, Bancor, Bitozz, Extradecoin, Metamorph.pro, ETERBASE, HitBTC, Coinbene, BitMart, TokenJar, DDEX, Waves, LAToken, BitForex

Why does Krosscoin need a blockchain?

Krosscoin uses the blockchain to create a decentralized monetization framework that disrupts the $200B mobile app market. Through smart contracts, app monetization can be made more efficient and revenue can flow to developers without going through third-parties. Krosscoin will also have own blockchain, Kross Blockchain in 2019.

Can every KSS holder potentially get paid monthly?

Using basic economics and if Krosscoin meets all product and partnership goals, Krosscoin could achieve its aim of being a $20 billion market cap project by Q4 2020.

KSS-WAVES to KSS-ETH swap rate?

Krosscoin is a multi-blockchain project with a maximum supply of 25.1 Billion tokens.

KSS-WAVES has a supply of 25 Billion tokens and KSS-ETH has a supply of 100 million. Both are regarded as part of the common 25.1 billion supply and will swap at 1:1 with each other as the token reserves can support. Community members may also swap P2P at same 1:1 swap rate As Krosscoin expands to other blockchains, the new KSS created in those blockchains will be burnt from the KSS-WAVES 25 billion supply. For example, if 300 million KSS is created on EOS blockchain, then 300 million KSS will be burnt from KSS-WAVES supply so that Krosscoin will always be a 25.1 Billion max supply token project

Did Krosscoin raise funds through an ICO?

Krosscoin has had 2 ICO events, for KSS-ETH in March-May 2018, and for KSS-WAVES in November - Dec 7, 2018. But on these occasions, the project refunded investors and shut down the ICO, preferring to raise funds from private and business partners and from tokens sold on the exchanges. Krosscoin has so far raised funds from team members, business partners and exchange sales.
