EVA APP

EVA APP connects with EVAIO Blockchain Main-Net, you can use it to issue transaction, delegate your EVA to Block Producers, propose and vote the change of parameters of EVAIO Main-Net. Also you can view the information and suggestion from vehicle data miner regarding your car, and order offline vehicle maintenance service and parts via EVA APP.

● A trustworthy EVA wallet
● Send a transaction to become the Block Producer
● Delegate your EVA to Block Producers
● Withdraw Rewards from EVA Delegation
● Burn EVA
● EVAIO Ecosystem


Use Cases

Vehicles contribute data and get EVA rewards from EVAIO blockchain, EVAIO also allows any third-parties to access certain portions of the collected data after paying in form of EVA tokens. EVA tokens will be kept in a wallet of EVAIO software in vehicle OS. Based on the wallet we create EVPAY, which will open APIs to third parties, allowing more auto-based express services to exist in EVA ecosystem.

Ecosystem



The data contributed will be analyzed, so EVA APP can provide list of proposals regarding vehicle health, Vehicle owners can use EVA APP to order and pay for parts and services. The profit of EVA APP will be used to buy EVA and refuel the Vehicle Mining Pool to ensure balance.
