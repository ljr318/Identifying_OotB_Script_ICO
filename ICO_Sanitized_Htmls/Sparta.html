SPARTA token, what is it exactly?

1 token to power 300 startups & the ecosystem around them!

SPARTA token is to be used inside the 300 startups as a kind of credits if the application is a non blockchain application, or as a utility token/precursor to other tokens if it is a blockchain application. In addition to the startups, several buisnesses will be accepting SPARTA to give access to products & services.

1 Million SPARTA token is given to each of the startups, a big part of it is then distributed to power the ecosystem. The token will not be sold, it is given for FREE. However, investors can get the token from the startups, or by being part of the ecosystem & supporters network.
