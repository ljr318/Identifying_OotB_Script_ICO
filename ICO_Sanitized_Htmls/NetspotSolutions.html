Why Netspot?

NetSpot allows administrators to fully control the process of physically managing, distributing, charging, and monitoring an organisation’s fleets of devices. What makes an ideal solution for many markets is that it facilitates:

  • Access Control
  • Inventory Management
  • Security
  • Privacy (all data can be wiped from device following rental)
  • User Identification
  • Content Deployment
  • Network Administration
  • Reporting

Interconnectivity between networks

Netspot is building a cutting-edge sharing economy for the Internet of Things. With Netspot, networks of all types (decentralised or centralised) become interoperable and establish linkages, opening up entirely new possibilities for consumers of devices and data.
