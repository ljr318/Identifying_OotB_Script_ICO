ABOUT KINGDOM GAME 4.0

The Game Industry Revolution on Blockchain Platform

Sharing
Economy model

Game publisher, Agents and Gamers play important roles as a trivet, consistently, supporting each other in this Economic model. Agents and Gamers can use available resources in Kingdom Game 4.0 to generate revenue and income for themselves. Kingdom Game 4.0 shall use 25 – 50% of the revenue to refund Gamers through promotions or Tournaments.

In-game
Assets Guarantee

All precious assets in game as characters, items,... shall be guaranteed (value guarantee). The point is optimize Personal assets in game. Guaranteed Level shall be up to 75% of the product value and ensuring the liquidity value in long term.

Payment
by Crypto-currency

Kingdom Game 4.0 released our own platform specific digital currency named KDG. In game payment transaction shall be applied Blockchain technology using KDG token. Gamers use KDG to buy items, upgrading level or use other in-game services transparently and safely.

Referral Program

We understand and appreciate the support from our users. It’s an important part of our success. Therefore, Kingdom Game 4.0 offers Referral Commission program to help users increasing their income and thereby, being our most sincere gratitude we would like to send to our users.

Playing game
generating income

By joining Kingdom Game 4.0, you have opportunity to increase from 30 – 50% of your current income through playing game, completing in-game quests or referral commission as well as other in-game activities.

Rights to invest
& own the Game

You shall have rights to invest by holding shares of the Project, thereby it would help you generating your profits with us.

Civil rights of
Kingdom Game 4.0

This is a different rights to other regular game models, you shall have the right to vote for in-game activities, events.
