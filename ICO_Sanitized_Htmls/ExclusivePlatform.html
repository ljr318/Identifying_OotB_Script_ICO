What is XPL?

Exclusive Platform connects project developers with the right blockchain audience in a simplified fashion. Our platform empowers everyone to develop and run an awesome blockchain idea without worrying about technical, funds, and management required. Exclusive Platform let you focus on your product development and enhance your productivity.


Event

Automated airdrop, bounty and contest can be hosted on Exclusive Platform with just a click of a button. Our airdrop and contest have a built­ in referral system which we have combined with a strict rule to prevent cheating the system by fraudulent participants.

Excrow

Excrow Platform is a reliable, secure, and simplified free to use escrow platform built on the blockchain ecosystem using our simple solution with a level of trust not yet known outside the traditional commerce space.

  • Crypto to fiat
  • Instant swap
  • Earn while you trade

Exsender

Free, simplified and fully automated token and ETH distributor on the blockchain. Distribute token to thousands of addresses in few minutes.

  • Save time and gas
  • Scan wallet and lift tokens
  • Free to use!
