PRESS QUOTES

One of the brightest, most intriguing, young startups promising to become the great companies of tomorrow, FintruX Network is a business financing platform for underserved startups and SMEs powered by blockchain technology. Key partners include Microsoft, Amazon Web Services and LexisNexis.

TruX, the world's first smart business financing platform, aims to empower startups and SMEs to achieve business growth and sustainability. The platform creates a global business network powered by blockchain technology, benefiting all stakeholders.

Harnessing over two decades of experience serving the securitization sector, FintruX Network applies cascading levels of credit enhancements to offer unprecedented risk reduction towards unsecured loans.

FintruX Network has a first-mover advantage globally and a strong foothold in its home base of Singapore. Partners in the region look at TruX as a global solution for predatory lending that is increasingly common.

FintruX Network has innovatively addressed the main challenges of SMEs with its blockchain technology. Many of these challenges are specific to SMEs and TruX is turning these challenges into business opportunities for SMEs to grow.

FintruX Network is playing in a clear market, with the goal to become one of the first blockchain applications that starts adding value in the real world. They are a well thought through company with a lot of good ambitions for the start up sector.

FintruX Network is a revolutionary new lending platform that incorporates blockchain to provide a fast and safe way of obtaining an affordable unsecure loan.

TruX empowers businesses to create a Trust Profile that establishes a dynamic credit reputation based on alternative credit data. This will help facilitate credit terms and commercial loans sustainably. The technology is available to any business across the globe.

This innovative concept solves major issues for small businesses, especially startups, to borrow from accredited investors and financial institutions.

Today, companies such as FintruX Network can offer funding solutions that were not available to small and medium-sized business owners of the past. The company leverages blockchain technology to work for businesses with its smart business financing platform, TruX.

FintruX Network seeks to neutralize the lender's credit risk and, in the case of a default, provide cascading levels of insurances to cover the loss – increasing the overall credit rating and lowering interest rates simultaneously.

Fintrux Network is making borrowing and lending a no-brainer for both borrowers and investors.

FintruX Network provides unambiguous, immutable, and censorship resistant records where no arbitration is required. This is further enhanced by innovative scoring mechanisms complemented by machine learning and artificial intelligence, to maximize the success of safe lending unseen by traditional financing.

FintruX Network disrupts the way unsecured loans are being originated and administered, making unsecured loans highly secure.

Decentralization is the future and when it comes to having an open and transparent financing market, FintruX Network is leading the way.

FintruX Network makes it easy for businesses to quickly secure loans with no collateral in any currency. The platform is powered by blockchain and built using no-code development, which enables all possible contract combinations to be generated and deployed in real-time without human error.

FintruX Network is backed by Robocoder Corporation which was established in 1999 and builds mission-critical securitization software for financial institutions and accredited investors, managing several billions of dollars in assets.

FintruX Network is a multi-sided marketplace and ecosystem that delivers value not just to borrowers and lenders, but also to third-party guarantors and specialized service agents.

FintruX Network has a long history with disrupting the online finance space. The company was the first to create an online credit adjudication platform (in Canada in 1994) and has a background of working with reputable funders with billions of dollars already under management.
