Lampix Announces New Listing on Swap.Online

Nov 13, 2018

Today, Lampix, a developer of tabletop augmented reality hardware and software, is excited to announce that its PIX token will be listed on Swap.Online, a multi-cryptocurrency wallet that works directly through browsers.  Users are not required to download anything, the entire transaction occurs on Swap.Online


The main feature of  the Swap.Online wallet is its built-in P2P exchange which atomic swap technology, allowing users to exchange cryptocurrencies between different blockchains and set their own exchange rate (e.g. Bitcoin and Ethereum) without the involvement of a third party.  Users don’t send money to the exchange, but directly to each other: wallet to wallet. Traders’ money and private keys are never stored – all the exchange processes are performed inside the browser.
