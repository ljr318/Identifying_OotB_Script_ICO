BlockPay Update

Munich – 23.09.2018

Dear valued BlockPay token holders,

We are excited to inform you that BlockPay has been rebranded to Payger. We decided to give the product a more international market orientated brand. With this move, we also consolidated our other brands into one global representation.

Our new brand is www.Payger.com

Most frequent questions:

What is going to happen to my BlockPay tokens?

The BlockPay tokens will be considered within the Payger platform and our plan is that they can be exchanged to Payger tokens in the future. We are working on this plan and we will come up with results soon. Today the BlockPay tokens can be traded freely at the BitShares DEX. www.bitshareshub.io

Will the token be exchangeable to a new token?

Our plan is that the BlockPay tokens will be exchangeable in the future for a fix exchange rate to Payger tokens. This offer will be valid until all BlockPay tokens have been exchanged. There will be a dedicated amount of Payger tokens reserved for the exchange.

Who is involved with BlockPay ?

BlockPay has been rebranded to Payger and is under active development and managed by Christoph Hering and team.

What is the current project status in 2018 and what has been delivered?

BlockPay has been rebranded to Payger and is under active development. Since 2016 we have created and published the following work:

  • Smartcoins Wallet – Android Wallet For the BitShares Blockchain
  • BlockPay S – mPOS for Android
  • BlockPay for Odoo – A BlockPay plugin for the Odoo ERP platform
  • C-IPFS – IPFS rewritten in C
  • Payger Wallet – Multi Currency Wallet & Chat for Browsers
  • More products will be added.
