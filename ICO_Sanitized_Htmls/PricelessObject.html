THE PRICELESS OBJECT PO9K TOKEN

THE ECONOMIC MODEL FOR A STANDARD OF WORLD CITIZENSHIP

The Priceless Object is an economic mechanism to execute the creation of a fiduciary reserve for the development of World Citizenship within a Supranation with its own issued currency. The Priceless Object states the development of the conscience about the respect for Human Rights above any dogma and doctrine, and the acceptance of any philosophy, cult, religion developed by any culture during any historical period. The Priceless Object was also created with the objective of becoming perennially “The most expensive object in the world”. The value of The Priceless Object is US$ 900.000.000.000 (nine hundred billion dollars).

This amount will provide the macroeconomic basis for the monetary issuance of this proposal (explained in more detail in the White Paper ) and will be acquired through an Initial Coin Offering (ICO).

This proposal will utilize theICO to execute a new world economic system through the interaction between the economic principles of the issuance of fiduciary money and The Priceless Object. This global project will start from scratch with The Priceless Object and a 30-year lapse is considered for the complete execution of the supranational proposal.

WORLD CITIZENSHIP

This proposal intends to develop World Citizenship within a Supranation with a global standard where a massive number of people declare themselves as World Citizens and their surroundings as Regions within The Supranation . These Manifestos will be expressed through a symbolic act.

Thus, this proposal represents a standardization of civic practices rather than the standardization of cultural heritages. This is explained in more detail in the White Paper.

WHAT IS THE PO9K TOKEN?

The Priceless Object will offer budget subheadings and its value (nine hundred billion dollars) will be marketed with a token named PO9K that was originated for this proposal and whose current value is based on the parity 1:1 with the American Dollar (US$). The amount will be granted to a Non-Governmental Organization (Devaes) , created in 2006 for this purpose. Devaes will be the manager of this proposal and the developer of macroeconomic policies for the creation of World Citizenship.

A residual amount will be issued for the ICO Pre-Sale and for the collaborators of this proposal.

BENEFITS OF THE PO9K HOLDERS

PIONEERING CITIZENS

Holders will enjoy the status of Pioneering Citizens of this supranational and economic proposal through the acquisition of the first PO9K tokens.

SUPRANATIONAL CURRENCY

The token PO9K is the preceding financial instrument prior to the creation of the supranational currency. Therefore, the Pioneering PO9K holders will be privileged during the financial reconversion PO9K – supranational currency in the very near future.

NEW ECONOMIC MODEL

Holders will be the pioneers of the creation of a new economic model where The Smart Passport and its modules are tools for the exchange of goods and services. PO9K tokens will be used as economic reference.

SHORT TERMS BENEFITS

Holders will have an exchange tool to be used in the most recognized and principal exchange markets of crypto coins when this ICO is finished. This provides benefits in the short term and it is based on the purchase of the first PO9K tokens at the low introductory price.

CONFIDENCE MARKET

Holders will lead the supply and demand dynamic of PO9K in the exchange markets of crypto coins (creating a  market with confidence for the commercialization of the token PO9K )

THE SMART PASSPORT

As Pioneering PO9K holders, they will be the first smart passport users transcending historically along the execution of this proposal.

DEVAES AND ECONOMIC FIAT RESERVE

Devaes will be the manager of the economic resources of the whole project. It will receive the initial budget (in PO9K ) for the creation of the economic reserve. This economic reserve will be used for the development of The Supranation and World Citizenship . It is worth mentioning that Devaes will not manage the tokens/ PO9K of collaborators.

Conversely, future world citizens should register in Devaes to be able to declare themselves as World Citizens and manifest their commitment to follow all the supranational principles (explained in more detail in The White Paper ).

THE SMART PASSPORT

The Smart Passport is a supranational identity document awarded by Devaes . Unlike conventional passports, it is oriented to the concept of World Citizenship . The Smart Passport will initially be an app for Smartphones and operated through B lockchain technology and its incorruptible record. The Smart Passport will also be the executing tool for this project. The Smart Passport intends to transcend the human history  by converting simple “Smartphones” into tools for supranational interconnectivity.

This identity document will converge supranational and economic elements/characteristics which are described below.
