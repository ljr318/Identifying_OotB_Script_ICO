About The Project


The DIM (Data Interchange Module) is an alternative coin (altcoin) that is available in multiple variations: DIMCOIN, DIM Currencies and DIM Cryptocurrencies. DIMCOIN is a speculative coin that is traded against other cryptocurrencies on exchanges according to market forces. DIM Currencies are stable means of exchange in the DIM Ecosystem (DIM-E), and they are supported by precious metals and highly liquid cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin. The DIM Cryptocurrencies are DIM denominated mosaics of popular cryptocurrencies, for example, Bitcoin (BTC) can be converted into DIM BTC in order to be used in the DIM Ecosystem. DIMCOIN offered the first entry into the ecosystem which offers services such as; cryptonization, cryptonized-equities (blockshares), cryptonized-equity exchange trading, cryptocurrency exchange, e-commerce, inter-business (B2B) transactions and further blockchain business solutions.

DIM and its Ecosystem operate independently from the DIM Foundation. DIM is based on NEM's blockchain technology, meaning DIM offers a unique multi-tier design using node reputation, spam protection, and incentivised infrastructure to ensure safe and secure online transactions. DIMCOIN and other offerings in the DIM-E can be managed on these online wallets, such as DEPOTWALLET, DIMWALLET and DIMPAY. DIM X is a platform that exchanges different cryptocurrencies into Data Interchange Modules. The Hybrid Stock Exchange (HYBSE) is the online market place for global issuers and users that provides a wide range of tradable cryptonized equities.

Frequently Asked Questions



What is DIM?

DIM is an acronym for Data Interchange Module. It is an umbrella name for cryptocurrencies, products and services that bear the DIM namespace.

What is DIMCOIN?

DIMCOIN is the speculative cryptocurrency of the DIM Ecosystem, and it is tradable on various exchanges.

What are DIM Currencies?

DIM Currencies are stable cryptocurrencies supported by precious metals and liquid cryptocurrencies, and they are the primary means of payment in the DIM Ecosystem.

What is DIMPAY?

DIMPAY is a mobile payment application and gateway that allows merchants to accept payment for goods and services digitally with further benefits of being able to generate invoices.

What is DIMWALLET?

DIMWALLET is a light mobile version of DEPOTWALLET that allows users to access their on-the-go DIM denominated coins and blockshares.

What is DEPOTWALLET?

DEPOTWALLET is a comprehensive online blockchain wallet that allows multiple functionalities to store, transfer and manage their DIM denominated coins and blockshares.

How do I register for DEPOTWALLET?

  1. Visit https://depotwallet.com
  2. Click Open Account and follow the prompts

Where can I get DIMCOINS?

You can buy DIMCOIN from one of the exchanges where DIMCOIN is listed. We are Listed on these Exchanges

What is the total supply of DIMCOIN?

The total supply of DIMCOIN is nine billion coins.

What can you buy with DIMCOIN?

Various product and services offered by DIMPAY merchants can be purchased with DIMCOIN.

Difference between DIMCOIN, DIM Currencies and DIM TOKEN?

DIMCOIN is a speculative coin that is mostly used for exchange trading. DIM Currencies are stable coins that are primarily used as a means of payment throughout the DIM Ecosystem. DIM TOKENs represent a right to suggest and vote on the direction of the DIM Ecosystem. DIM TOKEN holders with more then 50 tokens are rewarded with a portion of the fees collected throughtout the DIM Ecosystem.

What is the DIM Foundation?

It is a non-profit organisation that is responsible for the marketing, public relations, customer care and oversight of DIM brands (DIMCOIN, DIM Currencies, DIM Cryptocurrencies and future DIM denominated coins).

How do I send or receive DIMCOIN?

You can send and receive DIMCOIN by using DEPOTWALLET, DIMPAY and DIMWALLET. Please note that it is required to have XEM (NEM's native cryptocurrency) to send DIMCOIN from your Nem based address (account).

What is the DIM Ecosystem (DIM-E)?

The DIM-E is an interconnected compilation of products and services that use the DIM denominated coins as means of exchange. It is made up of DIMCOIN, DIM Currencies, DIM Cryptocurrencies, DIM TOKENS, Hybrid Stock Exchange (HYBSE), DIM X,DEPOTWALLET, DIMPAY, DIMWALLET, DIM Explorer and International Blockshare Identification Number (IBIN),and more products and services will be added as the ecosystem expands.

What is cryptonization?

Cryptonization is the process of dematerialising products and services onto the blockchain.
