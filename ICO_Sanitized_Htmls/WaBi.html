Utility of WaBi tokens

WaBi are used as loyalty points to incentivize scanning of anti-counterfeit labels on Walimai protected products.

Consumers paying with WaBi will gain a variety of benefits, including: discounts, improved delivery terms, bonus WaBi with every purchase.

WaBi encourages consumer retention for participating brands while also functioning as the catalyst for ensuring collective security.
