About

Inflr is focused on creating digital marketing solutions using the latest technology for micro-influencers. We believe that all micro-influencers should have the opportunity to participate in an exchange that stimulates the creation of high-quality advertising content and allows digital influencers to be compensated for the true value of their influence.

Inflr Platform

Inflr has created the first platform that connects advertisers to influencers within a marketplace using blockchain technology. With Inflr, advertisers can promote their products and services using the influence of social network users.

Join now at Inflr Platform

For Influencers

When registering using your social networks, Inflr calculates how much your influence is worthy to post, take pictures, make videos, and other activities.

- Sell your influence and make money
- Change your influence for products and services
- Influence people anywhere

Register at Inflr

If you have more than 10k followers, you can get registered at Inflr!

When registering your social network, our system will analyze the quality and engagement of your content to create a score that checks how much your influence is worthy.

Be invited to participate in campaigns

After registering your social networks, it's time to start using Inflr.

The more complete your registration is, the more changes you will have to be invited to participate in campaigns.

Accept the invitation and post

When accepting an invitation, follow the campaign recommendations to produce content and send it to the client.

After approval, just follow the guidelines to post the content in your social networks.

Mission accomplished! It’s time to request payment.

At the end of the campaign, your balance will be available in your Inflr account.

You can request payment and receive it within 48 hours.

For Advertisers

In Inflr platform, influencers count on a 100% automated and smart campaign management.

- Pay for the real renown of influencers
- Reach the ideal target for your brand
- Create profiles for different brands

Register at Inflr, create a campaign and choose influencers

Creating a campaign needs choosing the kind of posting, whether simple or editorial, with video or photo, and choose influencers who will create and post contents according to your instructions.

Review and approve the content created by the influencers

Influencers will send the content produced for approval. Now, it’s time to review if the content meets your instructions. If so, approve it and release for posting.

Follow results through comprehensive reports

We created a detailed report so that you won’t lose any result about your campaign. You will access data such as number of clicks, likes, comments, sharing, conversion rate of each post, social network and the influencer participating in your campaign.
