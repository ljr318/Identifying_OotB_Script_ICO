What is ELAD Network

ELAD Network is a Blockchain based estate agent. We have developed an online real estate platform that combines Blockchain, Smart Contracts and Artificial Intelligence technology to revolutionalise the way we buy, sell, exchange & invest in property all over the globe. Our main focus is fractional ownership of property using EQ (Equity) coins. We are working in collaboration with several government organisations to achieve this mutual goal.

  • Connectivity across payments networks
  • Instant, on-demand settlement
  • Low operational and liquidity costs

Blockchain Technology

ELAD Network sits the Ethereum Blockchain to provide a faster more efficient transaction speed thus creating a platform where property can be transacted globally in days as opposed to months. Because the Blockchain is worldwide it allows fractional ownership investment for anyone no matter their location.
