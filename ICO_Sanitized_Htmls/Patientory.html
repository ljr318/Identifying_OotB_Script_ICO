What is Patientory?

Patientory empowers patients, clinicians, and healthcare organizations to securely access and transfer protected health information while providing actionable insights to improve health outcomes.
Patientory uses blockchain technology via the PTOYMatrix to ensure end-to-end encryption while adhering to regulatory guidelines and compliance requirements.
