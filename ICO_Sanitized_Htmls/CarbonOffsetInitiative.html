WHY ARE WE A GAME CHANGER?
C
Carbon footprint and pollution are increasing in front of our eyes and in order to get back on right track we have to change our habits and act together, only together we can change present environmental issues, save our habitat and preserve Earth’s natural resources for further generations.
Carbon Offset Initiative is a green solution for waste and data traceability in different industrial spheres, using innovative blockchain technology to enhance productivity in collecting, tracking and recycling waste from different sources.
Our mission is to help organisations and enterprises save energy, reduce costs and build broader commercial value while addressing environmental and social issues.

In COI, we always think that our investors should have more options than just one, that is why we are allowing them to participate in ICO through our web application and in IEO through our partnering exchanges. The main reason behind the idea of doing ICO/IEO hybrid is simple, we would like to democratize the investing process and we would like to see people who support our sustainable idea to contribute and together we can bring back the nature that once we had.
We want our investors to actively participate in our green business activities through referendums, benefit from it and learn more about how they individually can make a difference.
In COI, we care about your opinion. Let’s make a difference together, while it’s not too late.
