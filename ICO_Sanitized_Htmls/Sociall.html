Who has your data? And what are they doing with it?

Do you ever feel like someone’s watching you? Listening to your conversations? You mention a brand and it pops up in your social media. You bump into a childhood acquaintance and they appear in your feed as a ‘Suggested friend’. Big networks are getting smarter and faster at collating your user data – sometimes without you even knowing.

At Sociall, we believe your user data is yours, not ours. We don’t track your online activity to curate your content. And we don’t sell your data to third parties. We protect our users. Because your privacy counts.
