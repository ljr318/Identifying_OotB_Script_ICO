Finance


Traders and analysts use our technology to generate computer readable alerts from the never ending wave of finance news. These alerts are based on our client’s own proprietary business logic and typically include the impact and sentiment of the original news message. This way, our clients can processes news messages within milliseconds of their arrival, gaining a clear competitive advantage.

Legal


National and international legal organizations use our technology to rapidly process large volumes of documents. The use artificial intelligence to detect legal issues in due diligence processes and extract valuable insights from historical case law archives. Our powerful visual browsing tools help to detect insights hidden in the mass of data, for instance allowing the extrapolation of relevant court cases to determine the most likely ruling.

Healthcare


Professionals use our software to quickly determine the medical urgency of their patient’s complaints. By quickly adapting the business logic of the system using our patented modeling environment, we incorporate any triage protocol that our clients require. At the call center, our intuitive interfaces remove the need for extensive training and empower the user to ask the right questions, improving quality and reducing cost.

Healthcare examples

Media


Our media clients benefit from record breaking semantic analysis, scalable to high density streams and big data repositories. They apply data extraction to detect facts and figures in real time providing valuable input to their CRM systems. The flexible language structure allows capturing modern syntax in twitter and other social media, combined with machine translation, in all European languages.
